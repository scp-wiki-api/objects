Venus

Luna

Mars

> **POI-9042:** They are defending their children. You know, I had always wondered if gods came about before their people, or were born of them, or grew together. Suppose I’ll have to wait and see

> 

> **Graham:** So if we got rid of them, would they-

> 

> *POI-9042’s eyes turn from the gun to Director Graham’s face, her expression darkening.*

> 

> **POI-9042:** Amanda... is something as trite and artificial as normalcy something your organization is genuinely prepared to commit omnicide over? 

> 

> *Director Graham looks away. She lowers the gun and begins pacing once more as POI-9042 steps towards her.*

> 

> **POI-9042:** Why are you here? Why are you asking me about these things, offering up all this information? Are you here to kill me, tying up loose ends before whatever fool scheme your superiors have conducted?

> 

> **Graham:** No! I-I don’t-

> 

> *She stares at her silently for several moments before reholstering the gun and glancing at the observing camera.*

> 

> **Graham:** Just tell me when you replaced her.

> 

> *POI-9042 blinks, and her expression softens.*

> 

> **POI-9042:** The girl you knew, the crush with all the gentle blushes. That was before.

> 

> **Graham:** Then you killed her.

> 

> *POI-9042 shakes her head, lip quivering.*

> 

> **POI-9042:** No. Then she let me in, she-we changed, growing together. We-I became the her who loved you.

> 

> *Graham slumps into a chair, rubbing her face.* 

> 

> **Graham:** There is a plan. A dreadful, stupid, ugly plan, but it’s all we have.

> 

> **POI-9042:** Death and horror, I imagine. Letting loose another of your caged dogs?

> 

> *Graham is silent. POI-9042 approaches the table and sits atop it beside her.*

> 

> **POI-9042:** There is another way to fix this.

> 

> **Graham:** How?

> 

> **POI-9042:** Let them in.

> 

> *Graham looks up, mouth agape.*

> 

> **Graham:** And let them slaughter us?!

> 

> *POI-9042 looks to the ceiling and closes her eyes, whole body tense.*

> 

> **POI-9042:** You don't know that is what they want! That's the problem with you Foundation types, too eager to find a threat, so rarely willing to put the blades down and wait to see what something's intentions actually are. That's how all this started.

> 

> **Graham:** It was a bomb!

> 

> **POI-9042:** It wasn't! You assumed the worst-case scenario, and now over a dozen fresh divinities and their children are looking at their eldest sibling and wondering why hers are trying so hard to kill them.

> 

> **Graham:** Of course, we went to the worst-case scenario! Dozens upon dozens of times before it did, and we clawed it back. We stopped the world from falling apart. Do you think I haven’t considered all the possibilities?

> 

> **POI-9042:** All the negative ones.

> 

> **Graham:** You are painfully naive if you think that isn’t an earned assumption!

> 

> *Both women stand, glaring at each other, centimeters apart.*

> 

> **POI-9042:** Have they ever once come at you when you weren't after them first?!

> 

> **Graham:** We... most of us don’t want to kill them! 

> 

> *POI-9042 points at her.*

> 

> **POI-9042:** Most?

> 

> *Graham exhales as her eyes dart about the room.*

> 

> **Graham:** Some want to- We need them to stay where they are, keep them isolated. We can build enclosures, integrate them into the Foundation, and attempt to separate them from the gods. My plan it- We need time to make it all work, and I-

> 

> **POI-9042:** You'd be devastating their culture, robbing them of their homes! Why can't you just let them Be?

> 

> **Graham:** It's too late for that! They're already on their way; we've wounded them too many times. This won't end peacefully.

> 

> **POI-9042:** Because you assume they are naturally just like you! Throughout all this time studying them, have you ever attempted to actually learn about them, or just debated ways of keeping them locked away, despite that modus failing you again and again? They want their sister; they want you to leave them alone. Why can't you do that?

> 

> **Graham:** Their sister is dead or as good as. What happens when they discover this?

> 

> **POI-9042:** She doesn’t have to be! The Foundation can work miracles instead of terrors, and even if you fail, you won't truly know what will happen until they arrive. So, what are you going to do? Continue to blunt yourself on the encroaching wall, or actually make a change for the better?

> 

> **Graham:** You act as if we have not discussed any of this, like we haven't thought any of this through.

> 

> **POI-9042:** Then enlighten me.

> 

> *She sits down, staring up at Graham, fists clenched atop her lap.*

> 

> **Graham:** You believe, so many of you believe that we support the Veil, support normalcy because we loathe the oddity. You refuse to accept that cautious steps forward lead to better results; there always needs to be this vast and immediate change! You think you were going to help her with your ritual? What if you woke her up and all she felt was pain? Would she have lashed out? How many would die until she stopped? Would she simply suffer in silence? What if something else went wrong? Rampant diseases, sudden reorientation of climates, and storm systems? Climates rebounding and then immediately destabilizing? 

> 

> **POI-9042:** You have no way of knowing things could go so poorly.

> 

> **Graham:** And you have no way of knowing it wouldn't! Regardless, that isn't our problem; our problem is a dozen world-mind class divinities deciding they need to come here. Is that our fault? Would they have done it anyway? I have no idea. What I do know is they can't come here! Not yet.

> 

> **POI-9042:** Why not?

> 

> **Graham:** Ignoring the tide of ontological radiation that will arrive with them, risking unraveling many carefully laid wards, challenging the domains of numerous jealous yet appeased divinities, and threatening dozens of locations that rely on dimensional gateways? It will upend society entirely; people will panic, they will do foolish, fearful things, and then, regardless of whether the Foundation intervenes or not, things will unravel. Even if they wind up being peaceful and understanding, someone, more foolish and hungry than anyone in this organization, will make a move, and they will react as Gods do—unthinking retribution.

> 

> **POI-9042:** That is so painfully, unthinkingly cynical.

> 

> **Graham:** A man made a mistake, and Luna turned him to mulch for it. He had no idea he would hurt her.

> 

> **POI-9042:** She didn't know what she was doing!

> 

> **Graham:** Neither did he. The difference is in the magnitude. We make a mistake, and someone gets hurt. They react, and a continent gets leveled. They can't come here. 

> 

> **POI-9042:** Amanda, what do you want from me?

> 

> *She reaches out and takes both of Director Graham’s hands.*

> 

> **Graham:** I don't know. T-They're planning to do something that we won't be able to take back, and no one seems eager to point out that escalation will break everything. We need more time to come up with something better.

> 

> **POI-9042:** Then why come here?

> 

> *Graham looks down at their hands.*

> 

> **Graham:** Because I was never more grounded, never less afraid than when I was with her. 

> 

> **POI-9042:** Until you ran away.

> 

> **Graham:** You don't get to bring that up; that was between me and the woman I loved!

> 

> **POI-9042:** I am that woman.

> 

> *Graham tried to pull away but POI-9042 holds on tighter.*

> 

> **Graham:** No. You aren’t; you changed. She’s gone.

> 

> **POI-9042:** I was the Her that loved you. That might still love you. I'm here.

> 

> **Graham:** I-

> 

> *She looks at POI-9042’s face and then suddenly draws her into a hug. POI-9042 reciprocates.*

> 

> **Graham:** Jasmine I-

> 

>  **POI-9042:** What are you going to do, Amanda?

> 

> **Graham:** Present an alternative.

> 

> **POI-9042:** A good one?

> 

> **Graham:** You'll hate it. 

> 

> **POI-9042:** Just tell me.

> 

> *The feed cuts and is not noticed for a period of ten minutes.*

> 

> **<End Log, 10:42 PM>**

She was momentarily incoherent, and when asked about the incident involving POI-9042, she was unable to provide a concrete response. She was remanded into temporary custody for a 48 hr observation period, while the site and her quarters were searched. She showed no signs of mental, physical, or emotional alteration during that period, after which she was released. POI-9042 is still missing.

**Addendum 9042.3 - Project Svalinn**

#### PROJECT SVALINN

**Description:** Project Svalinn is a three-part initiative created for the sole purpose of defending Earth and the non-anomalous population from the existence of SCP-9042. While the study of these entities will continue regarding their possible containment and termination, this will grant our organization the time to do so.

Or so is the hope. 

**Initiative I - Dead Skies:** This initiative is to be undertaken immediately: a global amnestic and propaganda campaign that erodes public confidence in space travel and the veracity of claims regarding life outside Earth, while also increasing long-term disinterest in the night sky. This procedure aims to limit humanity’s curiosity about the stars and facilitate the dissolution of organizations and jobs tied to space exploration and study through a combination of false flags and public pressure.

The hope is that refocusing humanity’s consciousness away from stellar interests, alongside campaigns regarding climate change, will lead to a heightened focus on repairing the ongoing issues on Earth. 

A quarter of the Foundation’s resources will be anchored in perpetuating this obfuscation on top of administering to any SCPs on Earth that will not be worked into the remainder of the Svalinn Project, while also continuing to seek a solution to the SCP-9042 dilemma. 

**Initiative II - Tip of the Spear:** This initiative is to commence immediately. Entrenched positions outside of Earth are to be dissolved as efficiently and promptly as possible. All essential materials are to be stripped, and any manageable SCP instances stored on site are to be evacuated as well. Those deemed too dangerous to transport must be placed under long-term lockdown, with automated personnel responsible for implementing workable protocols. 

Half of this personnel are to consolidate with the joint Foundation-GOC fleet currently orbiting Earth. Afterwards, the fleet will employ coordinated strikes against the SCP-9042 instances, targeting key locations and doing everything in their power to slow their progress. 

Numerous eigenweapons and SCP instances have been authorized for use during this process. Secondarily, the gathering of required resources from SCP-9042, namely SCP-9042-5, falls under their jurisdiction. 

**Initiative III - The Last Wall:** Construction of this aspect of Project Svalinn is already underway. The ultimate goal is to create an artificial thaumic shield that surrounds the entire Earth. 

The exterior will be reinforced with the remainder of the fleet. At the same time, anti-ontological energies and theological protections are inscribed across the exterior and readministered on a tight cycle by thaumic practitioners and exorcists under the Foundation's employ. 

The interior will be covered with a projection of the sky prior to the emergence of SCP-9042, drawing from Foundation video logs with continuous AIC alterations working in conjunction with the efforts of the Dead Skies protocols to keep the Veil in place. 

The shield’s power source is also under construction and requires the immediate transfer of all Type-Green material within Foundation custody.

Additionally, connected to all protocols within Project Svalinn is the acquisition of every space-based organization, both national and private, by the Foundation, anomalous or otherwise, by any means available. Individuals in the employ of these organizations will be vetted for their skill and trustworthiness, being brought into the Foundation or amnesticized accordingly. 

Resistance is to be expected. Delegates to the various Nexus-Points have been sent, as their extra-dimensionality may prove a risk to the stability of Project Svalinn. Foundation occupation may be required, or the termination of their connection locus. Other GOI, such as the CI or Serpent’s Hand, are likely to be resistant to our plans. Terrestrial MTF forces are officially on standby. 

---- 

This is it—our last Hail Mary.

I know some folks argued for a more proactive end to this conflict. Planet crackers, Type Greens, weaponized Keters — I heard it all, and so too, I imagine, did the O5s. But I am thankful they took my protestations to heart. We have no idea what effects such devastating actions could have, or, in truth, if they would have any effect at all, and not just leave us with even more problems to clean up.

Our observations indicate that the instances are in motion, but most have a considerable distance to travel. This means we have time to halt their progress, if even momentarily, or draw them back “home”. We stay on our toes, and it will keep them on their toes, or so is the hope. 

I imagine many of you are wondering why you were recalled to Earth. The answer is evident, yet in three parts, the first is that Luna is our biggest problem, close as she is, and we need all hands to keep her at bay. 

The second being, in regard to our farthest outposts, Ceres is closing ranks, threading the asteroid field together into a vast net that grows more and more by the day. 

We are not entirely cut off; there are ways out, but for now, Earth truly will be the lone home left to us. 

And, thirdly and most importantly, the SCP-9042 instances are coming for her. From their own words, they seek their sister. Humanity has done a number on her, and when they arrive, make no mistake, no matter our actions, they seem likely to reach us. We can't let them find her in this state.

Therefore, healing her must be the priority, on top of uncovering anything we can use to halt their advance. The Veil will stand, on altered and shaking foundations, but it will hold. We will make sure of it. 

We can not kill them, but we can maim them. We will become like locusts, tearing away and dying in the dark of the void while the rest of humanity lives under the light of a false sun.

All that needs to be done will take considerable time; we are here to make sure they have it.

Welcome to Project Svalinn.

-- Project Director Amanda Graham, Honorary 05-14
