# **SPECIAL CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES**:

SCP-9067 is to be considered uncontained at this time. Any surviving biological components are to be held for study. The whereabouts of Foundation employee JACK MAYNARD and POI-9067 “DOUG PARSONS” are considered high priority. The physical search for hardcopy files relating to PROJECT PSYCHO KILLER are underway.

# **DESCRIPTION**:

SCP-9067 denotes a weapon system utilizing ideometric contextual targeting and 4 dimensional non-Euclidean movement to deliver a payload. SCP-9067 is capable of targeting individuals based on a psychospheric signature consisting of 3 or more semantic markers. The current iteration has been modified for use in Foundation-manufactured SKYEYE, EVILEYE  and BIG SKY series drones.

The ICT module entered development in 1963, before being shelved due to logistical and targeting precision concerns. These assets were taken into Foundation custody per the Berne Treaty's Paratech Limitation Agreement and remanded to Site-56 Epochal Storage. Between discovery and the most recent audit of Epochal Storage, all listed material is no longer present.

The Foundation has no record of orders to construct SCP-9067, nor a location of manufacture.

# **DISCOVERY**

The first surviving sample of SCP-9067 was recovered from Site-56's cafeteria, where it partially remateralized during breakfast service. Initial investigation revealed the unit to be a modified EVILEYE Air Intercept Drone. The wireless uplink unit was replaced with biological components attached to the ICT module, alongside extended batteries and frame reinforcements. The drone's payload bay contained a 1TB SATA hard-drive, with hardware keys encoded to Jack Maynard's issued laptop.

The contents of the hard drive are enclosed below. Files may be presented out of order, owing to incomplete metadata. MAYNARD is already seated at the conference table as PARSONS enters, holding a stack of folders.

**PARSONS:** “It's all very hush-hush. We might be the best kept secret of the department. When you deal with information that can harm you, the safest thing you can do is stop making sense.”

**PARSONS:** “How much do you know about the differences between cognitohazards and infohazards? Stick with me here, I've seen your resumè.”

**MAYNARD:** “One is harmful information, the other is a thing that hurts to think about?”

**PARSONS:** “Close enough. The way I see it, one is information you have to understand for it to harm you. It has to make sense. We use a lot of low grade cognitive hazards to protect *harmful* information. However, we have gaps in the fence.”

**PARSONS:** “Some information is too singular and discrete to attach a memetic hazard to. Nuclear secrets, proprietary formulas, bank account numbers. All potentially harmful leaks that can be written down on a napkin or said in your sleep.”

**MAYNARD:** “Where do I come in? I don't work in memetics or infosec.”

**PARSONS:** “Guidance systems! I've seen your work, and it's brilliant! The Exorcist S2A missile was sublime, and the BUNYAN-”

*He snaps his fingers.*

**PARSONS:** “A sub-treeline cruise missile? You have any idea how impressive that is to a layperson? We need someone who can steer a missile through the eye of a needle. I'm a fan, Jack. I'm going to cut to the chase. We found a way to close that gap.”

**MAYNARD:** “So, where do missile guidance systems come in? You didn't find a way to track, like, a thought with a missile, did you?”

*Parsons taps his pen on the desk and smiles.*

**PARSONS:** “Jack, earlier this year, one of your missiles locked on to and [shot down a ghost](https://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/scp-8377). Don't tell me you've stopped dreaming.”

*Maynard purses his lips.*

**PARSONS:** “We've got our hands on some paratech that we can't crack, this D.o.D. guidance chip from the cold war with classified capabilities we can't talk about outside of a cleanroom. It's a legacy system, sure, but we've made a number of parallel breakthroughs in other related fields. Just need a neat little bow to tie it all together.”

**MAYNARD:** “How urgent is it?”

**PARSONS:** “We would go right to the cleanroom after this meeting, take the elevator straight down. However: we're going to be down there until it's done, total sequestration. Conditional L6-COSMIC clearance, pay grade bump and you leave your mark on history.”

**MAYNARD:** “I don't th-”

**PARSONS:** “I brought you in because we just couldn't crack it. We've got a breakthrough on the level of infrared targeting or night vision here. You cracked thaumic targeting, you can crack psychospherics. They don't see your genius, Jack.”

**MAYNARD:** “I could take a look, I think. Will I have time to inform my team lead?”

**PARSONS:** “No need, just say yes and I can make all the proper phone calls.”

*MAYNARD pauses to consider.*

**MAYNARD:** “Yeah. Yeah, let's do this.”

They shake hands, and PARSONS produces a black bag from the stack of paperwork.  

**PARSONS:** “Go ahead and put this on, the physical location of the lab is above our clearance.”

PARSONS ignores his protests and slips it over his head.

PARSONS produces a second bag, slipping it over his own head. He stands, guiding MAYNARD out of the conference room.

NOTES: Neither man appears on any footage from the site's security cameras.
