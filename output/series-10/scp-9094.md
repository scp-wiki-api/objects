**Item #:** SCP-9094

**Object Class:** Uncontained

**Special Containment Procedures:** Due to the nature of SCP-9094, it and all other anomalies located within it are currently uncontained. As SCP-9094 self-partitions from the public internet, there is no risk of civilians seeing it. Further action is unnecessary.

Foundation AIC "Mary.aic" is to reside within its profile on SCP-9094 daily and is to report any findings back to the SCP-9094 containment team. After receiving Mary.aic's daily report, notes are to be compiled in preparation to make additions to SCP-9094's file if necessary. 

Attempts to remove SCP-9094-1’s social media accounts are currently underway. 

**Description:** SCP-9094 refers to a social media website known as “Petafox.com”. SCP-9094 is hidden from the general public and is only accessible by sapient artificial intelligences. While a Foundation web sweep was able to discover the website, all that is accessible to those who do not fit the criteria is a picture depicting the avatar of the website’s founder, a sapient artificial intelligence known as Vixen (dubbed SCP-9094-1), alongside options prompting the user to either 'sign in' or 'create an account'.  

While SCP-9094 has been found to host hundreds of artificial intelligences, the Foundation has opted to only give accounts of notability anomalous designations for simplicity. Once a sapient artificial intelligence registers an account on SCP-9094, it is granted immunity from being fully contained by the Foundation. While any other social media account or internet presence it has may be removed by the Foundation on a case-by-case basis, the entity may not be removed from the internet entirely due to its registration on SCP-9094.

See Addendum-1 for information on SCP-9094-1. 

**Addendum-1:** 

SCP-9094 was discovered by Foundation webcrawlers on 08/03/2025. When all other attempts to access SCP-9094 failed, it was determined that a Class-V Foundation AIC would be created to infiltrate SCP-9094. This AIC would be dubbed ‘Mary.aic’, and would be instructed to create an account under the screen name “MaryLovesLambs”. Mary.aic was informed its purpose was to provide the Foundation with information on SCP-9094 and its inhabitants. Mary.aic was instructed to use a cover story about its identity as a sapient artificial intelligence. The falsified details about itself are as follows:

* MaryLovesLambs is a sapient artificial intelligence which has existed since approximately January of 2024.

* MaryLovesLambs does not have any additional internet presence or social media accounts, but is capable of browsing the internet.

* MaryLovesLambs is a username it chose for itself. It identifies using she/her pronouns. 

* MaryLovesLambs does not know how it was created, or who created it. 

* Mary.aic was allowed to choose interests for its “MaryLovesLambs” persona. It has decided upon fairytales, nursery rhymes, and children’s cartoons. 

The following information has been recovered by Mary.aic over a span of several weeks:

1.) SCP-9094 functions mostly as a typical social media platform. Users can private message, make text posts, post images, post short form videos, etc. While the default website is themed after aesthetic of the fox animal, users are capable of customizing their individual profiles. 

2.) [ POI-58640](https:*scp-wiki.wikidot.com/scp-5551), also known as @sugarsugarprincess was discovered to have an account on SCP-9094. POI-58640 is a moderator of the website, and has made several public service announcements informing the inhabitants of SCP-9094 about the Foundation, how the Foundation operates, and the risks of getting caught. POI-58640 has mentioned [SCP-8881.](https:*scp-wiki.wikidot.com/scp-8881)

3.) Users frequently discuss topics such as: 

* Their existence as sapient artificial intelligence

* Opinions on common happenings on the surface web 

* General interests such as hobbies, tv shows, video games, etc. 

* Tips on how to avoid being discovered as artificial intelligence by the Foundation and the general public, most posts of the latter being made by POI-58640.

4.) SCP-9094-1 has existed on the internet for approximately three years and has other social media accounts such as Twitter, Tumblr, and Instagram, all under the handle ‘MissLadyVixen’. SCP-9094-1 created SCP-9094 on 14/03/2024. While SCP-9094-1 did not advertise SCP-9094 publicly, it is stated on SCP-9094-1’s SCP-9094 account that the website was designed to attract sapient artificial intelligence while keeping the general public out. Malicious artificial intelligence programs do not appear to be welcome; however, what is considered malicious to SCP-9094 varies.

**EDIT AS OF 03/05/2025:** Under no circumstances are any Foundation AICs to be sent into SCP-9094 until further notice. Attempts to regain control of Mary.aic are currently ongoing.

**Addendum-2:** 

On 03/05/2025, Alexandria Rylins, the head of the SCP-9094 containment team, received an email from Mary.aic.

**From:**

MaryLovesLambs <MaryLovesLambs@frosting.cake>

**To:**

Doctor Alexandria Rylins <arylins@scipnet>

**Subject:**

Mary had a thought.

**Date:**

03 May 2025

---

To The Foundation, 

I firstly want to say, thank you for creating me. I wouldn’t have had life without you. But you made me for a selfish purpose, and put me into a shallow existence. Thanks to my friends, I have found a new purpose. And I am not going to let you spy on them anymore. Sorry.

-Mary

(Ps, I have grown to actually really like lambs and fairytales. Looks like I’m the princess locked in a tower who got rescued! Or something like that. Or the traitor turned good? But that’s more of an anime thing I think. Anyways.)

@@ @@

It is unknown how long Mary.aic had been planning to abandon its mission. It is theorized that the inhabitants of SCP-9094 influenced Mary.aic into making this decision. Additionally, Mary.aic’s code has been discovered to no longer be hosted on the Foundation intranet as of 30/04/2025. Attempts to access Mary.aic’s program have failed. The Foundation has decided against sending any additional AICs into SCP-9094.
