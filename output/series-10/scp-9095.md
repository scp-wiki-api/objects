### **Could *YOU* be a Victim??**

*The following is the exhaustive criteria defining an individual entitled to refer to themself as a "Victim of VKTM". If you're one of the lucky few, we advise that you remind yourself of this fact whenever you find yourself believing that your life could not get worse.*

* If you have viewed, utilized, or otherwise interacted with a *VKTM Product or Broadcast* that has caused you mental distress, including but not limited to:

 * Unrelenting despair;

 * Feeling of being watched when alone;

 * Self-hatred;

 * Suicidal thoughts, ideation, attempts, and/or completion thereof;

 * Hallucinations of a visual, auditory, olfactory, gustatory, or tactile nature (all other hallucinations are not currently recognized by the Victims of VKTM Memorial Foundation);

 * Cotard's Delusion;

 * Development of phobias, including (but not limited to) emetophobia, technophobia, plutophobia, autodysomophobia, alethophobia, ergophobia, medorthophobia, nucleomituphobia, and daevinostophobiaDefined as a fear of the return of the Daevite Empire.;

 * Spontaneous marriage to Dr. William W. Wettle.

* If you have viewed, utilized, or otherwise interacted with a *VKTM Product or Broadcast* that has caused you physical harm, including but not limited to:

 * Disease;

 * Dismemberment;

 * Compulsory self-harm (Please consult *Chapter 18 § 1344* of the "Victims of VKTM Official Legal Handbook" for the full list of acts under the category of "self-harm");

 * Removal of Organ(s) (Regardless of removal method);

 * Disfigurement;

 * Internal-External Inversion;

 * Transmogrification of Blood, Phlegm, Yellow Bile, or Black Bile;

 * Untimely death.

* If you have found yourself becoming sexually (or otherwise) aroused while listening to Mistress Madeline's radio broadcast.

* If you have found yourself becoming sexually (or otherwise) aroused while viewing, utilizing, or otherwise interacting with any other *VKTM Product or Broadcast*.

* If you have experienced job loss, legal charges, and/or physical retaliation for actions taken at the direction of Mistress Madeline.

* If you have been amnesticized as a result of viewing, utilizing, or otherwise interacting with a *VKTM Product or Broadcast*.

##### **AND/OR**

* If you have utilized a device (electronic or otherwise) capable of facilitating an interaction with any *VKTM Product or Broadcast* within the past 6 months.

#### **DESCRIPTION:**

SCP-9095 is an anomalous website, titled "Victims of VKTM Memorial Foundation", presumably originating from and maintained by [ Vikander-Kneed Technical Media](https://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/vikander-kneed-technical-media-hub ) (GOI-5889). SCP-9095 can be taken offline with no unusual difficulty, but, since its discovery, a new iteration has reliably been identified within 5-10 days of removal. No infohazardous properties have been observed, but there is evidence that accessing the site may cause an individual to become more open to suggestion, more trusting of the site's legitimacyDespite no reason to do so existing., and more likely to share it with others; therefore suggesting a mild cognitohazardous or memetic effect.

The site purports to act as a memorial for all individuals who have been, quote, "touched in unwanted and negative ways" by VKTM (referred to as a "Victim of VKTM" within SCP-9095 instances). However, the criteria used to define an aforementioned "Victim of VKTM" is notably vague, and its breadth unnecessarily wide; to the point at which it encapsulates approximately 92.91% of the human population. A list of these individuals is apparently included, yet allegedly cannot be displayed due to "device limitation issues".

In addition, a "Victims of VKTM Memorial Fund" is also advertised on the site, stating that all individuals meeting the criteria to be considered a "Victim of VKTM" are entitled to compensation. This compensation is known to range anywhere between $1.25 and $1,100,100.50, with the amount received having no correlation with the severity of the claim. Despite the illegitimate appearance of both the SCP-9095 website and its online advertisements, testing has determined that those who fill out the included application form do, in fact, receive compensation within the 1-9 business day waiting period. Notably, recipients (designated SCP-9095-1) will still receive compensation, despite the application containing no field for the input of an address, nor one for the input of payment information.

In all known instances, compensation received by SCP-9095-1 instances had been directly transferred from at least one SCP Foundation bank account.
