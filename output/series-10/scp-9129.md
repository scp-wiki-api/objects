**Item #:** SCP-9129

**Object class:**EXPLAINED

**SPECIAL CONTAINMENT FEATURES:** All forested areas must be securely closed off if any sightings of SCP 9129 are confirmed. No civilian is permitted to go within 10 miles of secured forest, and all public civilians will be removed from the premises. Every animal in the forested area immediately gets removed, and soldiers or other cavalry from the foundation will be sent to the area during day hours.

**DESCRIPTION:** Commonly known as a 'skinwalker' by the public, the foundation adopted the name to it. It takes the form of a forest animal, but the skin and joints are likely stretched and mutilated under moonlight, as if the moon gives it a genetic mutation. Healing process is rapid, and it takes the shape of the skin it steals until in moonlight. Most often a deer, but the options can vary. They self-reproduce only once, but the one made immediately finds a forest uninhabited by another one. Speed is roughly 150 mph when aggravated. It is extremely carnivorous. The foundation currently has 5 in captivity until further notice.
