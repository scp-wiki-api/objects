**Special Containment Procedures:** All recovered instances of SCP-9147 are to be anchored at Sector-44 using heavy lead-weighted restraint rigs. Each instance must be continuously fitted with a cranial electrode array. This array is to maintain a constant electrical loop simulating a deep-sleep REM state within the deceased subject's brain tissue.

Under Protocol Keter-Lux, the chemical compound "Stasis-7" is to be kept at a 0.02% concentration within all public municipal water reserves. In the event of an uncontained post-mortem manifestation in civilian areas, Mobile Task Force Pi-1 ("City Slickers") is to deploy immediately to induce a localized negative feedback stasis before altitude escape velocity is achieved.

**Description:** SCP-9147 designates an anomalous alteration in gravitational physics affecting specific deceased biological matter. Upon cessation of all vital signs, affected human or large mammalian corpses instantly develop a massive negative gravitational mass, accelerating vertically into the upper atmosphere.

If an instance of SCP-9147 breaches the upper stratosphere without termination or anchoring, it functions as a resonance node. Any living biological organism within a variable radius (ranging from 120km to 450km beneath the node) will experience immediate and catastrophic cellular de-cohesion.

During a "Resonance Event," the molecular bonds holding cellular walls together are instantly severed. Affected subjects do not experience typical necrosis; instead, all soft tissue, musculature, and internal organs liquefy within 3 to 5 seconds, reducing the victims to a homogenous, non-viable organic sludge.

Bone structures remain intact but lose all calcium density, collapsing into the resulting mass. Due to the rapid propagation of this field, single uncontained instances have the potential to cause localized HK-Class Biome Subversion scenarios within minutes of atmospheric insertion.

Incident Log 9147-Alpha

> **Date:** November 14, 2024

> **Location:** Bio-Physics Research Site Area-12 (Subterranean Facility, Testing Sector C)

> **Subject:** D-4432 (Male, 31 years old)

> **Monitoring Personnel:** Dr. Marcus Vance

**[BEGIN LOG]**

**Dr. Vance:** D-4432, you are currently positioned in the center of the testing chamber. Please confirm that the biomechanical sensors on your suit are active and securely fastened.

**D-4432:** Yeah, doc, they're secured. But why the hell am I chained to the floor with these heavy lead weights? What kind of test is this anyway? There isn't even anything in the room with me.

**Dr. Vance:** Experimental protocol mandates precautionary gravitational anchoring. Just remain still. We are commencing the injection of the test compound now.

(An administrative error in the supply chain caused the dosage administered to D-4432 to contain an impure variant of the chemical compound, triggering sudden cardiac arrest.)

**D-4432:** Wait... something's wrong. My chest feels like... like it's tightening up. I can't...

**Dr. Vance:** Medical team, stand by for intervention. D-4432, maintain your position.

**D-4432:** (Heavy gasping, followed by the subject collapsing onto the concrete floor. Cardiac monitors indicate asystole — death confirmed).

**Dr. Vance:** Intervention cancelled. Subject is deceased. Activate the artificial REM system through the electrode array immediately! Move!

**Technician:** The system isn't responding, Doctor! The cranial electrodes failed to adhere properly due to the subject's perspiration prior to collapse! The subject's gravitational mass is dropping rapidly! It has already entered negative values!

**Dr. Vance:** Lock down the anchoring magnets! Now!

(Video footage shows the inert corpse of D-4432 suddenly rising from the ground, floating vertically. The lead chains attached to the suit stretch to their absolute limit under massive upward tension. The sound of groaning, strained metal becomes deafening.)

**Technician:** Tension on the anchoring rings is exceeding 15 metric tons! D-4432's suit is tearing apart!

(The suit straps snap violently with a loud crack. The body of D-4432 instantly accelerates toward the ceiling of the testing chamber, reaching an estimated velocity of 180 km/h indoors. Jude's corpse breaches the concrete structure of the ceiling, continuing its forced ascent straight up through the ventilation shaft and exiting the perimeter of the subterranean facility Area-12.)

**Dr. Vance:** No, no, no! It's breached the atmosphere! Sound the breach alarm! Contact Mobile Task Force Pi-1 immediately! We have an active instance in the stratosphere!

**Technician:** (Panicked voice) Doctor... the barometric alarm sensors are indicating the onset of a Resonance Event. The radius is expanding.

**Dr. Vance:** (Silence for 3 seconds) Oh, no...

(At this moment, the instance reached an altitude of 12 km directly above Site Area-12, activating its resonance node. Surveillance cameras inside the control booth capture Dr. Vance and the technician instantly collapsing to the floor.)

(No signs of blood or mechanical trauma are observed. Within 4 seconds, the laboratory coats of both researchers deflate entirely, leaving nothing on the floor but a liquefied, homogenous, grey organic mass. The skeletal structures of the bodies silently collapse into the fluid, losing all physical density.)

**[BLOOD AND LIQUEFIED ORGANIC MATTER FLOOD THE CONTROL SECTOR C. RECORDING CUTS OUT DUE TO LIQUID LEAKING INTO THE RECORDER'S CIRCUITS]**

> **Closing Note:** The instance generated by D-4432 was neutralized 14 minutes later by MTF Pi-1 supersonic aircraft using an experimental thermobaric warhead in the mesosphere. Site Area-12 has been chemically decontaminated; 142 fatalities due to cellular liquefaction were recorded among surface and upper-sector personnel.
