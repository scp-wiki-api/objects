Special Containment Procedures:

SCP-9211 is to be contained within a dedicated deep-subterranean containment facility constructed beneath Site-███. The containment chamber is to consist of a 100 m × 100 m × 50 m reinforced isolation vault lined with multiple layers of thermal-resistant and gravity-dampening materials. The chamber must remain completely isolated from all nonessential personnel areas, with no direct access points connecting the containment area to the main facility.

The primary containment method for SCP-9211 is environmental regulation. The containment chamber must be maintained at a constant temperature of -57°C to suppress SCP-9211's activity and reduce its mobility. The chamber is to be monitored continuously by an array of remote-operated sensors capable of detecting temperature changes, gravitational fluctuations, and movement patterns. No biological personnel are permitted within 200 meters of the containment chamber unless specifically authorized by Site Command.

Due to SCP-9211's ability to detect and pursue thermal signatures, all power systems, maintenance equipment, and monitoring devices located near the containment chamber must operate at minimal heat output. Excess heat produced by necessary equipment is to be redirected through underground cooling systems before entering the containment area. Personnel stationed within the containment sector are required to wear specialized thermal regulation suits to minimize their detectable heat signature.

SCP-9211 is not to be approached, touched, or interacted with directly under any circumstances. Attempts to communicate with SCP-9211 are prohibited following Incident 9211-7/10/2023, in which the entity demonstrated increased aggression and advanced predictive behavior during testing.

SCP-9211's feeding schedule is to be maintained through an automated thermal lure system. Every 16 hours, the containment chamber's internal systems are to release a controlled heat source designed to replicate biological thermal patterns. The lure is to be accompanied by the delivery of two live penguins through an automated containment transfer system. All feeding procedures must be performed remotely, and no personnel are permitted to enter the containment chamber before, during, or after feeding.

In the event of a containment failure or SCP-9211 leaving its designated containment area, Site personnel are to initiate Protocol BLACK WINTER. Upon activation, all nonessential power throughout the affected sector is to be disabled, and the surrounding area is to be lowered to subzero temperatures to minimize available thermal signatures. Personnel are instructed to evacuate rather than attempt retrieval or confrontation with SCP-9211.

If SCP-9211 exits containment, Mobile Task Force Delta-14 ("Cold Front") is to be deployed with specialized equipment designed to manipulate environmental temperatures and redirect SCP-9211 toward a prepared containment zone. Under no circumstances are conventional weapons to be used against SCP-9211, as attempts to damage or destroy the entity have proven ineffective and may increase its activity.

Due to SCP-9211's demonstrated intelligence and ability to learn from previous encounters, containment procedures are to be reviewed and updated following every observed behavioral change. Personnel are reminded that SCP-9211 is not an automated phenomenon but a sentient anomalous organism capable of planning, adaptation, and strategic decision-making.

Description:

SCP-9211 is a mobile anomalous black hole measuring approximately 3 meters in diameter. Unlike conventional black holes, SCP-9211 does not possess a fixed gravitational position and is capable of actively moving through its environment. Despite its relatively small size, SCP-9211 exhibits gravitational properties consistent with a black hole of unknown mass, although its effects are significantly localized and do not follow standard astrophysical models. The exact composition and origin of SCP-9211 remain unknown.

SCP-9211 was discovered on October 23, 1989, approximately 5 kilometers west of Vostok Station in Antarctica, following the complete destruction of the research facility and the disappearance of all personnel stationed there. Initial investigations determined that the station had not been destroyed by natural causes or conventional weaponry. Structural damage throughout the facility indicated that an unknown object had moved through the area, causing extreme localized gravitational disturbances and the collapse of multiple structures. All researchers assigned to Vostok Station at the time of discovery were confirmed deceased, with their remains showing signs of severe gravitational trauma.

Following the discovery, Mobile Task Force Delta-14 ("Cold Front") was deployed to locate, study, and contain SCP-9211. The task force initially struggled to track SCP-9211 due to its ability to move unpredictably across terrain while leaving minimal physical evidence of its passage. After several weeks of observation, Foundation researchers determined that SCP-9211 does not move randomly but instead actively seeks out sources of thermal energy.

When active, SCP-9211 is capable of moving at speeds of approximately 5 meters per second. SCP-9211 will continuously navigate toward the warmest detectable source within its environment, regardless of obstacles or distance. It has been observed altering its path to pursue living organisms, geothermal activity, machinery, and other sources of significant heat output. SCP-9211 is capable of detecting thermal signatures from distances exceeding 100 meters and will prioritize targets based on temperature intensity.

Although SCP-9211's primary method of interaction appears to be the absorption of matter through gravitational forces, it does not indiscriminately consume all objects within its vicinity. Observations indicate that SCP-9211 demonstrates preference-based behavior, frequently ignoring colder objects while actively pursuing warmer targets. This behavior suggests that SCP-9211 possesses a form of instinctive decision-making rather than acting solely through gravitational attraction.

Further study has confirmed that SCP-9211 is sapient, displaying problem-solving capabilities far beyond what would be expected from a natural phenomenon. Although SCP-9211 lacks any known method of vocal communication and has not demonstrated the ability to produce language, it has shown evidence of awareness, strategy, and intentional behavior.

Testing has revealed that SCP-9211 is capable of solving complex problems, including navigating mazes, identifying optimal paths toward a target, bypassing containment obstacles, and completing puzzle-based tasks. SCP-9211 has also demonstrated hunting behavior, including stalking potential targets, waiting for advantageous moments to approach, and altering its movement patterns to avoid detection.

During controlled experiments, SCP-9211 displayed the ability to learn from previous interactions. After encountering repeated containment procedures, SCP-9211 began adapting its movements to counteract security measures, suggesting that it is capable of retaining information and modifying its behavior based on past experiences.

The full extent of SCP-9211's intelligence remains unknown. Due to its unique nature, the Foundation currently considers SCP-9211 both a severe containment risk and a highly unusual example of anomalous artificial or natural intelligence. Research into SCP-9211's origin, purpose, and potential communication methods is ongoing.

<summary>Test Log 9211-3/19/2023</summary>

**Test Type:** Behavioral Observation  

**Lead Researcher:** Dr. ███████  

**Subject:** SCP-9211 Hunting Behavior Analysis  

**Foreword:**  

Following previous observations of SCP-9211 demonstrating awareness, problem-solving abilities, and intentional behavior, researchers conducted a remote observation experiment to determine whether SCP-9211 actively hunts targets rather than simply moving toward the nearest available heat source.

A controlled thermal source designed to replicate the heat signature of a human subject was placed within SCP-9211's containment chamber. No personnel were permitted within the testing area. SCP-9211's movements were monitored using thermal imaging cameras, gravitational sensors, and automated recording systems.

---

**<Begin Log>**

**09:14:03** — SCP-9211 detects the thermal source from approximately 87 meters away. The entity immediately alters its trajectory toward the target.

**09:16:21** — SCP-9211 stops approximately 40 meters from the thermal source. Instead of approaching directly, SCP-9211 begins moving in a circular pattern around the target.

**09:18:47** — SCP-9211 continues circling the target while gradually decreasing the distance between itself and the thermal source. Researchers note that SCP-9211 repeatedly adjusts its movement pattern, suggesting deliberate positioning rather than random movement.

**09:21:09** — SCP-9211 reaches a distance of approximately 20 meters from the thermal source. Movement speed decreases significantly, and the entity continues circling the target.

**09:23:55** — SCP-9211 reaches a distance of 10 meters from the thermal source. The entity remains stationary for approximately 12 seconds.

**09:24:07** — SCP-9211 rapidly accelerates toward the thermal source. The target is destroyed upon contact.

**09:24:19** — SCP-9211 ceases movement and returns to its previous inactive state.

---

**<End Log>**

**Researcher's Notes:**  

Thermal footage confirms that SCP-9211 demonstrates a consistent hunting pattern. Rather than immediately pursuing a heat source, SCP-9211 appears to circle its target while gradually closing distance, similar to predatory stalking behavior.

The reason SCP-9211 waits until reaching approximately 10 meters before attacking remains unknown. However, this behavior strongly suggests that SCP-9211 possesses awareness of distance, timing, and target vulnerability.

Following this observation, SCP-9211 is to be considered an intelligent predatory entity rather than a simple heat-seeking anomaly.

<summary>Incident Report 9211-7/10/2023</summary>

**Incident ID:** 9211-07-10-23  

**Date:** 07/10/2023  

**Location:** Site-███, SCP-9211 Containment Wing  

**Incident Classification:** Level-3 Containment Breach  

**Status:** Resolved  

**Personnel Casualties:** 6 Confirmed Deceased  

**Involved Personnel:**  

- Dr. ████████ — Level-3 Researcher  

- Containment Staff assigned to SCP-9211  

- Mobile Task Force Delta-14 ("Cold Front")

---

**Incident Summary:**  

On July 10th, 2023, SCP-9211 experienced an unexpected activation event during a scheduled research experiment conducted by Level-3 Researcher Dr. ████████. The experiment was intended to analyze SCP-9211's response to controlled environmental changes and further evaluate its behavioral patterns while exposed to artificial thermal sources.

At approximately 16:07, monitoring equipment detected abnormal movement from SCP-9211 within its containment chamber. The entity began increasing its movement speed and altered its path toward a localized heat source being used as part of the experiment.

Researchers attempted to terminate the test and return the containment chamber to standard conditions. These efforts were unsuccessful, as SCP-9211 continued exhibiting active behavior and began applying force against the containment structure.

Approximately four minutes after activation, SCP-9211 breached the primary containment area and entered the surrounding containment sector.

---

**Response Log:**  

Following confirmation of the breach, Site Command initiated emergency containment procedures and ordered the evacuation of all nonessential personnel.

Mobile Task Force Delta-14 ("Cold Front") was deployed to track and secure SCP-9211. Due to SCP-9211's ability to detect thermal signatures, personnel relied on specialized thermal imaging systems to monitor the entity's movements.

During the breach, SCP-9211 demonstrated behavior consistent with previously recorded hunting patterns. Thermal footage showed SCP-9211 avoiding direct approaches and instead circling areas containing personnel before gradually closing distance.

The entity appeared to prioritize individuals producing higher levels of body heat and adjusted its movement patterns in response to personnel attempting to escape.

---

**Containment Recovery:**  

Personnel operating remotely activated emergency temperature controls within SCP-9211's containment chamber. The chamber temperature was increased to create a stronger thermal attraction point and redirect SCP-9211 away from occupied areas.

After detecting the artificial heat source, SCP-9211 changed direction and began moving toward its original containment chamber. MTF Delta-14 used thermal equipment to guide SCP-9211's movement and prevent further deviation.

SCP-9211 successfully reentered containment after approximately ██ minutes. The chamber was resealed, and temperature levels were gradually reduced to standard containment conditions.

---

**Casualty Report:**  

Six Foundation personnel were killed during the containment breach.

All casualties occurred within SCP-9211's movement path and were determined to have been caused by exposure to SCP-9211's gravitational effects at close range.

No civilian casualties were reported.

---

**Post-Incident Review:**  

The investigation following Incident 9211-07-10-23 concluded that SCP-9211's intelligence and adaptability had been significantly underestimated.

The entity demonstrated the ability to react to containment responses, identify advantageous movement patterns, and actively pursue personnel rather than simply moving toward the nearest available heat source.

Following this incident, all direct experimentation involving SCP-9211 was suspended indefinitely. Future research is restricted to remote observation and non-invasive analysis.

SCP-9211 is to be regarded as a sentient predatory anomaly capable of learning from previous encounters and adapting its behavior accordingly.
