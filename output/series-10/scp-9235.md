Item #: SCP-9235

Object Class: Safe

Special Containment Procedures: Due to the rural nature of Kisaragi Station and the local circumstances regarding SCP-9235, no containment is required.

See **Addendum | Logistics** for further details.

Description: SCP-9235 is coin locker #4, kept *in situ* at the Kisaragi Station. SCP-9235 possesses a selective spatial anomaly which allows it to be significantly larger than is physically possible, but only when it is used to store newborns. Non-newborn materials — such as swaddling cloth, good luck charms, or letters — cannot be placed within the deepest part of SCP-9235 and thus can be extracted without physically interacting with attached newborns. The exact mechanics of SCP-9235's spatial anomaly are unknown. Regardless, SCP-9235 culminates in a storage solution that can efficiently handle newborns, as long as regular maintenance is provided to remove non-newborn material from SCP-9235.

Addendum | Sociality: SCP-9235 appears to be an open secret; civilians in the local area are aware of SCP-9235 and its general usage, but are distinctly ignorant of who specifically utilizes SCP-9235. Public opinion regarding SCP-9235 can be divided into two sections, which are respectively male and female dominated.

**Summary:** Primarily male opinions.

---

Male subjects tend to profess ignorance of SCP-9235 initially. Further questioning reveals that they do know of SCP-9235, but generally do not concern themselves with its specifics. Male subjects believe SCP-9235 possesses a feminine aspect to it, which discourages male subjects from pursuing further information.

As a result, male subjects tend to deny ever operating or visiting coin lockers *in general*, let alone SCP-9235. Additionally, they tend to avoid Kisaragi Station entirely, preferring private transportation. Security cameras in Kisaragi Station corroborate this statement.

Finally, in regards to male subjects with relations to female subjects who likely visited SCP-9235, they become evasive or hostile to questioning.

**Summary:** Primarily female opinions.

---

Female subjects are aware of SCP-9235 but consider it extremely impolite to speak about, reacting negatively to questioning. The Foundation was forced to use a female interviewer to proceed further.

Once in good company, female subjects spend a majority of their time accusing other females of utilizing SCP-9235. These individuals are typically poverty stricken, sexually explicit, or exceptionally young. Regardless, they are not viewed favorably.

Attempts at interviewing female subjects who have interacted with SCP-9235 is difficult, if not outright impossible. Local clinics and maternity wards lack any documentation regarding pregnancies in relation to SCP-9235, while family and other relationships — such as friends and lovers — report little contact. 

Addendum | Logistics: Due to common knowledge among the civilian population, a lack of resources required to address SCP-9235, and its discrete anomalous properties, the Foundation will allow SCP-9235 to operate without objection.

According to internal estimates, SCP-9235 is approximately 3m^^3^^, which can support up to 1200 newborns. Current SCP-9235 capacity is at 124 newborns. Thus, at the current storage rate, SCP-9235 can continue to operate for another 3 decades. At that point, SCP-9235 itself should make enough money to pay for the personnel and resources necessary to empty SCP-9235 without issue.

Addendum | Incident: During a regular inspection, the Foundation discovered the corpse of Aoba Mizuki, a sixteen year old female. The corpse was in a state of active decay from open air exposure for at least two weeks. A forensic autopsy concluded that she most likely died from exsanguination. The Foundation has constructed a timeline of events leading up to her death and subsequent handling of the corpse to determine if an anomalous cause was present.

18:00 —

Neighbors report shouting coming from the Aoba household; the most prominent voices belong to Aoba Daiki and Aoba Chieko, the father and mother of Aoba Mizuki. There are occasional bouts of silence.

19:30 —

Aoba Chieko exits the house and stands in the doorway of their house. She leans against the doorframe, placing a hand over her eyes. Aoba Daiki appears, whispers something into her ear, touches her shoulder, and they both return, leaving the door open.

20:00 —

Multiple drawers are slammed open and closed. Aoba Mizuki exits the house with her purse and schoolbag. Aoba Daiki shouts something at her as she walks away. He slams the door behind him.

21:55 —

Aoba Mizuki (henceforth referred to as Aoba), walks on foot to Hide Yuki's apartment. He is her boyfriend, was approximately fifteen years older than her, and was most likely to have impregnated her.

22:00 —

She knocks on his door, then texts him from her cellphone. She repeats this twice before Hide opens the door. They converse in the doorway, with Hide in apparent annoyance.

22:05 —

He questions her one-sidedly, and eventually lets her in.

22:25 —

It is unclear what they were discussing while inside. Hide opens the door, panicked, and leaves without shutting the door. Aoba dashes out, realizes Hide is leaving her, and angrily berates him. Aoba stumbles, grabs her shoes, and throws one after the other as he drives off.

22:30 —

She stands in the middle of the road, panting.

= TWO DAYS PASS

07:35 —

Aoba does not leave the apartment. Eventually, with a lack of food, she is forced to go to the nearby convenience store. There, she purchases an assortment of microwave meals, snacks, drinks, and a pregnancy test. She avoids streets leading to her school.

07:50 —

She returns home in time to see the landlady standing in front of Hide's apartment. They converse. Aoba learns that rent was due today, and since Hide defaulted on two earlier payments, the landlady was officially evicting him that day.

08:00 —

Unwilling to share her circumstances with the landlady, she leaves. Now homeless, Aoba goes to a nearby park to eat.

08:20 —

She sneaks into a playground set. Aoba attempts to contact Hide, but his cellphone number has been disabled.

14:00 —

Aoba wanders around aimlessly before going to a library. She rotates between reading manga, checking her phone, and staring at the ceiling. Additionally, she uses the restroom, presumably to use the pregnancy test.

18:30 —

The library closes, and Aoba is forced to leave.

20:15 —

Aoba stops by an intersection and stares at the traffic. She is still carrying a bag of food she cannot cook. She checks her cellphone one last time.

20:30 —

She begins walking towards Kisaragi Station. While traveling there, she stops by another convenience store, buying a bottle of water, chopsticks, cooking oil, painkillers, and a towel.

01:15 —

She makes it to Kisaragi Station.

01:45 —

Aoba sits at a bench to recuperate after walking for so long. She begins looking for SCP-9235 and manages to locate it by opening each locker. She stares into it, glances around, then prepares herself.

01:50 —
