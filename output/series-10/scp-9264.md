**Item #:** SCP-9264

**Object Class:** Keter 

**Special Containment Procedures:** No physical containment procedure can be implemented for SCP-9264. Failure to maintain global astronomical monitoring will trigger a CSK-Class Random Rotation Scenario, which will inevitably result in irreversible Veil compromise.All Foundation Global Astronomical Observation Stations shall maintain round-the-clock real-time monitoring of fluctuations in Earth’s rotational speed, alongside continuous synchronous calibration of global chronometry systems and satellite orbital parameters.

No viable physical containment method currently exists for SCP-9264-1, and conventional containment protocols cannot be applied to the entity. The Foundation must sustain continuous real-time observation to prevent the entity from ceasing its motion due to anomalous effects, an event that would precipitate an OK-Class "Earth Standstill" Scenario.

 

Should this scenario occur, global Protocol "Wayfarer" shall be enacted to maximize human survival. Full details regarding Protocol "Wayfarer" can be found on pages 301 through 351 of *Handbook for K-Class Scenario Contingencies and Associated Reports*. Disinformation protocols are pre-established to disseminate falsified data, misleading the scientific community into believing no lifeforms reside within the Earth’s core.

**Description:** SCP-9264 designates an anomalous phenomenon affecting Earth’s rotational cycle, whose activity is judged based on the diurnal cycle observed around Mount Chimborazo’s equatorial region. Mount Chimborazo is located in Ecuador, South America. Standing at an elevation of 6,272 meters, it is the tallest mountain situated directly on the equator. While the Chimborazo area experiences daylight  06:00 to 18:00 local time , the planet’s overall rotational velocity decreases at a consistent, regular rate. Conversely, rotational speed rises uniformly during nighttime hours within the same region  18:00 to 06:00 local time . 

SCP-9264-1 is a single Syrian hamster (Mesocricetus auratus) residing within the Earth’s core. The entity sustains its existence underground via anomalous properties: extreme somatic regeneration, complete immunity to senescence, and a total absence of metabolic processes. SCP-9264-1 maintains a circadian rhythm identical to surface-dwelling Syrian hamsters, despite the complete lack of day-night cycles within the planetary core.

SCP-9264-1 has been identified by the Department of Biological Habits, Subterranean Exploration Division and Geographical Department as the causal source of SCP-9264.
