## *CHRONICLE OF THE DUAL KNIGHT*

**Item #:** SCP-9269

**Object Class:** Keter

**Special Containment Procedures:** SCP-9269 is to be contained within a 15.2 m x 12.5 m x 9.8 m reinforced humanoid containment chamber (Room 414-B) at Site-17. The chamber's ambient temperature must be maintained at 18.3°C. The interior is lined with high-density kinetic-absorption plating.

Personnel assigned to SCP-9269 must undergo P-3 psychological profiling focusing on baseline narcissistic tendencies. Only personnel scoring below a 12 on the Hare Psychopathy Checklist are permitted within 50 meters of the chamber. To minimize the risk of a Type-B manifestation, personnel with high "Aggression-Index" scores or documented histories of impulsive behaviour are strictly prohibited from entering the Red Zone.

Personnel are permitted to provide SCP-9269-A with Earl Grey tea (specifically Twinings brand) upon request. Consumption appears to prolong the SCP-9269-A active state by approximately 14%.

Should SCP-9269-B manifest, Sector 4 is to enter immediate lockdown. The chamber will be flooded with aerosolized sedatives until the subject reverts to its primary state.

**Description:** SCP-9269 is a humanoid entity acting as the physical vessel for two sapient ontological anomalies, designated SCP-9269-A and SCP-9269-B. The physical body resembles an adult male standing 2.14 meters in height. The subject wears an unremovable suit of armor composed of an unidentified dense alloy. Microscopic analysis of the left pauldron revealed a faded serial number ("MK-IV-992A"). A persistent low-frequency hum (approx. 40 Hz) emits from the armour regardless of the active persona.

The entity exists in a state of flux between its two personas. While one persona assumes physical control of the body, the other is expelled as an intangible spectral projection. The spectral entity is conscious and observes its surroundings but cannot interact with physical matter.

**SCP-9269-A ("Arthur"):** The primary active state. Dermal patterns manifest as blue fractals (approx. 470nm). SCP-9269-A exudes a neurochemical field inducing measurable spikes in oxytocin in nearby subjects. It possesses a form of visual synesthesia, claiming to perceive localized fluctuations in the human bio-electric field, which it interprets as "stains" or "rot" correlated to the subject's past actions. SCP-9269-A wields SCP-9269-1, a large blunt-force instrument. Striking a human subject with SCP-9269-1 transfers no kinetic damage. Impact results in a temporary cessation of chronic PTSD symptoms and a suppressed acute-stress response.

**SCP-9269-B ("Mordon"):** The secondary active state. Triggered by high concentrations of narcissistic behavior or "Pride" signatures in the immediate vicinity. Due to the hostile nature of SCP-9269-B, visual confirmation of dermal patterns is limited to high-speed camera footage. Current imaging suggests jagged red markings (approx. 650nm) and occasional flashes of a secondary yellow luminescence near the collarbone. SCP-9269-B wields SCP-9269-2, a bladed weapon absorbing ambient thermal radiation. SCP-9269-B will attempt to terminate individuals it perceives as possessing high "moral entropy."

No recorded instances of animosity between SCP-9269-A and B have occurred.

**Addendum 9269.1: Interview Log 9269-A-04**

**Interviewer:** Dr. Aris Thorne

**Date:** 10/14/2024

**Subject:** SCP-9269-A

---

**[BEGIN LOG]**

**Dr. Thorne:** Good morning, SCP-9269-A. How are you feeling today?

**SCP-9269-A:** Please, Doctor, I have told you. You may call me Arthur. I am feeling well. The hum from the suit… it's louder today. I think it's the humidity.

**Dr. Thorne:** I want to ask you about the event yesterday. Researcher Vance breached protocol by boasting about his recent classification clearance.

*(SCP-9269-A visually slumps.)*

**SCP-9269-A:** It was loud, Doctor. The way he was talking… *(SCP-9269-A pauses, staring at the tea cup)* …it was just too much. I couldn't keep him back. I'm sorry.

**Dr. Thorne:** You became the observer. The spectral state. What is that like?

**SCP-9269-A:** It is cold. I watched the other one draw his blade. *(SCP-9269-A taps the table twice)* I watched him judge Vance for what he did to those Class-D personnel last year. I don't want people to get hurt. But he does what he thinks he has to do.

**Dr. Thorne:** Do you ever try to stop him?

**SCP-9269-A:** *(Shakes head)* He's a byproduct, Doctor. You can't vent the heat without a radiator. We're part of the same system.

**[END LOG]**

**Addendum 9269.2: Incident 9269-B-Sigma**

On 11/04/2025, an experiment tested SCP-9269's reaction to D-8832. D-8832 is diagnosed with severe schizoid personality disorder, profound apathy, and possesses a verified history of absolute emotional and moral neutrality.

**Test-879**

**Date:** 11/04/2025

---

**[BEGIN LOG]**

**00:00:** Upon D-8832 entering the chamber, SCP-9269 exhibited biological distress.

**00:15:** Dermal patterns rapidly strobe, settling on a static violet gray.

**00:45:** The physical structure of SCP-9269 exhibits micro-fissures. Vocalizations from SCP-9269-A and B overlap, consistent with extreme cognitive disorientation.

**01:10:** A localized spatio-temporal instability event initiates. The kinetic plating of the containment chamber temporarily transmutates into a brittle silicate. Silicate samples recovered from the chamber floor post-incident matched the chemical composition of standard industrial quartz, though emitting mild ionizing radiation at 0.4 mSv/hr.  SCP-9269’s weapon rapidly shifts between SCP-9269-1 and SCP-9269-2.

**01:30:** Extraction of D-8832 completed. SCP-9269 collapses. Reversion to the SCP-9269-A state occurs after 184 minutes of inactivity.

**[END LOG]**

---

**Observation:** D-8832 represented a baseline conceptual void. SCP-9269 requires specific neuro-signatures to anchor its physical state. A purely neutral subject causes severe spatio-temporal instability. Further testing with emotionally inert subjects is strictly prohibited.
