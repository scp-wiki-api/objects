**Special Containment Procedures:** Despite our best efforts, SCP-MMMM remains uncontainable, as success in containment causes widespread breaches within the site and anomalous events. It is to be supplied with rare resources native to earth such as wood, certain plants and animals, and certain O5 approved SCPs and Foundation equipment.

**Description:** SCP-MMMM is a humanoid entity standing at about 1.95 meters tall and appears as a 1940s-50s mob boss. It consists of striped black and gray fedora with a red rim and a black suit with a red tie accompanied black trenchcoat with red rims and an inside with the pattern of a red and black checkerboard.

SCP-MMMM has various confirmed cross-universe buyers and suppliers such as designated SCP-MMMM-A, it deals with supplies verified as possibly planet destroying weapons along with enchanted items that radiate what D-Class describe as “Pure evil bottled up”. 

SCP-MMMM is shown to possess hume manipulation to alter its surroundings turning them into shades of gray, black, and white and growing in size 1 meter every 5 seconds at a constant rate until reaching a maximum of 20 meters at maximum size. SCP-MMMM has also been confirmed to cause anomalous effects including the sound of smooth jazz originating from an unknown source.

Although SCP-MMMM has remained silent on information regarding its employer, reconnaissance with alternate realities have shown useful in providing information regarding its higher ups. This anomaly, designated SCP-MMMM-1 along with it currently being considered as a GoI, is a large black-market conglomerate that offers a large variety of services including shipping, weapons, possible strike teams for hire, along with a variety of mercenaries.   

> **Interviewer:** Dr. Exo

> **Interviewee:** SCP-MMMM

> **[Foreword]:** The following interview was conducted shortly after SCP-MMMM's initial manifestation inside Sector-4. A localized Hume fluctuation occurred immediately prior to the interview, accompanied by an unidentifiable auditory phenomenon (smooth jazz) echoing from the room's ventilation system.

> 

> ----

> 

> **Dr. Exo:** Testing, one two... Alright. Good afternoon, SCP-MMMM. Can you explain how you breached our baseline reality?

> 

> **SCP-MMMM:** *(Adjusts fedora, leaning forward)* Let's skip the formalities, Doc. You call it a "breach," I call it opening a fresh market. I'm a businessman, and I hear the Foundation has a lot of capital. 

> 

> **Dr. Exo:** We are a containment organization, not a corporation. We aren't interested in buying what you're selling.

> 

> **SCP-MMMM:** *(Laughs softly)* Everyone's buying, Doc. It's just a matter of currency. You want to keep your little reality nice and stable, right? I've got clients in places you can't even dream of who want what you've got growing in your backyard. Wood, iron, fresh water... simple stuff to you. But to a guy from a dead universe? It's premium asset material. And what I give you in return? Let's just say it'll keep your walls standing a whole lot longer.

> 

> **Dr. Exo:** And the items that our D-Class described as "pure evil bottled up"? What exactly are those?

> 

> **SCP-MMMM:** A little something from the 3rd party network. High quality, lethal, and strictly off-the-books. Take a look at the catalog, Doc. First trade is on the house.

> 

> ----

> 

> **[Closing Statement]:** Following this exchange, SCP-MMMM expanded by approximately three meters before completely de-manifesting, leaving behind a single wooden crate containing a high-tier anomalous artifact. Cross-verse tracking of "SCP-MMMM-A" has been classified as a Priority-4 objective.

> **[Foreword]:** The following interview was conducted three (3) days after SCP-MMMM requested a meeting with select O5 council members and Dr.Exo to officiate a select price for its services. Due to the high security risk, the interview took place in a reinforced neutral containment cell in southern Nebraska.

> 

> **O5-1:** Good morning, SCP-MMMM. We have come at the request of establishing a more permanent deal for your "services", is that correct?

> 

> **SCP-MMMM:** Ya ya, sounds about right, so you "Overseers" review that list I sent you yet?

> 

> **O5-6:** Yes, we did, but some of the payments listed on there are out of our reach and can't provide, such as a Keter class soul. We don't even know if some of our Keter class anomalies can be killed let alone their soul extracted.

> 

> **SCP-MMMM:** I don't hear you suggesting any other payments on the list, and besides, you want the items on the list I gave you to stay out of your rivals' hands, right? So, I suggest you pay me before things get messy. *(SCP-MMMM grows in size to 3 meters, and the surrounding area converts black and white)*.

>

> **Dr.Exo:** *(face sweating)* Well...there is one on this list we can provide, that is if the O5 approve.

> 

> **O5-1:** SCP-MMMM, we will not fold to your threats, you cannot even comprehend the way we could have you removed from our timeline. Along with you, Dr.Exo, I suggest you stick to your interview notes and stop, improvising. 

> 

> **SCP-MMMM:** Ah, O5. I get that threat in every timeline. You don't think I've heard that one before, even if you remove me, the boss will just send the next one, and the next, or maybe, just maybe he'll shatter your timeline himself and sell the scraps.

> 

> **O5-1:** *(All O5 monitors shut down leaving Dr.Exo alone in the room with SCP-MMMM)* **[O5-1, O5-3, and O5-6 all turn toward the council chamber's monitors and begin to review charts and reports from other realities with similar encounters.]**  

> 

> **O5-3:** *(Clearly Stressed)* Look at reality R-4000, a man described as a black-market dealer arrives at their Site-19, files suggest they refuse every deal it offers the O5s, and approximately five (5) days later SCPs are found in possession of highly anomalous objects along with our connection to theirs being severed and our scientists unable to stabilize the connection.   

> 

> **O5-6:** Our charts show it's not a very dangerous entity, it also seems nervous and twitchy every time he makes one of those threats or mentions his boss, I believe it's not it destroying the reality's, rather it's the employer.

> 

> **O5-1:** *(Understanding flashing across his face)* Contact Dr.Exo, tell him the meeting is postponed until further notice. Once we have our leverage, we will bargain with it.

> 

> **Dr.Exo:** *(Dr.Exo receives an encrypted message from O5-6 commanding him to inform SCP-MMMM that this meeting has been postponed, Dr.Exo's face relaxes)* It's my duty to inform you that this meeting has been postponed, do not try and contact us, we will contact you, good evening SCP-MMMM.    

> 

> **SCP-MMMM:** Very well, I will allow this postponement, but I expect my payment within the month. Oh, and don't forget to breathe doctor. *(Dr.Exo is shown to display weakened breathing capabilities exiting the cell, no cause has been identified)*
