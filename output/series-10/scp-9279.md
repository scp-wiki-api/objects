**Item #:** SCP-9279

**Object Class:** Safe

**Special Containment Procedures:** SCP-9279 is contained in a refrigerated container at 4°C in Site 33. Testing or removal of any portion of SCP-9279 requires Level-2 authorization.

**Description:** SCP-9279 is a round of Gouda cheese weighing ten kilograms. Biochemical and molecular analysis indicate that the composition of SCP-9279 is identical to non-anomalous Dutch Gouda cheese.

SCP-9279's primary anomalous properties manifest upon the removal of any portion of its mass. The "primary mass" of SCP-9279 is defined as being any portion ~~≥50%~~ ≥51% of the pre-removal weight. When any dairy product is within a 3-meter radius of the incomplete primary mass of SCP-9279, SCP-9279 will remotely absorb the additional material, transmuting it into identical Gouda cheese. No matter is created or destroyed during this process. Regenerated portions of SCP-9279 will be identical in age and flavor profile to the primary mass.

Removed portions of of SCP-9279 do not exhibit anomalous properties provided they remain further than three meters away from the primary mass of SCP-9279. If a removed portion remains within the 3-meter radius, it will be re-absorbed by the primary mass after three hours.

SCP-9279 will enter an inactive state when the round is completely restored to ten kilograms.

**Test Log:**

| Test | Input | Result |
| --- | --- | --- |
| #1 | 100g removed. 200ml of liquid nacho cheese introduced. | Portion of liquid nacho cheese is absorbed. |
| #2 | 50g removed from SCP-9279. 10g cube of Monterey Jack introduced. | Cube is absorbed. |
| #3 | 40g removed from SCP-9279. One liter of cow milk introduced. | Portion of milk is absorbed. |
| #4 | 40g removed from SCP-9279. Dr. Hauger approaches SCP-9279 ten minutes after consuming cheese curds. | No reaction. |
| #5 | 100g removed. 100g of cashew cheese introduced. | No reaction. |
| #6 | 20g removed from SCP-9279. One liter of soy milk introduced. | No reaction. |
| #7 | Quarter wedge of SCP-9279 removed and placed at Site-32 holiday potluck among non-anomalous cheeses. | No reaction. Wedge was consumed by staff before retrieval. |
| #8 | SCP-9279 is cut into two precise halves weighing 5kg and placed into separate locations. 5kg of Provolone is introduced to both halves. | Both halves of SCP-9279 absorb the Provolone. Two identical wheels of SCP-9279 are created, designated SCP-9279-1 and SCP-9279-2. |
**Addendum 9279-1:** Following duplication from Test #8, SCP-9279-1 and SCP-9279-2 are stored in separate refrigerated containers. Testing involving precise splits of 50% is no longer permitted.
