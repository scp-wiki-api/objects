****Special Containment Procedures:**** ~~Integrity checks of the soundproofing and restraints are to be carried out daily. Noise cancelling headphones are to be tested before every confession.~~ Until further notice SCP-9304 is to be held in the Items of Religious Significance wing of Site-12. Requests for access are to be sent to your Ethics Committee representative.

****Description:**** SCP-9304 is a confessional constructed in late 18th Century Wales. It is carved from a single piece of Roloab An evergreen tree native to Nx-03  wood and features iconography from multiple religions and backgrounds, primarily Catholic Christian but also includes the Eldar Futhark rune Gebo, the Hindu deity Lord Vishnu, and the Egyptian Goddess Ma'at. Two non-human deities associated with Fey practices are also included. According to early photographs the confessional originally had kneeling pads for both Penitent and Confessor, an uncommon arrangement at the time.

It is believed to have been constructed as an alternative to the outlawed practice of Sin-Eating A practice carried out in Wales and bordering counties whereby a ritual meal was eaten over the recently deceased, the food absorbing their sins and passing them to the eater . According to notes gathered during Operation ROSEBUSH, the confessional purportedly allows for the guilt of the Penitent to be transferred to the Confessor.

****History:**** Installed in Cowlys Church, Pembrokeshire, in 1834, it remained in place until 1847, when spiralling repair costs caused a new church to be built to replace the failing building. At some point during the building and move, the confessional was lost and didn't resurface publicly until the late 1940s in the hands of Dr M Francis, a therapist specialising in treating World War Two veterans. They gained popularity in the Oxfordshire area, with claims of a "miracle cure" that led to an expose in Psychology Weekly and the Doctors arrest for kidnapping and false imprisonment in 1949. The therapist had removed both of the kneeling pads, replaced with simple chairs bolted to the floor, and hidden one side of the confessional behind a false wall where the kidnapped individual was found. Littlemore Hospital had recently joined the NHS and took them in until they passed in 1956. They were never identified.

The expose was flagged by the British Occult Service who sent agents to investigate, but upon reaching the therapist's office discovered that it had been bought wholesale by Marshall Carter & Dark and had been stripped bare.

****Acquisition:**** In 2021 as part of Operation ROSEBUSH, a raid was carried out on a health spa operated by Marshall, Carter & Dark. The intention was to uncover a Pudelan An anomalous marsupial native to ██████ which can alter its mass to facilitate gliding between trees  smuggling ring, and recover any other items of interest. SCP-9304 was clad in soundproof panelling with one door removed and only identified as an item of interest when an individual was found inside the locked partition. Upon discovery of the security logs, object was identified as potentially anomalous pending testing.

****Testing:****

#### NOTICE FROM THE ETHICS COMMITTEE

On 23/07/██, D-84427 was conducting one of their secondary duties which included the transportation of hazardous materials for destruction, when they intentionally released the pressure valves on several volatile waste containers, causing a large explosion. The resultant damage to the site infrastructure allowed SCP-███  to temporarily escape containment. SCP-███ was recaptured 42 minutes later and the containment breach declared secure. There were 4 fatalities including D-84427. It is not believed that they had intended to cause the containment breach.

Lead Researcher Larkin has been suspended pending psychological evaluation after repeated daily use of SCP-9304

SCP-9304 will remain secured under EC control until such time as suitable Ethical Guidelines have been drafted

-- The Ethics Committee
