Waffle House 5 Miles is an anomalous sign with the words Waffle House 5 Miles and an arrow, once touched, whatever touched it will be instantly teleported to Nevada within 5 Miles of a waffle house. but everything will be blue. this IS Nevada, and the object that touched it still exists, its just blue. to make it back, travel in the direction of the sign until you reach a waffle house, ask for a greenberry pancake. workers in Nevada waffle houses are trained to give it, you will then be transported back to the sign but everything will be green for 5 minuets.
