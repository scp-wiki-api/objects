**Item #:** SCP-9371-ARC

**Object Class:** Safe

**Special Containment Procedures:** SCP-9371-ARC is contained at its site of discovery. The warehouse containing SCP-9371-ARC is to be regularly patrolled by two guards.

**Description:** SCP-9371-ARC is a carved, vaguely humanoid sculpture constructed primarily out of concrete. SCP-9371-ARC is roughly 144 centimeters in height. SCP-9371-ARC has the following characteristics:

* It has a prominently carved phallus measuring approximately 10 cm in length.

* It has a rounded, disproportionate lower body with exaggerated curves.

* Its face is covered with red Krylon brand spray paint, determined to have been applied a significant time after its construction.

* It is always facing the nearest viewer.

The sculptor and origin of SCP-9371-ARC are unknown.

Any attempt to physically lift, relocate, or otherwise directly interact with SCP-9371-ARC results in the object vanishing from the individual’s immediate reach and subsequently reappearing in one of the four corners of the warehouse. SCP-9371-ARC has not been observed leaving the location of its discovery.

SCP-9371-ARC was discovered inside of an abandoned warehouse. At the time of recovery, the facility had been cleared of valuables; SCP-9371-ARC was the sole item remaining.
