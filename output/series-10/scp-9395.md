**Item #:** SCP-9395

**Object Class:** Safe

**Special Containment Procedures:** SCP-9395 is stored within Secure Holding Locker 412-C at Site-██. Access is restricted to personnel with Level 2 clearance or higher. Personnel handling SCP-9395 must wear heavy-duty protective gloves. Under no circumstances is the eraser component of SCP-9395 to make contact with any surface outside controlled laboratory conditions. Following Incident 9395-Alpha, all kinetic testing has been indefinitely suspended.

**Description:** SCP-9395 is a standard wooden pencil measuring approximately 18 cm in length. Testing of its core material confirms a non-anomalous composition consisting of a standard clay and HB graphite mixture, which functions identically to standard consumer stationery.

The anomalous properties of SCP-9395 are strictly localized to its attached pink eraser component. When a human subject deliberately applies the eraser to a solid material using a standard rubbing motion, the targeted material undergoes rapid disintegration.

The effect varies depending on the material affected:

* Organic materials reduce to fine gray ash within seconds.

* Metals and plastics oxidize into a brittle, unbonded powder.

* Silicate structures (glass and concrete) collapse into a fine crystalline dust.

The eraser exhibits no measurable wear or mass loss regardless of the quantity of material degraded.

**Addendum 9395.2: Incident Log 9395-Alpha**

**Date:** ██/██/20██

**Location:** Site-██, Material Testing Chamber B

**Personnel:** D-4921

[00:01:14] Test 01 concludes. Research Assistant █████ instructs D-4921 to return SCP-9395 to its transport case.

[00:01:22] D-4921 fumbles SCP-9395 while securing the lockbox.

[00:01:24] D-4921 attempts to catch SCP-9395. The eraser makes direct contact with the palm of his left hand.

[00:01:25] The tissue of D-4921's left palm immediately begins disintegrating into gray ash.

[00:01:26] **D-4921:** "What the—  my hand is gone."

[00:01:28] **Research Assistant █████:** "Don't touch anything else! Security!"

[00:01:29] D-4921 stumbles backward, brushing his left arm against the testing desk. A section of the desk rapidly disintegrates. Security personnel activate the chamber's emergency pneumatic guillotine, amputating the affected limb at the mid-forearm.

[00:01:31] The severed limb completely disintegrates. No further spread is observed.

**Post Incident Medical Analysis:** Medical examination determined that nerve tissue disintegrated before pain signals could reach the central nervous system. D-4921 reported no pain, exhibiting severe psychological shock until chemical sedatives were administered.

**Status:** Due to the biological and kinetic hazards demonstrated during Incident 9395-Alpha, all subsequent testing requests have been denied. SCP-9395 remains in secure storage pending further review. Personnel are reminded that SCP-9395's mundane appearance should not result in relaxed handling procedures.
