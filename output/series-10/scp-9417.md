**Item #:** SCP-9417

**Object Class:** Euclid

**Special Containment Procedures:** Provisional Site-84 has been constructed around the entrance of SCP-9417 in Mato Grosso, Brazil. The anomalous perimeter is enclosed within a reinforced concrete containment dome and classified as a restricted military zone under the guise of an ecological preserve. 

The entry portal is to be monitored 24/7 by automated point-defense turret systems. In the event of an SCP-9417-A containment breach attempt, lethal force is authorized. Following the events of Incident 9417-Tau, all further manned exploration of SCP-9417 is strictly prohibited without unanimous Level 4 approval.

**Description:** SCP-9417 is a stable extradimensional gateway measuring approximately 4 m × 4 m. The gateway opens into an alternate terrestrial environment that geographically corresponds to baseline Earth. Every explored location matches its real-world counterpart with remarkable accuracy, including roads, buildings, vegetation, and infrastructure.

Despite this correspondence, all objects within the environment exhibit a uniform pale blue coloration. Spectroscopic analysis has failed to identify any physical cause for the phenomenon. Personnel consistently describe the environment as "familiar but fundamentally wrong," reporting persistent feelings of unease within minutes of entry.

No human inhabitants have been encountered. Buildings contain furniture, vehicles, and personal belongings positioned as though their occupants departed without warning. No evidence of decomposition, weathering, or recent human activity has been observed.

The environment is inhabited by hostile humanoid organisms designated 9417-A, which display coordinated pack behavior and aggressively pursue intruders.

SCP-9417 is inhabited by an indigenous population of hostile entities, designated SCP-9417-A. These entities are humanoid, roughly 2.1 meters in height, with elongated limbs and no visible facial features except for a wide, predatory jaw. SCP-9417-A instances display extreme, immediate hostility toward all baseline terrestrial life. They do not act as solitary predators; instead, they exhibit advanced, coordinated pack-hunting tactics, strategic flanking, and communication via high-frequency vocalizations.

**Addendum 9417.1: Incident Log 9417-Tau**

On 12/04/2025, Research Team Gamma-6 (4 personnel) entered SCP-9417 to conduct a structural analysis of the distorted environment. Six hours into the expedition, all telemetry and radio contact with Gamma-6 were abruptly lost. 

Mobile Task Force Tau-44 ("The Gateway Guides"), consisting of an 8-man rescue and data-retrieval unit, was deployed into the anomaly.

**After-Action Summary:** Only Operatives Vance and Reyes successfully exited 9417. Both sustained multiple traumatic injuries and were treated immediately upon extraction.

The recovered storage devices confirmed the deaths of all four members of Research Team Gamma-6.

Analysis of Tau-44's helmet-camera footage identified at least 113 instances of 9417-A within visual range during the retreat. Fewer than one-third actively engaged the task force. The remaining instances maintained observation from surrounding structures until visual contact was lost.

No further manned expeditions have been authorized pending review of Incident 9417-Tau.
