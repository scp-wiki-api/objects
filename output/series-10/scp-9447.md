*****SCP classification number:***** SCP-9447

*****Anomaly class:***** keter

SCP-9447 is a keter class anomaly. It attacks anything and anyone by envelope them with it's shadow. Appearance: an anomalous shadow wearing a piece of clothing that ranges drastically from a cloak, to shoes, to a hat, to nothing. SCP-9447 is not in containment because it can travel under doors and even into different realities. It has been known to have appeared in: Minecraft, the wizerding world, and more. SCP 9447 can travel to anywhere fiction on non-fiction whenever it pleases. A special phenomenon is under testing and review where a d class (D class number:1387) went into a room with SCP-9447 and had the same affect to the shadow as SCP-999: they calmed it down. We currently do not know what about the D class of 1387 calmed it down but nobody else can. D class 1387 was instantly promoted to C class with an exception of SCP-9447 an now is known as C class 9447.
