**SCP-9449:** Themselves Defy

**Author:** 

[*https://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/whenwerewe Here's my page!]

**Item #:** 9449

**Object Class:** Euclid

**Special Containment Procedures:** Under no circumstances is SCP-9449 to expose both hands simultaneously. SCP-9449 is to remain equipped with articulated locking gauntlets at all times; removal of these devices requires dual authorization from both Senior Researcher Klein and Senior Researcher Siksek.

SCP-9449 is to be housed in a ground-floor or subterranean habitation and denied access to all edged implements.

**Description:** SCP-9449  is William Park, an American male demonstrating ectoentropic reality-bending traits. By maintaining physical contact with an object and focusing on a possible alternative timeline, SCP-9449 produces a persistent duplicate corresponding to the selected counterfactual state of that object.

**Discovery:** Foundation assets were alerted to a suburban neighbourhood in Spokane, Washington, after local reports documented an atypically large and persistent congregation of turkey vultures. Together with anomalous purchasing activity associated with the residence,Specifically, a tendency to buy far less groceries than woud be expected for a household of its size. and repeated neighbourhood complaints of late-night disturbances, these reports prompted a covert investigation.

At the time of containment, SCP-9449 was a 27-year-old, living in a property titled to his father, who resided with him. SCP-9449 was not in full-time employment and had been working as a freelance high-school tutor for approximately eighteen months. Following a search of the premises and confirmation of anomalous indicators, Foundation operatives detained SCP-9449 and administered amnestics to the father.
