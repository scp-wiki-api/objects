**SCP-9504:** No Outside Beverages

*Word count: ~550*

**Author:** [KatyaStrangelove](https://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/katyastrangelove)

---

"You ever feel pathetic? Imagine being outranked by a jug of fruit punch..."

**Item #:** SCP-9504

![](http://scp-sandbox-3.wikidot.com/local--files/katyastrangelove-j/coffee.jpg)

SCP-9504-1, *01-03-2012*

**Clearance Level:** 04

**Special Containment Procedures:** An inquiry has been submitted to the Department of Personnel Classification, requesting that the Level-04 Clearance possessed by SCP-9504-1 instances be revoked. Should it be the case that Senior Researcher Orville Lassen's own clearance is inextricably linked to SCP-9504, Site-19 Administration has ordered that action be taken regardless.

Until SCP-9504 is resolved, the following items are to remain readily available to personnel in the immediate vicinity of Dr. Lassen:

* One roll of standard issue paper towels;

* One double-sided kitchen sponge;

* One portable Scranton Reality Anchor;

* One bottle of all-purpose spray cleaner;

* Two 6 oz bottles containing hydrogen peroxide and 92% ethyl alcohol.

**Description:** SCP-9504 is a bureaucratohazardous phenomenon by which Site-19 Senior Researcher Orville Lassen's Level-04 Clearance extends to any and all beverages within his possession (referred to as instances of SCP-9504-1). Often, liquids are strictly forbidden within the areas where Dr. Lassen performs his duties, yet he insists that the instances *"have a right to be present,"* suggesting that the anomaly may possess a compulsion effect.

All policies functionally prohibiting instances of SCP-9504-1 are categorically superseded by the Level-04 Clearance held by these items.
