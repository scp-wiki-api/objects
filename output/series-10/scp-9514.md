**Item #:** SCP-9514

**Object Class:** Keter

Special Containment Procedures: Must be fed at least 5 D-Class as It seems to make SCP-9514 a longer dominant period. Must be contained in a 30x30x40 room comprised of acid-proof, fire-proof 10 meter steel wall.

**Description:**SCP-9514 looks like Giraffa camelopardalis but with a larger head with fangs of up to 13.6cm long. Its body seem in a rotting state with many bones protruding out. It was discovered when the locals of African savanna reported to the authorities that they found several carcasses of animals and human forcefully ripped open.
