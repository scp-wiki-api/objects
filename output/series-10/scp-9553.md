Item #: SCP-9553

Object Class: SafeSpecial

 Containment Procedures: SCP-9553 is to be contained in a standard humanoid containment cell at Site-142, located on Saaremaa Island. Due to its cooperative nature, standard low-security protocols apply. Following Incident-9553-A, any testing involving the mass-consumption capabilities of SCP-9553 requires prior approval from the Project Lead (currently Dr. ██████)

 Description: SCP-9553 is a sapient humanoid entity. From the neck down, the subject physically resembles a human male dressed in a white formal suit, gloves, and a tie. Radiographic imaging indicates that the interior of the suit is entirely devoid of physical matter, despite the clothing retaining a solid, human-like structure. SCP-9553 exhibits physical strength and mobility consistent with an average human adult.

 The head of SCP-9553 is a perfect sphere that absorbs 100% of incident light. This sphere exhibits localized gravitational properties identical to a micro-black hole. SCP-9553 possesses complete conscious control over this gravitational field, preventing atmospheric decompression or structural damage to its surroundings. Any matter entering the event horizon of the sphere is instantaneously vaporized and ceases to exist within baseline reality.SCP-9553

demonstrates high cognitive capabilities, scoring exceptionally well on standard intelligence and scientific aptitude tests. Due to its continued compliance and lack of escape attempts, SCP-9553 has been granted provisional Level 2 research privileges, strictly limited to theoretical physics and under the direct supervision of Dr. ██████.
