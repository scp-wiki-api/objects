**Special Containment Procedures:** Trespassers near SCP-9561 are to be exposed to the countermeme RA-MI-BO, amnesticised, and relocated away from the area.

Records of SCP-9561's location as well as relevant documentation regarding Richard Magni (PoI-1701) are maintained by the [Department of Archaeology Naval Task Force Chi-30 "The Very Model of a Modern Mobile Task-Force."](https://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/scp-9014) Any civilian records of SCP-9561 discovered are to be replaced with a forgery or removed.

Any personnel exploring SCP-9561-A are to obey the following protocols:

* Explorations into SCP-9561-A should not exceed 6 hours. Personnel who have exceeded 8 hours of exploration are to be considered dead.

* A high value object approved by one or more on-site researchers is to be carried in a secure water-proof container on your person.

* A two-way communication device, a navigational compass, and a headlight are to be carried on your person. All devices must be tested and deemed operational prior to entry.

* Making contact with or carrying any objects present in SCP-9561-A is strictly forbidden unless prompted to do so by communications personnel.

* Before exiting SCP-9561-A, enter chamber A and place your assigned object at the centre of the room.

* After exiting SCP-9561-A, personnel will have their physiology, psychology, and personal property examined. Additional examinations of a personnel's social connections, ontology, and nomenclature may be performed depending on the situation.

@@@@

**Description:** SCP-9561 is a large tropical island located in the Caribbean Sea. SCP-9561 possesses an antimemetic effect, such that subjects are incapable of perceiving SCP-9561's landmass, even on satellite imagery. This antimemetic effect is removed upon seeing geographical illustrated depictions of SCP-9561 or making physical contact with its landmass.

SCP-9561-A consists of several interconnected tunnels and chambers below SCP-9561, accessible from an eastern cliff near the shore and a western subaquatic tunnel. An anomalous effect is present throughout SCP-9561-A, affecting anything that enters it or interacts with items present within. Any items within SCP-9561-A's area of effect will be considered 'deposited' and teleported to one of SCP-9561-A's chambers within 8 hours. SCP-9561-A's chambers grow or shrink in size depending on the amount of items deposited within them. These shifts are sometimes observable in SCP-9561's landmass.

If a subject attempts to leave SCP-9561-A without depositing an item of monetary or perceived value it will result in something of high or personal value being taken from the subject; attempting to remove an object from SCP-9561-A or damage it results in a similar effect.Adjusted for the value of the removed / damaged object. A damaged object will return to its appropriate chamber in SCP-9561-A undamaged. 

Depositing an item of high monetary or perceived value, and then removing an object of lesser value from SCP-9561-A will result in the item of lesser value being safely removed. Items perceived as priceless can only be safely exchanged for two other priceless items.

**Addendum 9561-A-B:**

> A painting titled 'The Plight of Scotia', created in 1656 and believed to have been lost since 1705, located in chamber B. The painting depicts vessels at the docks of Halifax, with attacking pirates.

**Addendum 9561-A-E:**

> The ship's manifest of a boy named Richard born aboard a passenger ship (name illegible) to Blair Magni and Donella Degnan, located in chamber E. Dated 1645.

> A letter of marque issued by England to Richard Magni of Nova Scotia, located in chamber E. Dated 1677.

**Addendum 9561-A-F:** 

> An English frigate vessel located in chamber F. A seabird figurehead was affixed to the bow, and the ship was named "The Sanntach".

**Addendum 9561-A-I:**

> Approximately 4600 seal pelts belonging to Caribbean Monk Seals *(Neomonachus tropicalis)*, located in Chamber I.

**Addendum 9561-A-M:** 

> Several 'language' items containing the extinct Taíno language located in chamber M. A single item was extracted for study, now belonging to Senior Researcher Bonnie Anne.

**Addendum 9561-A-N-1:** 

> Several 'memory' items located in chamber N. The following are descriptions of the memory items audio-visual elements:

*****Brajan Nowicki*****

*Brajan is mopping up the deck of a ship, with another man clinging to the shrouds and looking off in the distance.*

**Unidentified Voice:** OY! All hands on deck!

*Brajan and the other man run quickly to the starboard of the ship. A man wearing a puffy red undershirt, captain's hat, and black coat is currently pulling up a rope. Brajan as well as other crewmates assist him in pulling.*

**Captain:** Pull! Pull like you really mean it!

*At the other end of the rope is a humanoid creature entangled in a rope net; A woman with a seal-like tail, long black hair, and tattoos and wounds across her body. As she struggles in the net, the captain pulls out a knife.*

**Humanoid Creature:** <Do not kill!>Translated from Taíno Help!

*The captain pulls the rope net down onto the deck and cuts the ropes open with his knife.*

**Captain:** Calm down lass, settle down! Yer not dying on my watch.

*The captain grabs the creature's hand and lifts her up. The creature's tail shifts into human legs, and she backs up against the edge of the ship.*

**Richard Magni:** Yer under the care of Captain Richard Magni, privateer for Charles the second of England. What's yer name, lass?

**Yahíma:** Yah-Yahíma.

**Richard:** Well Yahíma, I can 'ave me crew take a look at those cuts o' yours and send ye on yer way. Brennan!

*Richard points at a crewmate, waves him off, then stares at Yahíma's legs.*

**Richard:** If ye don't mind me askin', jus' what exactly are you, and what happened?

**Yahíma:** I am an Aycayi, who was under attack by the nets and spears of your kind.

**Richard:** Cannae say I've ever heard of such, but ye look like a Selkie to me. In any case, Brennan! Quit your strolling and get over here!

*The crewmate that was waved away earlier comes back with a large medicine bag. Brennan and Richard Magni treat Yahíma's wounds.*

**Yahíma:** Thank you! Do I owe you a debt for your kindness?

**Richard:** No reason for debts to be owed, go on your way.

*Yahíma stands up and walks to the edge of the ship, then dives into the water. A tall and dark-skinned man with a coat similar to Richard Magni's climbs up from below deck.*

**Richard:** Murdoch, there ye are! Get over here, or you'll miss- Ah, she's already gone.

*****First Officer Edward Murdoch*****

*Edward Murdoch stands at the port of the Sanntach, standing next to the shorter Richard Magni. Edward and Richard look over the port and see an island in the distance. In the water, several seals swim alongside the Sanntach.*

**Richard Magni:** What say ye, Edward?

*Edward rubs his chin.*

**Edward Murdoch:** I would say you've gone crazy what with this matter of sealfolk, but considering you're not drunk, I-

**Richard:** Yet.

**Edward:** ...I found it easier to believe. So what is this isle we're going to anyway?

**Richard:** The Aycayi I had saved some months ago wished to guide me here as a measure of goodwill, though only for a visit. Secrets must be kept, I suppose.

*****First Officer Edward Murdoch*****

*Edward Murdoch wanders the deck of a ship. Several individuals on deck are restrained with ropes.*

**Edward:** All clear, Captain.

*Richard Magni is seen approaching from behind, then grabs Edward's elbow and pulls him in tight. Richard looks towards an entrance to the hull.*

**Richard:** Good work, Ed! Time to clean up, eh?

*Edward Murdoch and Richard Magni climb down into the hull. The hull is full of crates and barrels. After several minutes of hauling up barrels of food and drink, Richard Magni pushes on a crate that seems too heavy for him.*

**Richard:** The hell is- Ed! Gimme a hand!

*Richard Magni and Edward Murdoch wrench a side of the crate open. Gold and jewels spill out of the crate. The two stare at the pile for a minute. Richard drops to his knees.*

**Richard:** I- How-

**Edward:** Captain?

*Richard Magni stands up after a moment, righting his attire and posture.*

**Richard:** Get some barrels out here, post-haste.

*****First Officer Edward Murdoch*****

*Edward Murdoch and Richard Magni sit at a table in Magni's quarters. The room is darkened but lit by candles. An assortment of food is sprawled on the table.*

**Edward:** A bit much for one meal, don't you think?

**Richard:** It's the anniversary of me best haul yet! Why shan't I gorge meself?

*Richard Magni starts eating a chunk of pork. Edward Murdoch picks at a lime.*

**Edward:** Speaking of which. We serve England, when exactly will this haul be sent to the king?

**Richard:** King... Which king are those Englishmen serving now?

**Edward:** James the-

**Richard:** Doesn't matter. You know what I reckon? The king hasn't done a fair nor charitable act to my home country in all the years I lived. Not a single king or queen in all those royal lines spent a coin out of their pockets to help US! We've been loyal privateers, helping clear out the ports from pirates and doing their dirty work, fer nothin'!

**Edward:** What best be done with all this gold, then? Takes up too much space in the hull.

*Richard pauses for several seconds, thinking and muttering to himself while sipping at his drink.*

**Richard:** A-ha! I know! The Libertalian Federacy. I've heard rumours of their whereabouts, but it's always been far too risky to sail there.

**Edward:** You want to join up with pirates!?

**Richard:** Pirates they may be, Murdoch, but freedom is their lifeblood. If we get in their good graces, we might have a chance to live as we want to live: no orders from the crown, naught but the open sea.

*Richard raises his cup and Edward joins him.*

**Richard:** Here's to new horizons!

---

*****Brajan Nowicki*****

*Brajan and other crewmates are pushing a heavy crate off of the Sanntach and onto a somewhat settled island. The sun is setting, and several pirates are relaxing around a fire. The crate is moved over to a large building, and the job is taken over by several unidentified men. Brajan heads over to the fire and sees Richard Magni drinking at the fire with Edward Murdoch nearby.*

**Richard:** All's well?

*Brajan nods, and Richard pats him on the back.*

**Richard:** Excellent work. Here's yer pay.

*Brajan is handed a small pouch of coins by Richard, who turns back towards the fire and Edward.*

**Richard:** How goes your natters with the Federacy?

**Edward:** I am as persuasive as I can be, but only a scant few share interest in shutting down pirate activity in Nova Scotia. The gold ye've given me so far isn't enough for these rogues to risk their lives.

*Richard leans forward towards Edward, and Brajan turns away to return to the Sanntach.*

**Richard:** Why not?

*****Brajan Nowicki*****

*Brajan swabs the deck of the Sanntach, glancing up at a man hoisted by a rope, wearing shackles and a bag over his head. Richard Magni and Edward Murdoch are seen, already in the middle of arguing with each other.*

**Richard:** I swear on me mother's life, Murdoch. I saw that scab pilin' up my doubloons into his bag.

**Edward:** I don't doubt your vision, Captain; but that topman out there doesn't deserve to die, much less in such a humiliating way.

**Richard:** 'Tis merely the distribution o' justice. If ye want to suggest a better method for keepin' thieves at bay, speak up.

*Edward Murdoch is silent.*

**Richard:** I thought as much.

*Richard walks up to a series of gunmen aiming at the hoisted man, then points towards him.*

**Richard:** Fire when ready.

*Gunshots are heard. Brajan Nowicki clasps a hand over his eyes, and the mop he's holding is heard dropping to the deck.*

*****Brajan Nowicki*****

*Brajan sits in a booth in a public house, crying to himself. Richard Magni is celebrating with the crew of the Sanntach at the main bar of the public house. Several unaffiliated women surround him and his crew. Richard cheers his drink.*

**Richard:** Here's t' the best damned crew this side o' the globe!

*Richard laughs, Edward Murdoch approaches Richard from the side and taps him on the shoulder.*

**Richard:** Hm?

**Edward:** Throwing your money around for all this, I think we ought t' slow down!

**Richard:** Nonsense! Raises morale, gets my boys well fed 'n happy!

**Edward:** True that may be, but-

*Richard slams his fist against the bar, the noise quiets down.*

**Richard:** BUT WHAT?! HUH?!?

*Edward backs up a few steps.*

**Richard:** God forbid I decide t' share me spoils! Maybe they ought to call ye 'the greedy one', huh Murdoch?!

**Edward:** Pardon my disrespect. The crowns have caught wind of our staying here, and our ship, no doubt thanks to our spending. I've already heard reports of Spanish ships on their way as we speak.

**Richard:** You mean we're being ambushed?!? Those posh bastards seem fiercer by the week.

**Edward:** Maybe they grow less afraid. In any case, I've already prepared for our departure, captain.

*Richard slumps backwards into his chair, looking around at the crew.*

**Richard:** Well? Ye heard the first mate, finish yer drinks and yer fancies n' set sail!

*Richard sits back up, swaying back towards the exit of the public house. Brajan and the crewmates leave the women and drinks behind, departing soon after.*

*****Caonabo*****

*Caonabo stands next to Yahíma as the Sanntach pulls into the shores of SCP-9561. The dialogue between Caonabo and Yahíma is spoken in Taíno, which was translated.*

**Caonabo:** <Ten years ago, those humans saved your life?>

**Yahíma:** <Yes. I was speared and then made helpless, but my life was spared by them.>

**Caonabo:** <Most kind of them, now why and how are they here?>

**Yahíma:** <I do not know, but I imagine they are here to collect my debt to them.>

**Caonabo:** <You allowed them passage here?!>

*Richard Magni and Edward Murdoch approach Caonabo and Yahíma, nodding and looking between the two of them.*

**Richard:** Greetings Yahíma, n' greetings...

**Yahíma:** This is Caonabo, my father and leader of this chiefdom.

**Richard:** I am captain Richard Magni, esteemed captain o' the Libertalian Federacy. I seek refuge in this fair isle, fer I'ave accumulated many enemies who wish fer me death. My partner here is Edward Murdoch.

*Caonabo exchanges glances with Richard and Edward, then back towards SCP-9561. He looks between various stone structures before landing his vision on SCP-9561-A. Caonabo points at the cave's entrance.*

**Caonabo:** My group does not sleep inland, you and your crew can have the caves.

**Richard:** Caves? Ye've got plenty o' trees, we could use that wood fer a manor or-

**Caonabo:** No, you will take the caves.

*Richard crosses his arms, turning his head over to Edward, who nods. He turns his head back to Caonabo.*

**Richard:** Ach... Very well.

*Richard Magni signals for the crew to start moving into SCP-9561-A. Various containers holding treasure are transported by crew members.*

*****First Officer Edward Murdoch*****

*Edward Murdoch leans against a wall, with Richard Magni on the other side of an indistinct chamber in SCP-9561-A. Richard Magni is restless.*

**Edward:** What 'ave you called me for, captain?

**Richard:** Edward... Edward, I cannae take much more o' this. This place is too dangerous, enemies are at every corner.

**Edward:** What enemies? Where? How?

**Richard:** The Aycayi! Ye don't think 'em suspicious, but ye'd be wrong. I caught one o' 'em, having scuttled a crewmate and trying to take off wit' me treasure! They cannae be trusted I tell ye!

**Edward:** This happened?! Show me at once!

*Richard nods and runs off into the tunnels, and Edward Murdoch follows.*

**Richard:** We cannae let them stay on this island any longer!

**Edward:** How I wish it weren't this way...

*Soon Edward Murdoch and Richard Magni reach a clearing with a pool of water, and the bodies of an Aycayi and a nameless pirate.*

**Richard:** Ye see?! Ne'er shoulda' trusted the scum t'keep their word...

**Edward:** A grim sight indeed. Caonabo should know of this-

**Richard:** Never! I forbid ye!

*Richard Magni runs to Edward, placing his hands on Edward's shoulders.*

**Richard:** If those Aycayi hear word of this- this murder, they might try t' kill you, n' kill us all! They'll ambush us in the night, take our sabres, chop our heads off, n' loot our earnings!

**Richard:** Listen Edward, we needs t' get at 'em first! Make 'em pay, wit' either their gold or their lives!

*Edward removes Richard's hands from his shoulders.*

**Edward:** Their lives?! If ye plan for the Aycayi's blood t' be shed, I won't hesitate t' mutiny.

**Richard:** Mutiny?! Do you not trust me, after all these years?!

*Edward and Richard stare at each other for two minutes, before Richard walks away.*

**Richard:** So be it. Godspeed.

*****Brajan Nowicki*****

*Brajan Nowicki enters a public house, soon walking over to a table with a limp. Sitting at a table across from him is Edward Murdoch with a map in front of him.*

**Edward:** You! You are... Nowicki?

*Brajan nods.*

**Brajan:** It's been too long, sir.

*Silence for ten minutes, Brajan is given a drink.*

**Brajan:** What's become of the captain? You seen 'em since?

*After a moment, Edward Murdoch breaks down into tears.*

**Edward:** No, no I haven't.

*Brajan walks over to Edward, before looking at the map on Edward's table. The map depicts SCP-9561, and nearby islands.*

**Brajan:** Is that a map of that bastard's isle?

*Moments pass without a response.*

**Brajan:** Can- Can I have it?

*Edward slides the map forward. Brajan brings the map to his table, gets out some parchment paper, and begins to copy the map.*

**Addendum 9561-A-N-2:**

> The following 'memory' items were found obscured behind the bookshelves of chamber N:

*****[NOMENCLATURE ABSENT]*****

*[NOMENCLATURE ABSENT] is crawling through the tunnels of SCP-9561-A when it hears a loud scream and runs towards the sound of the noise. It approaches a clearing with a pool of water, Richard Magni holding his sword, and the body of an Aycayi with a large slash across her chest.*

**Richard:** That'll teach ye... Huh?

*Richard turns to face [NOMENCLATURE ABSENT], he sheathes his bloodied sword.*

**Richard:** [NOMENCLATURE ABSENT], was it? Good afternoon. Clean this up, will ye?

**[NOMENCLATURE ABSENT]:** Captain... What did you do to her?

**Richard:** Hm? It's rather obvious.

**[NOMENCLATURE ABSENT]:** Then why?!

**Richard:** Take a look o'er here, I'll show ye why.

*Richard waves [NOMENCLATURE ABSENT] closer to the pool of water. The Aycayi woman grasps at Richard's leg but is swiftly shaken off. Richard Magni points the sword at the pool of water, pointing out a tunnel below the surface.*

**Richard:** See that? A secret tunnel goes all the way into these caves from the sea, right t' the treasure. I found this one trying t' crawl out o' the pool, the wretched thief she is.

*[NOMENCLATURE ABSENT] backs up. Richard Magni turns to stare at [NOMENCLATURE ABSENT], then grabs it by the shoulder.*

**Richard:** What? Did I not ask ye t' clean this up?

**Richard:** Or... Do ye think 'tis a murder? You think... She didn't deserve to die?

*Several seconds of silence.*

**Richard:** Don't go silent on me now, lad. This cave is what's owed to me, my property, to do what I want with.

**Richard:** It's mine, and you-

*Richard Magni raises his sword into the air.*

**Richard:** -Have the stink of treachery on ye.

*****Brajan Nowicki*****

*Brajan Nowicki lies flat against sand, raising his head. The sands of SCP-9561 are littered with the dead or dying bodies of humans and Aycayi. Crawling on his hands, Brajan comes across Richard Magni in a fight with Caonabo.*

**Richard:** Ye should 'ave taken me offer while ye could!

**Caonabo:** No chief would ever sell out their own isle for mere metals. Yahíma put her trust in you, Magni!

**Richard:** This trust be lost, ye can blame yer own!

**Caonabo:** My own?! We have been nothing but gracious!

**Richard:** Stealin' MY treasure, infiltratin' MY caves! Be thankful ye were given an opportunity fer mercy!

*Caonabo jumps back.*

**Caonabo:** All this over your useless gold?! For coins, you would kill thousands?!

**Richard:** Shut yer trap! Ye've no idea how much I've fought t' protect this. That 'useless gold' means everything to me!

**Richard:** Even if I wished t' leave MY island, thar's nary a Federacy to protect me!

*A minute passes, Caonabo lays his weapon on the ground.*

**Caonabo:** If you wish to stay here on this island, it is increasingly clear I cannot stop you.

*Caonabo raises his hands, then yells in Taíno.*

**Caonabo:** <May our blood purify these sands, and may our bones mark our grave!>

**Richard:** At least speak English when you threaten me!

**Caonabo:** <To me!>

*The ground shakes. Several injured Aycayi stand up from the ground and approach Caonabo. Yahíma, with several cuts along her legs and a slash across her stomach, glances at Richard Magni while standing at Caonabo's side. All of the Aycayi seem to be in a trance, murmuring, and placing their hands on Caonabo.*

*Richard Magni pulls out a pistol and aims it at the group.*

**Richard:** Now that yer all here, leave.

*A minute passes by. Caonabo falls forward onto the ground, and the Aycayi around him bury his body in the sand.*

**Richard:** I said, leave!

*Richard Magni fires his gun into the air. The Aycayi do not react to it, then walk away towards the ocean.*

*Richard stares at the injured pirates on the beaches, then leaves towards SCP-9561-A. Brajan Nowicki reels in pain and raises a hand towards Richard Magni, before falling unconscious.*

*****First Officer Edward Murdoch*****

*Edward Murdoch carries a pistol in his hand, moving through SCP-9561-A until reaching a chamber resembling chamber Q.*

**Edward:** I know ye're in here somewhere.

*Edward moves over to a portrait of Richard Magni, placing a hand on the face of the portrait before pushing the portrait away to reveal a short passageway into a decorated chamber.*

**Edward:** Always a vain one.

*Richard Magni is sitting at a desk, counting coins at a pile of gold in front of him, when he notices Edward Murdoch and smiles.*

**Richard:** You! Ye came back!

*Edward lowers his weapon and looks around the room. There are several piles of treasure and a bucket collecting water dripping into the chamber.*

**Richard:** Do ye have any food, Murdoch?

**Edward:** There is... Some on my ship, but why? There be plenty of trees here that bear fruit, and fish at the shores-

**Richard:** Aye, that may be so... But the Aycayi, I see them! Seals frequent these shores, day aft day, hour aft hour, watchin' me!

**Edward:** I didn' see any when I docked.

**Richard:** How could ye not notice!? Their eyes are like lights, skewerin' through the void t' match mine! Now, I want you to retrieve your ship's grub 'n bring it t' me afore they catch wind o' yer presence!

**Edward:** Then what have you eaten all these years?

**Richard:** Whatever it takes t' survive; bats, crabs, I- This- this doesn't matter! Bring yer food here immediately, Edward! Please! I'm starving, and me crew's all gone!

*Edward Murdoch walks towards the desk, then looks at a map of SCP-9561. He grabs the map, then turns away. Richard struggles to stand up from his chair.*

**Richard:** Huh- what! Where are you going?!

**Edward:** I cannae believe I ever used to admire you.

**Richard:** Get back here! Bring that back, ye hear me!?

*Edward leaves the chamber and walks through several tunnels, Richard's voice echoes.*

**Richard:** BASTARD!

**Richard:** TRAITOROUS WELP! COME BACK HERE!

**Richard:** YOU'D LEAVE A MAN TO STARVE?! HAVE YE NO SHAME?

**Richard:** I BET- I BET YE WANT TO STEAL FROM MY COLD DEAD HANDS!

**Richard:** You'll never! I won't let you! Drown in the bloody pits of HELL you...

*Richard's voice fades out, as Edward Murdoch nears the exit of SCP-9561-A.*

*****Captain Richard Gosnold Magni*****

*Richard sits in a chair in his chamber, body severely atrophied. He carefully picks up a gold coin and eats it. As he is picking up another coin, he drops it, and lets out a low gasp.*
