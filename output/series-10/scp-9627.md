**Item #:** SCP-9627

**Object Class:** Safe

**Special Containment Procedures:** Upon manifestation SCP-9627 instances are to be divided into two sets: Standard and Nonstandard using the following procedure.

Initial set.

]

Place polygons exhibiting self intersection in the Nonstandard set.

Place irregular polygons in the Nonstandard set.

Standard set.

]

Nonstandard set.

 The Nonstandard set is to be discarded after sorting.

**Description:** SCP-9627 describes a set of plastic tiles that manifest daily at 7pm in the break room of Site-627. This set will contain 9 to 24 plastic tiles with a varying number of sides and varying dimensions. These tiles will remain in the room for approximately 2 hours before they de-manifest at around 9pm.

Approximately ~20% of tiles manifest as nonstandard tiles. Nonstandard tiles are tiles that manifest exhibiting self intersection or irregular side lengths. If these tiles are not separated from the standard tiles and discarded before de-manifestation there will be an increased number of nonstandard tiles in the next manifestation.

SCP-9627 after 3 days of non-containment.
