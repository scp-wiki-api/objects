**Item #:** SCP-9692

###990000|**Level 1/9692**##

**Object Class:** Safe

###990000|**Classified**##

---

![](https://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/local--files/scp-9692/home.jpg)

 

An entrance along Site-72 Perimeter A. Individuals affected by SCP-9692 prefer to attempt ingress at entrances with easy terrain and pleasant scenery if available.

---

**Special Containment Procedures:** Individuals susceptible to SCP-9692 are to be monitored by the Department of Surveillance's sAccade.aic and tagged as Level 3 (mild/moderate) Veil threat. Further action to maintain the Veil is to be taken with case-by-case evaluation.

Affected individuals who reach a Foundation Site are to be admitted, briefed by a consultant, and presented with their options. Those electing re-incorporation as D-Class personnel are not to be exposed to anomalies with mnestic effects.

This document is assigned summer reading for Foundation Academy students entering Year 6..Addenda in this document were chosen by scribboleth.aic for maximum aid in comprehension. Full collection of related records available at SCiPNET terminals.

**Description:** SCP-9692 is a behavioral phenomenon affecting resigned, defected, or discharged Foundation personnel who have been administered Class F amnestics and introduced to mundane society under Naturalization Protocol-I76. Persons affected by the phenomenon attempt to return to a Foundation Site despite total supplantation of memory relating to the Foundation and the location of its facilities.

While at first investigated as a defect in the Class F amnestic, the phenomenon has since been reclassified as an anomaly due to its exclusive presence in lineaged ex-Foundation personnel. The behavior that characterizes SCP-9692 does not manifest in amnesticized civilians, members of anomalous Groups of Interest, or first-generation employees.
