**Item:** SCP-9772

**Ocject class:** safe

**Special Containment Procedures:**  Currently, both SCP-9772-A and SCP-9772-B are contained in a room measuring 3 m × 4 m × 5 m, which is equipped with a bathroom and a kitchen, as well as all standard human necessities, including furniture, household appliances, and Wi-Fi. However, both SCP-9772-A and SCP-9772-B are permitted to roam freely throughout the facility, as this has proven beneficial to staff and Class D personnel throughout Site-19, and as a precautionary measure to prevent any confusion regarding the meanings of buttons and electronic panels based on color during SCP-9772-A-1 and SCP-9772-B-2 events, labels have been affixed to the buttons within the facility.

**Description:** SCP-9772 consists of two entities that differ in form, behavior, and, to some extent, anomalous phenomena; they share no genetic relationship, but a relationship of “love” is observed between them.

**SCP-9772-A:** A memetic-physical entity representing the total sum of negative energy and dark energy, and the concept of “darkness” in the form of a human boy who appears to be of Caucasian origin, 18 years old, 1.91 m tall, and weighing 65 kg, with the physique of an average person. He has slightly pale skin, black hair, and eyes with deep black pupils. He exhibits signs of depression; he is silent, sad, gloomy, and emotionally vacant overall, and shows no desire for significant physical activity. Nevertheless, he has demonstrated intellectual prowess, particularly outstanding abilities in physics, mathematics, quantum physics, and space technology, as well as a scientific and philosophical interest in the meaning and origin of “darkness.” It appears that SCP-9772-A possesses an involuntary ability to change the color of clothing to black.

Medical examinations have confirmed that SCP-9772-A’s body is effectively dead; all body cells are dead, and 25% of the internal organs are in an initial state of decomposition. There is no heartbeat, and the brain cells are dead and severely damaged. Furthermore, Preliminary examinations indicate that SCP-9772-A’s body is covered in bruises, first-degree burns in some areas, and even partially open wounds. The blood is a viscous, blackish-red substance; due to the blood’s viscosity, it cannot flow from wounds unless they are wide and deep. This anomaly is accompanied by no change in the blood volume; it appears that these blood cells do not belong to a specific blood type, as the cells are dead yet simultaneously functional, and due to the nature of the blood, the body lacks an immune system and is devoid of any pain receptors. It is noted that despite these factors, SCP-9772-A itself does not report any pain; rather, it possesses normal physical capabilities and mental abilities that surpass those of an average human.

Subject SCP-9772-A appears to have the ability to control light, both voluntarily and involuntarily. When the subject enters a lit area, the brightness of the lights decreases by approximately 26% to 87%. The subject can also control the level of darkness in a space—from making it so dark and dim that lights fail to function to slightly lifting the darkness—meaning the subject has the ability to partially control darkness. To date, scientists do not know the cause of this phenomenon.

**SCP-9772-A-1:** This is a random and involuntary phenomenon for the subject itself. It begins by creating an invisible dome with a radius of up to 3 m. Anything that enters the dome shifts from its normal colors to shades of gray and black, with a 75% reduction in brightness. The dome has been shown to absorb 80% of light and 95.7% of the seven primary color spectra.

**SCP-9772-B:** A memetic-physical entity representing the totality of positive energy and the spectrum of colors, and the concept of "light" in the form of a human girl who appears to be of Caucasian origin, 18 years old, 1.89 m tall, and 58 kg in weight. She is characterized by multicolored irises and blonde hair, and appears to suffer from a form of hyperactivity; cheerful, carefree, somewhat talkative, innocent, and positive. She has demonstrated an active and positive demeanor toward the public, shown an ability to treat depression, and expressed an interest in the concept of “light,” botany, and photosynthesis, as well as her interest in how to improve people’s mental well-being. It appears that SCP-9772-B has the ability to randomly and chaotically alter the color pigments of clothing in terms of both patterns and hues; however, most clothing turns white and cool yellow with some random colors.

From a medical examination perspective, the subject appears to have no marks or bruises on her body; however, it was observed that her heart beats at a rate of 587 beats per minute, which explains the composition of her blood, characterized by its reddish-pink and light red color, and its texture, which resembles oil in a certain way. Furthermore, the blood’s flow velocity within her body is approximately 2,806 m/s, which causes even the smallest wound to bleed profusely, while the total blood volume in her body remains constant. It appears that her blood cells contain all blood types (A+, A-, B+, B-, O+, O-, AB+, AB-, C+, C-); All organs, without exception, are functioning at levels exceeding their normal capacity, yet the subject has not reported any pain.

Subject SCP-9772-B appears to have the ability to control light levels up to 99% brightness, even when the lights are off. If the subject enters a dark room, the room will begin to emit light out of nowhere. The subject appears to have the ability to control light—its emission and distribution—that is, partial control over light, and no one knows why.

**SCP-9772-B-2:** is a random and involuntary phenomenon of the subject itself. It begins by creating an invisible dome with a radius of up to 3 m. Anything that enters the dome shifts from its normal colors to a mixture of colors dominated by white and light yellow, with illumination increasing to 75%. The dome has been shown to reflect 80% of light and 95.7% of the seven primary color spectra.

The nature of SCP-9772-C has not yet been determined, but the closest concept scientists have arrived at is:

**SCP-9772-C:** is an anomalous external agent of unknown origin, structure, or form, considered the primary cause of the complete immortality of both SCP-9772-A and SCP-9772-B. It has the ability to preserve physical structure despite damage to the body; in other words, the SCP-9772-C anomaly is the primary factor sustaining the lives of both subjects despite the absence of limbs and/or organs. The anomaly initiates cellular regeneration to restore the body to its normal state, beginning immediately after the removal of up to 5% of the body, and in specific cases, the anomaly operates in a quantum manner. When SCP-9772-A and SCP-9772-B face any inevitable lethal threat (radiation, all sharp weapons and destructive weapons, anomalies from other SCP entities, extreme heat, hallucinations, memetic entities… ). It is unknown how SCP-9772-C is activated in these specific situations; it is likened to a sensor within each body capable of detecting and activating or deactivating itself in known specific or general situations, Or, as some scientists have said, [“a luminous and dark external force”](“a luminous and dark external force”)

The anomaly has not been generally defined, but certain factors and symptoms affecting subjects SCP-9772-A and SCP-9772-B are known, including:

**For SCP-9772-A:**

* A drop in temperature to 0 degrees Celsius.

* The appearance of a black aura around the subject’s body.

* A decrease in the brightness of all lights within a radius of 6 meters.

* An increase in SCP-9772-A’s control over darkness and gloom.

**Regarding SCP-9772-B:**

* Temperature rises to 43 degrees Celsius.

* A luminous white halo appears around the subject’s body.

* The brightness of all lights within a 6-meter radius increases.

* SCP-9772-B’s control over light increases.

<summary>+ Open Document 9772-1</summary>

Document 9772-1: Interview between Dr. "Alex Bayer," the SCP-9772 Case Officer, and SCP-9772-A:

***Start of log***

Dr. Alex Bayer enters and sits at the desk across from SCP-9772-A. She looks at him, but he tries to avoid eye contact.

**Alex              :** Hello 

**SCP-9772-A:** Hi

*Three minutes of silence pass between the two parties*

**Alex              :** What’s your name?

**SCP-9772-A:** Mahdi

**Alex              :** Okay, do you know where you are right now?

**SCP-9772-A:** A kind of organization, isn’t it?

**Alex              :** You’re right. *sighs* I’ll be honest with you—we’ve detected some anomalous phenomena that you’ve caused, either directly or indirectly, and that’s why you’ll be detained here so we can analyze those phenomena.

**SCP-9772-A:** good

**Alex:** @@*Surprised*@@ Aren’t you surprised?

**SCP-9772-A:** @@*Silent*@@

**Alex:** Okay, let me ask you a question: What’s your daily life like?

**SCP-9772-A:** Normal.

**Alex              :** Explain further.

**SCP-9772-A:** I live like any ordinary person—the same boring daily routine. My girlfriend annoys me a little, too. As for me, trading is enough of a job; it meets my needs.

**Alex              :** @@*Writing in a notebook*@@ I have another question: What are your interests?

**SCP-9772-A:** What do you mean?

**Alex              :** I mean, what do you try to do or explore in your free time?

**SCP-9772-A:** Mathematics and calculus, science, astrophysics—including general Riemannian geometry, the study of black holes, as well as quantum superposition, particle division, and quantum interactions.

**Alex:** Interesting. What else?

**SCP-9772-A:** @@*Deep sigh*@@

**Alex              :** Is something wrong?

**SCP-9772-A: _Darkness_

**Alex              :** Darkness?

**SCP-9772-A:** _Darkness, everything about it_ 

**Alex              :** Everything

**SCP-9772-A:** I’ve always studied these things just for its sake.

**Alex              :** What draws you to the darkness?

**SCP-9772-A:** Everything, it’s like “illogic” within logic: its color, its concept, and everything else.

**Alex              :** Okay.

**SCP-9772-A:** I’ve always felt connected to the darkness, but I don’t care about it.

**Alex              :** Okay, what about your friend?

**SCP-9772-A:** She’s the only one I know.

**Alex              :** Okay, let’s end our discussion here.

**SCP-9772-A:** Are you going to talk to her?

**Alex              :** Yes, why? Is she dangerous?

**SCP-9772-A:** Nothing.

***End of log***

Document 9772-2: Interview between Dr. "Alex Bayer," the SCP-9772 Case Officer, and SCP-9772-B:

***Start of log***

Dr. Alex Bayer enters and sits down at the desk across from SCP-9772-B. She notes that the subject is very lively and gives her an innocent, positive look.

**Alex              :** Hello

**SCP-9777-B:** Hello, ma’am. It’s a pleasure to meet you. My name is Farah. I hope we can be friends. @@*smiles*@@

**Alex              :** It’s a pleasure to meet you too. @@*She takes a notebook out of her pocket and begins writing.*@@

**SCP-9772-B:** What’s that? A notebook? What are you writing in it? I think that’s information about me? @@*looks puzzled*@@

**Alex              :** Yes, I’m trying to study you.

**SCP-9772-B:** Wait a minute, @@*thinks*@@, maybe I have a disease I don’t know about.

**Alex              :** That’s not true. Listen to me carefully. We’ve observed some anomalous phenomena occurring in you, and we’re going to try to study and analyze those phenomena.

**SCP-9772-B:** @@*Expresses joy*@@ “Great!” I’m going to be the center of attention.

**Alex              :** Good, good. How about a joke?

**SCP-9772-B:** A joke? Really!? I want to hear it, I want to hear it. Wait—is this part of my study? *laughs* Never mind—as long as the joke is funny.

**Alex              :** Once, a man went to the doctor and told him:

“Doctor, I have a problem! Every time I drink tea, I feel a pain in my eyes.”

The doctor replied, “Have you tried taking the spoon out of the cup before you drink?”

(Approximately 1 minute and 23 seconds of continuous laughter, accompanied by tears, has been edited out)

**Alex              :** Did you like the joke?

**SCP-9772-B:** Oh my goodness, oh my goodness, I’ve never heard a joke this funny in my whole life.

**Alex              :** I’m glad you laughed. Now I’m going to ask you a question: what’s your lifestyle like?

**SCP-9772-B:** You know, it’s a long story. I wake up in the morning and make breakfast, then I kiss my best friend on his very, very wet cheek to wake him up. Our breakfast is somewhat varied, but not that much. After that, I go for a walk in search of an adventure to embark on. I help people and pet animals. Well, I can’t describe my day exactly, because it’s a big day full of amazing events. Once I rescued a cat from a tree. Oh, oh, I just remembered once I stopped a thief with my superhuman speed. I sprinted toward him, and then, boom! I knocked him to the ground, and everyone thanked me for it. I’m a daring heroine, what days those were! Oh, girl, I still remember that day, I worked with the garbage collectors for a whole day and saw the beauty of the city. And I still remember one day when I got lost in the forest; poor Mahdi was looking for me everywhere while I was lying with the squirrels in the green meadows, And in the evening, I’d be so, so, so tired that I’d suddenly fall asleep standing up, or most of the time I’d fall asleep on my friend Mahdi’s shoulder, and he’d gently lay me down on the bed like a baby. Hehehe, it’s the best feeling. @@*A brief moment of silence*@@—Wait, did I go on too long?

**Alex             :** No, no, that’s great. Keep going.

**SCP-9772-B:** Oh, oh. As for my boyfriend Mahdi, my eternal love he might seem boring, but deep down he’s a caring person who thinks of others more than himself. How could I forget our first meeting? I was tired in the park, so I fell asleep on one of the benches, and he told me he liked to sit alone in the park and listen to music. He’s an introvert, so he used to hate people and hated sitting with them, but eventually he sat down near me, He saw me fast asleep, and sunset had fallen. Since I was a little tall but somewhat slight, Mahdi knew it was dangerous for me to sleep there, so I continued sleeping while he carried me all the way across the park, I was in such a deep sleep that I was drooling on his hands, and I woke up in his arms. Honestly *she blushes*, I didn’t expect this. At first, there was a slight barrier between us. I wanted to talk to him, and he wanted to talk to me, but I broke that barrier and was the one who started the conversation. It turned out he suffers from social anxiety and depression, despite his good looks, so I resolved to help him overcome his condition. And since I knew I had hyperactivity, we were going to make a good pair.

***Alex              : That’s a long and interesting story, but what’s the point of telling me all this?

**SCP-9772-B:** We’re friends, remember?

**Alex             :** That’s very kind of you. Okay, let’s continue. What do you do in your free time, aside from your adventures?

**SCP-9772-B:** _Light and its effects._ I’ve tried to understand light, not physically, but on a _broader level in terms of how it shapes the universe._ How can light have the power to sustain plant life? Light, in all its forms, is the source of art, pigments, and life; it is the foundation of the entire universe. So I asked myself, “How can light possess these abilities even though it’s just tiny, beautiful particles?” And I’m still asking that question.

**Alex              :** Very good. Well, we’ve finished our session.

**SCP-9772-B:** What?!?! So soon? I still want to talk about something important and understand your feelings.

**Alex              :** No problem, we’ll talk more later.

**SCP-9772-B:** They told me there’s accommodations for me and my beloved; I’m eager to see them.

**Alex              :** @@*Small laugh*@@ Okay. Bye!

**SCP-9772-B:** Goodbye, It was a pleasure meeting you. Take care of yourself.

***End of log***
