**Special Containment Procedures:** As no other organizations are known to have successfully received SCP-9779 and future broadcasts will be blocked by the effects of Project Chelone, no containment procedures are necessary. Research is to instead focus on explaining the consequences of Project Chelone to the public.

**Description:** SCP-9779 was an anomalous broadcast received by the Foundation on 21/10/2026. This broadcast could be received even if the laws of physics are radically different between the sending location and the receiving one. These properties also prevented it from being detected by non-anomalous equipment, thus preventing civilian access to the broadcast. A translation of the first part of the message is provided below.

= **Translation of SCP-9779 contents**

---

You are not the first iteration of this universe, and will likely not be the last. We were not the first to send this message. You are in grave danger. The foundations of the universe are not stable and can change without notice. This will change the laws of nature to something incompatible with life as you know it, in a sphere expanding as fast as your laws of nature allow. Once this reaches your planet, you and all you know will be destroyed. You might have already calculated that the risk of this happening is negligible, but if you have received this message, you probably already know not everything in the universe can be calculated so easily. Some things cannot be explained in any system of physics, and these things can make the astronomically improbable vastly more likely. We have compiled everything we and our predecessors have tried to stop this cycle. We can only hope you are in [UNTRANSLATABLE]'sPresumed to be the name of a deity. favor and will be the ones to break the cycle and find a working solution.

---

The Foundation's Department of Theoretical Physics has identified the phenomenon described in the broadcast as false vacuum decay, a nonanomalous process that occurs when a quantum fieldA field that permeates the universe and gives rise to a particular force or particle, such as electromagnetism or down quarks. randomly reaches a lower, more stable energy state. This produces a knockon effect where the change spreads throughout the field, and thus the universe, at the speed of light, producing an expanding bubble whose interior has completely different laws of physics to the outside universe. As described in the message, the risk of this happening is believed to be negligible, but the contents of the message strongly imply the existence of anomalies increases the chance of a decay event occurring.

The rest of the broadcast is split into three parts. The first describes what these previous civilizations have tried to avert their destruction, such as isolating the decay event in a pocket dimension, injecting large amounts of matter into the affected region of space to collapse it into a black hole or using [REDACTED] to reverse the flow of time in the affected region. No evidence has been discovered that any of these attempts have succeeded.

The second contains information on the culture and history of several previous civilizations, including several audio and video recordings. Translations of two of these recordings are included below.

The final part of the broadcast contains instructions for creating two anomalous machines. The first is a device capable of sending messages regardless of differences in the laws of physics, with instructions to use it to pass on the message with the addition of anything tried by the current civilization to ensure their survival. The other is capable of detecting instances of vacuum decay instantly, regardless of distance from the device.

**Addendum:** On 2/11/2026, the O5 Council granted permission to build the devices described in SCP-9779 , designated SCP-9779-Alpha and SCP-9779-Beta respectively. Construction was completed on 17/12/2026 and SCP-9779-Beta was activated for the first time on 19/12/2026. A decay event was detected approximately one lightyear from Earth. 

#### NOTICE FROM THE O5 COUNCIL

SCP-9779-Beta has revealed an existential risk to humanity's continued existence in the form of the false vacuum decay event detected on 19/12/2026. Any Foundation staff with suggestions on how to counter this event are encouraged to come forward.

Proposed solution

Outcome

Description

Use of eigenweapons to retroactively erase decay event from existence

Failure

While some of the bubble was successfully erased, no Foundation eigenweapon is powerful enough to erase the entire event in the time available. It has been theorized erasing the origin point of the event would completely erase it, but this point has yet to be located.

Use of [REDACTED] to retroactively erase the concept of decay events from existence

N/A

Rejected by the O5 Council. The contents of SCP-9779 strongly imply the current iteration of the universe only exists due to a decay event in a previous one. Attempting to retroactively erase this would likely destroy humanity.

Teleport Earth a large distance from the event

N/A

This would require at minimum teleporting the Sun and Earth and all the space in between. The Foundation does not possess any means of safely teleporting such a large volume. Temporally displace decay event into the distant future

Failure

No visible effect. It has been suggested this means time behaves differently inside the bubble.

**Chambers:** But is it really better? Eventually we'd use up all our resources and then-

**Chambers:** We are *not* presenting that to the O5s.

**Moon:** ...moving on, let's say you're right and humanity eventually gives in to infighting. Are you to blame for sapient nature?

**Closing Statement:** On 3/12/2027, Dr. Chambers' proposal was put to vote by the O5 Council. The measure was passed unanimously. SCP-9779-Alpha was then used to pass on SCP-9779 as instructed. Moon requested a copy of themself be uploaded to the broadcast before it was sent, claiming they wanted to "give pep talks to any future scientists who need it". This was permitted as they have been cooperative with Foundation interests.

By 12/12/2027, the delivery of dark energy around the solar system was complete. Shortly after, civilians began noticing stars disappearing from the sky as their light could no longer pass the expanding space between them and Earth. The Foundation's additions to the message can be viewed below.

= **Foundation additions to SCP-9779-Alpha**

We believe we have discovered a solution to this threat. We have included the schematics of the devices used at the end of this message. We used one to produce dark energy to hasten the expansion of the universe and the other to deliver it. Our intended goal was to isolate the bubble that threatened us from the wider universe, but we could not produce sufficient dark energy in time. Instead, we isolated ourselves, preventing the bubble from reaching us but at the cost of forever cutting ourselves off from the rest of the universe. Whatever gods you may believe in willing, you may have better luck than we did. Godspeed.
