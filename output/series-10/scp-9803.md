**Special Containment Procedures:** All viable samples of SCP-9803 are to be stored in triple-sealed cryogenic containment vials within Area-██. Access is restricted to personnel with Level 3 Security Clearance or higher. All handling and experimentation involving SCP-9803 must be conducted under BSL-4 containment protocols and requires written authorization from two Senior Researchers and the Site Director.

Any confirmed SCP-9803 must result in a full lockdown of the facility. Exposed personnel are to be quarantined immediately and monitored continuously. Individuals exhibiting symptoms beyond Stage II (see Description) are to be transferred to reinforced humanoid containment chambers. Foundation personnel are authorized to terminate infected subjects if containment integrity cannot be maintained.

Foundation assets embedded within the Global Occult Coalition are responsible for monitoring reports of unusually violent rabies outbreaks, unexplained neurological syndromes, or mass aggression events consistent with SCP-9803 infection. Civilian awareness is to be suppressed through coordinated disinformation campaigns attributing incidents to mutated rabies variants, synthetic narcotics, or episodes of mass hysteria.

Individuals reaching Stage III infection are designated as SCP-9803-1.

**Description:** SCP-9803 is an anomalous strain of the rabies virus exhibiting neurological and physiological properties significantly exceeding those of any naturally occurring Lyssavirus. Transmission occurs primarily through infected saliva via bites, scratches, or contact between infected bodily fluids and exposed mucous membranes.

Unlike conventional rabies, SCP-9803 does not induce rapid systemic failure. Instead, the pathogen selectively restructures the host’s central nervous system, progressively eliminating higher cognitive function while preserving motor coordination, sensory perception, and instinctive predatory behaviors.

The resulting organism remains biologically alive but demonstrates profound neurological degeneration.

Infected hosts, designated SCP-9803-1, exhibit:

* Extreme aggression toward uninfected mammals.

* Near-complete absence of fear or self-preservation.

* Dramatically reduced pain perception.

* Persistent compulsive biting behavior.

* Increased physical endurance resulting from suppressed fatigue and pain responses.

Despite catastrophic cortical degradation, SCP-9803-1 instances retain sufficient motor function to navigate complex environments, pursue prey over extended distances, climb obstacles, and coordinate with nearby infected individuals through primitive social behavior.

**Infection Progression**

**Stage I — Incubation (1–5 Hours)**

Subjects experience low-grade fever, headache, anxiety, and heightened irritability.

Functional MRI imaging reveals rapid viral colonization of the limbic system and brainstem despite minimal outward symptoms.

**Stage II — Behavioral Degradation (6–12 Hours)**

Subjects develop:

* Hyper-salivation

* Aggression

* Increasingly erratic motor activity

* Compulsive attempts to bite nearby organisms

Higher reasoning deteriorates rapidly, although subjects retain enough cognitive ability to pursue targets, avoid obstacles, and manipulate simple objects.

Attempts at verbal communication become fragmented and incoherent.

**Stage III — Neurological Collapse (13+ Hours)**

Subjects lose virtually all higher brain function.

Instances demonstrate coordinated pack behavior, relentless pursuit of nearby humans, and a complete absence of fear responses.

Neurological examination shows widespread necrosis throughout the cerebral cortex while the cerebellum, basal ganglia, hypothalamus, and portions of the brainstem remain functionally intact. This selective preservation allows continued locomotion, balance, feeding behavior, and primitive learning despite the destruction of conscious reasoning.

SCP-9803-1 instances remain biologically alive and metabolically active. Cellular respiration, circulation, and organ function continue normally. Without access to food or water, however, subjects typically succumb to dehydration, malnutrition, organ failure, or secondary infection within three to six weeks.

**Addendum 9803-A:** SCP-9803 first came to Foundation attention following a cluster of violent attacks in █████████, India.

Local authorities reported multiple civilians exhibiting extreme aggression while continuing to pursue victims despite sustaining injuries that would normally incapacitate a human subject.

Mobile Task Force Beta-7 (“Maz Hatters”) was deployed under the guise of an international hazardous disease response.

Seventeen infected individuals were neutralized, while three living subjects were successfully contained for study.

Genomic sequencing revealed extensive artificial modifications inconsistent with any known Lyssavirus, suggesting SCP-9803 is either an engineered pathogen or the product of an unknown anomalous evolutionary process. No organization or individual has yet claimed responsibility for its creation.

**Addendum 9803-B:** No known antiviral therapy has demonstrated measurable effectiveness against SCP-9803. Standard rabies post-exposure prophylaxis Post-Exposure Prophylaxis is a series of vaccines and immunoglobulin injections to treat potential rabies exposure and prevent the infection from reaching the central nervous system. , including human rabies immunoglobulin and vaccination, has failed in 100% of documented exposure cases, even when administered within minutes of infection.

Experimental antiviral compounds, induced hypothermia, broad-spectrum immunotherapy, and surgical excision of bite sites have likewise proven ineffective.

Current Foundation consensus holds that SCP-9803 becomes neurologically irreversible almost immediately following successful transmission.
