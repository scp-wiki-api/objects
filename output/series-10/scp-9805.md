**Item #:** SCP-9805

**Object Class:** [*https://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/scp-9336 Euclid]

**Special Containment Procedures:** Photos of the SCP-9805 event are held in a small plastic bag within the [Site-19](SCP-173 ) containment lockers.

**Description:** SCP-9805 was an entity which briefly manifested during an unrelated scientific project. No other materializations of SCP-9805 have been recorded.

The [*https://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/scp-9607 photo reel] containing evidence of the apparition was first located by the US Military following abnormal reporting emerging from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. A subsequent investigative raid revealed a constructed anechoic chamber, used to conduct the experiments which first discovered SCP-9805. 

According to the research team, an individual was placed within the chamber and ordered to take a series of 12 photos over the course of approximately 3 minutes, each within an evenly spaced duration. Reportedly, no abnormal events occurred until the photos were developed, at which point the figure was first noticed.  Despite this, all participating in the event reported a loss of notable retrospective coherency, rendering them unable to properly recall logical transpirations during the period. 

Further interviews with the involved staff would confirm the following details: Paraphrased directly from witness accounts, minimizing outside interpretation or intervention. 

* SCP-9805 had a physical presence. 

* SCP-9805 had no noticeable smell. 

* SCP-9805 had no noticeable taste. 

* SCP-9805 did not attempt physical interaction.

* SCP-9805 produced noticeable sound.

* SCP-9805 produced the faint sound of chattering teeth. 

All evidence would be seized, including the [*https://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/scp-9853 chamber used] in the original study. Later attempts at replication would fail to reproduce the event. 

The photographs themselves display no identifiable anomalous properties. Many viewers report the pictures bring them a strong sense of calm.
