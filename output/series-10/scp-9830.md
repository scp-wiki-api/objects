**Item #:** SCP-9830

**Object Class:** Nonexistent

**Special Containment Procedures:** SCP-9830 does not exist, and therefore containment is unneccessary. Any individual or group who states the contrary is to be tracked, located, and terminated by order of the O5 Council.

**Description:** SCP-9830 does not exist.

**Addendum 9830-1:** A series of actions taken in response to statements of SCP-9830’s existence, by order of the O5 Council.
