**Item #:** SCP-9908

**Object Class:** Contained

**Special Containment Procedures:** In storage. Use outside of testing is under consideration.

**Description:** Recovered by local authorities in Soddy-Daisy, Tennessee upon receiving a report of several youths smoking in public. All but one managed to escape before apprehension; during questioning, the youth stated that the group was smoking a folding table, rather than tobacco or any illicit substance. He then demonstrated SCP-9908's properties to justify his claim. The object was subsequently confiscated.

SCP-9908 is a package of Marfil brand rolling papers. When emptied and left unobserved for two hours, it will refill with a fresh supply of rolling papers. Its primary property, however, is that any solid material can be used to fill a cigarette made with SCP-9908's papers, regardless of its material properties. This is done by applying one of the papers to an object, a portion of which will crumble into the paper.

Materials harvested with SCP-9908 can be smoked to produce a physical and/or mental effect typical of a drug. In the case of substances that are already commonly consumed in this fashion (such as tobacco), the effects of doing so are as expected; in other cases, the effects have little relation to the expected consequences of igniting and inhaling the substance in question, and no obvious pattern has emerged.

While many substances produce pleasurable effects when smoked, none (other than conventional addictive substances) have been shown to have addictive effects. Substances that would normally be highly toxic or even fatal can be smoked without permanent effect. All known effects are transient, lasting no more than two hours.

As SCP-9908's crumbling and self-restocking properties are adequately understood, current research is focused on testing the effects of smoking various substances.

### Testing Data Sequence 9908-D

---

**Foreword:** As tests with SCP-9908 have shown consistent effects between subjects, and with the same substance across multiple tests, the recommended testing regimen is to have one subject smoke each test substance exactly once. The current selected test subject for SCP-9908 is [ C. Wynn](scp-9508 ); if she is not available, another test subject who can competently roll cigarettes is preferred.
