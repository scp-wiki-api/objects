**Item #:** SCP-9916

**Object Class:** Safe

**Special Containment Procedures:** SCP-9916 requires no specialised containment procedures besides being held in a secure location. The composition of the location does not matter. There has only been a single breach of containment procedures concerning SCP-9916 (see Incident-9916-A).

**Description:** SCP-9916 is held at Site-18. It appears as a somewhat-functioning BIC Cristal pen lacking a lid, about a quarter full with black ink. Upon attempting to write with SCP-9916, it will only work intermittently.

SCP-9916 has been found to cause the possessor anomalous amounts of, what might usually be referred to as, 'bad luck'. This bad luck can vary wildly, ranging from stubbing a toe all the way to death by what appears to be bad luck, usually though far overcomplicated manners. During testing, this bad luck has been found to be limited to purely human possessors, with non-humans suffering no anomalous effects from the SCP.

**Incident-9916-A:** During a routine clean, a member of the Site-18 janitorial team, Mr. Jedidiah Jones, mistook SCP-9916 for a regular pen and it was taken off grounds at approximately 02:34:40. A team of Site-18 security was swiftly dispatched and the SCP brought back into it's containment. Mr. Jones was reprimanded, but not fired.

This incident prompted further signage indicating the anomalous nature of SCP-9916.
