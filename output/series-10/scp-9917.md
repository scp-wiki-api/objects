**Item #:** SCP-9917

**Object Class:** ~~Safe~~ Euclid

**Special Containment Procedures:** ~~SCP-9917 is to be contained in its Leather satchel in the Storage sector of Site-██~~

After Specific Events Regarding an Incident, SCP-9917 is to be contained in Site-██ in a Specialized Containment Chamber with Leaded shielding and 15 Cm wide Concrete walls.

personnel should not Interact or Approach

 SCP-9917 without Authorization from the Ethics commitee

**Description:** SCP-9917 is a **Modified DR-M3 Geiger counter**, it was made by a Yugoslavian company called Rudi Čajavec, and was modified by █████████. SCP-9917 resembles the Previous older model (made in the 1970s) and measures approximately 27 x 15 x 10 cm (about 10.6 x 5.9 x 3.9 inches) and weighs roughly 2 to 2.5 kg (4.4 to 5.5 lbs).

SCP-9917 was discovered in a Rural Area located in **Slovenia** near ████████

SCP-9917 On normal circumstances preforms normally, detecting **Beta** and **Gamma** radiation. SCP-9917 activates when its **Touched, Interacted with** by a Sentient Being, after prolonged Time without Interacting with SCP-9917 it also Activates for a longer period of around **1 to 5 minutes**. When SCP-9917 is active it randomly Shows signs of radiation in a Non-radioactive zone. After SCP-9917 shows signs of radiation that area will be simultaneously contaminated by Radiation and Anomolous hazards. In question it forms SCP-9917-1, which is a Unidentified and anomolous form of radiation. When a victim comes upon contact with SCP-9917-1, the victim will be Immediately irradiated with Gamma, beta and alpha particles, in rare occasions the victim might undergo accelerated Mutation which results in 80% to 90% fatalities. Victims who were in contact with SCP-9917 are **Temporarly demoted** to Class-E and Put into quarantine under further notice.

**Addendums and Reports regarding SCP-9917**

---

**Addendum #9917-01:** 14/02/20██

Dr. █████ initiated the Test with Ethics Commitee Approval. Test includes 1 D-class, Lead blocks and SCP-9917.

D-6311 was instructed to enter the chamber and Use SCP-9917. After half an hour SCP-9917's dial Randomly spiked up. The D-6311 was instructed to Use the Higher rating of **R/h** instead of **mR/h**, SCP-9917 read 450 Roentgens per hour. Afterwards D-6311 collapsed and fainted.

**Test Aftermath:** D-6311 suffered a Panic attack and Mild Radiation sickness, the Panic attack was in result of his overall stress and fear and had nothing to do with the test

---

**Addendum #9917-02:** 20/02/20██

Dr. ██████ initiated the test with Ethics Commite Approval. Test includes 4 Detectors to Test accuracy and Theories set by Personnel working on SCP-9917

The detectors being:

-EMF reader

-Modified Radiacode 103 Geiger counter

-N-THEO Gen IV Akiva Counter

-Kant counter

**Test:** 01

An EMF reader was placed 50 cm away from SCP-9917 and was slowly pushed towards it. The EMF reader had little reaction

**Test concluded**: SCP-9917 does not 

Affect Electromagnetic Fields/pulses

**Test:** 02

The Modified Radiacode 103 Geiger counter was placed near SCP-9917, both were presented by a **St-90 Beta emmision source**. While SCP-9917 read 50 mR/h due to it being a Analog dial, The Modified Radiacode 103 read 53.4 mR/h

**Test concluded**: SCP-9917 is still Up to date with accuracy while using a Analog dial

**Test:** 03

The N-THEO Gen IV Akiva Counter was placed near SCP-9917, Suprisingly the Akiva counter read 15 cAk near SCP-9917. To test More akiva radiation, personnel used a number of Akiva emitters. Placing SCP-9917 near a Highly Religious artifact that read ███ cAk. SCP-9917s effects and dial was disrupted by a unknown force

**Test concluded**: Akiva Radiation affects SCP-9917

**Test:** 04

The Kant Counter was placed near SCP-9917,

It read a Low amount of Humes or about ██ humes.

**Test concluded**: SCP-9917 has minor reality bending properties

---

**Addendum #9917-03** 05/03/████

Regarding tests with SCP-9917, Test was initiated by Dr. ██████ and Authorized by the Ethics commite. Tests followed as usual, D-6433 was instructed to interact with SCP-9917,

D-6433 Resisted instructions which followed in a termination, D-6433 Threatened the staff that "he will break" SCP-9917. In a unfortold event SCP-9917 spontaneously Activated and put the room into 150 mR/h which was not a lethal dose, but there were also traces of SCP-9917-1. D-6433 collapsed and nearly fainted, after which he got up Unscathed and highly hostile. D-6433 attacked the observatory glass which resulted in a Guard being dispatched, D-6433 Got Shot 16 times into the chest without effect. Test resulted in The guards death via **Blunt trauma** and Later D-6433's death was caused by Blood loss
