**Item #:** SCP-9926

**Object Class:** Euclid

![](Traveller_Phase_In_Process.Jpeg)

*One of the few images of SCP-9926, due to it's video influence trait.

**Special Containment Procedures:** SCP-9926 must be contained in a standard sapient anomalies cell, including a bed and other required items. The room must have a psychokinetic shield around it at all times, to prevent breaches of containment. SCP-9926 may request items for its room, with approval from a site leader or the 05 council. Whenever SCP-9926 leaves its cell, it is to wear specialized cuffs that restrict SCP-9926’s phasing abilities.

**Description:** [SCP-9926 appears to be a normal teenage female, around the age of 17. She is tall in build and has a non-anomalous appearance consisting of brown long hair, pale freckled skin and brown eyes. She wears D-class uniform, to disguise her identity to those who do not have clearance to know of her. 

SCP-9926 was originally recovered from a small town of ██████ in Austria, after multiple reports of a ‘Disappearing Girl’ were mentioned in the local paper. The capture team found her with relative ease, hiding in a sewer system to sleep. She was administered Class-C Sedative for transport, doses continued until phase constricting cuffs and specialized containment cells were created. She was found wearing non-anomalous clothes made of natural plant fibres of an unknown origin. She was also found in possession of a leather bound notebook, which appears to be blank to any creature other than SCP-9926. This notebook, known as SCP-9926-2, will demanifest when not in contact with or in use by SCP-9926. She seemingly has the ability to manifest and demanifest the notebook at will. The notebook itself seems to react to any verbal or non-verbal cue from SCP-9926, including the removal and reattachment of pages and the manifestation of words, symbols and pictures on the pages.

SCP-9926 has the ability to phase through time and space, an ability that she originally denied possessing. With focus from her, SCP-9926 can split her form into particles that in close proximity to each other resemble the blurry form of SCP-9926. In this state, this anomaly can travel though solid objects, travel long distances, and, with enough time and focus, travel to other universes or timelines. It is due to this feature that she has been classified as Euclid, due to not difficulty of initial capture, but the impossibility of recapture.

As well as her phasing abilities, SCP-9926 has a degrading effect on cameras and other recording equipment. This effect is at it's weakest during the phasing process, allowing rudimentary capturing of SCP-9926 in photograph or video form. Otherwise, all footage of SCP-9926 appears to be corrupted or highly degraded. This effect does not extend to audio recordings however, so complete audio dialogue from SCP-9926 can be recorded. 

SCP-9926 has an average human intelligence, having even requested to be referred to as ‘Her’ and not ‘It’. She knows how to speak English and scattered phrases in German, as well as rudimentary sign language. Where she learned these languages is unknown, as she still refuses to inform the foundation of certain aspects of her past. 

SCP-9926 has requested several items and other objects for her cell, which have been listed below.

**Addendum:** SCP-9926 has requested items for her cell as well as other requests, including requests regarding SCP-9926 by foundation personnel, which have been recorded as follows: 

06/02/███, SCP-9926 requested release from containment, Request Denied

28/02/███, SCP-9926 requested chalk, coloured pencils and paper, pencils and paper were granted and chalk initially denied, later granted

02/03/███, SCP-9926 requested a meeting with the foundation scientists, Request denied

02/03/███, SCP-9926 requested ‘dog posters’ to hang in her cell, Request granted

08/03/███,SCP-9926 requested edits to food deliveries, including removal of mashed potato and addition of extra fruit, Request granted

23/03/███, SCP-9926 requested time off foundation tests, 10 days rest granted

04/05/███, SCP-9926 requested socialization with any personnel, Request was originally denied but is currently under reconsideration due to SCP-9926’s apparent chronic loneliness

21/05/███, Doctor █████ requested a reevaluation of tests Poppystem Test to see if subject (SCP-9926) can phase to locations with anomalous traits, such as [ SCP-087](scp-087 ) or [ SCP-093](scp-093 ). , Pentadrone Test details are currently unavailable  and Mirrorviel Test to see if the subject (SCP-9926) has versions of itself in other realities. , Request pending

Other requests have not been added until approved by the site manager.

Initial interview of SCP-9926 has been recovered and shown below. Other interviews are awaiting approval before declassification.

> **SCP-9926:** [Anomaly featured in interview]

> 

> **Doctor Avery:** [Foundation scientist and researcher, level 04 clearance]

> 

> **Foreword:** [Doctor Avery has been requested to find out more of SCP-9926's origins]

> 

> **<Begin Log>**

> 

> **Doctor Avery:** [SCP-9926, can you understand me?]

> 

> **SCP-9926:** [Where am I, who are you?]

> 

> **Doctor Avery:** [I cannot answer that. Where are you of origin?]

> 

> **SCP-9926:** [To hell if I'd tell you that! Why can't I leave?]

> 

> **Doctor Avery:** [...Have you explored many worlds?]

> 

> **SCP-9926:** [Sure... you could say that.]

> 

> **Doctor Avery:** [Why have you done so?]

> 

> **SCP-9926:** [To flee from them, from him... at least, originally.]

> 

> **Doctor Avery:** [Who is it you are referring?]

> 

> **SCP-9926:** [I... will not speak of them.]

> 

> **Doctor Avery:** [How old are you?]

> 

> **SCP-9926:** [Older than you.]

> 

> **Doctor Avery:** [Sigh, why were you in ██████?]

> 

> **SCP-9926:** [Hmph! None of your business.]

> 

> **<End Log>**

> 

> **Closing Statement:** [SCP-9926 refused to talk after this point, and she was thus taken back to containment.]
