Item #: SCP-9936

Object Class: Keter

Special Containment Procedures: SCP-9936 is to be contained in a 4 meter x 4 meter x 4 meter containment cell lined with hardened steel, with constant sound monitoring. Any images or recordings capturing SCP-9936 are to be confiscated and contained in a storage unit away from any access. All physical access to SCP-9936’s containment is strictly forbidden and transfers to new cells are performed using temporary closed cells to avoid contact and visuals of SCP-9936.

Description: SCP-9936 is an extraterrestrial entity that is capable of shapeshifting into any organism it has witnessed or made contact with. It poses as a cognitohazard to any organisms who have clear sight of the entity or make contact with its body.

Addendum: Any individuals who are exposed to SCP-9936’s cognitive effect will die within 12 hours of the infection by unknown causes such as, random heart attack, sudden aneurysms, or death from spontaneous spatial relocation into the containment chambers of an hostile entity.
