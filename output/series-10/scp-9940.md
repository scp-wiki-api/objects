#### Special Containment Procedures

---

No cure nor alternative method of managing SCP-9940 is known to the Foundation at this time. The SCP-9940 Research Team at Site-19 is tasked with identifying and studying SCP-9940-1 instances, with the goal of determining how the anomaly functions and how it may be managed.

Short-term subjects are to be provided with sedatives, intravenous pain relievers, and epidural anesthesia as deemed necessary. No life-saving efforts are to be performed, beyond those intended to prevent premature exsanguination.

#### Description

---

SCP-9940 is an anomalous medical condition, with sufferers referred to by the designation SCP-9940-1.

When an instance of SCP-9940-1 neglects to engage in penetrative sexual congressSpecifically, intercourse that results in the emission of seminal fluid into the instance's vaginal orifice. for a case-dependent period of timeThe length of this time period varies greatly between individuals. Mild cases can abstain for 10+ days without symptoms, while the most severe are unable to go more than 3-5 days before the onset of vaginal bleeding., their abdominal cavity will begin to internally deform. Initial expansion begins with the upper vaginal canal, cervix, and uterus before spreading. No anomalous physical elasticity of the affected area, nor decrease in the number of pain receptors has been observed. As SCP-9940 progresses, internal organs in the path of this expansion are compressed, displaced, ejected, and/or punctured.

If SCP-9940's progression were to continue indefinitely, it likely would not cease until the sufferer's abdomen had been rendered virtually hollow, with all organs expelled from the bodyThis has been observed to occur both vaginally and anally. or compressed upwards into the ribcage. This has not yet been observed, as death will inherently occur much earlier, with the most common proximate causes being blood loss and sepsis following bowel rupture. Notably advanced cases may occasionally end in death caused by traumatic asphyxia.

Should the instance engage in sexual intercourse after the onset of SCP-9940, the prior expansion will revert, and the resulting lacerations will be replaced by a thick layer of scar tissue. No other healing properties are observed. If reversal occurs beyond SCP-9940's earlier stages, the newly-formed scar tissue is likely to irreparably impede the function of vital organs and blood vessels.

Despite the primary method of symptom management being consistent and frequent sexual conduct, the anomaly itself has no effect on the sufferer's libido. SCP-9940-1 instances who have experienced multiple mild activations with reversal soon after onset will often display an increase in sex drive, but this is likely to be a mental association between arousal and the relief of pain.

### Short-Term Case Study

= ^^**Subject:** D-29318 (3 of 17)^^

#### Introduction

---

SCP-9940's Short-Term Study Group is the primary manner by which the later stages of SCP-9940 are studied. There have been a total of 17 individuals in this group during the period of study, with 16 of them being individuals found under the care of doctors among the general public. Most cases were advanced prior to Foundation involvement -- to the point where the subject would not be expected to survive, notwithstanding medical intervention and the satiation of the anomaly. To date, only one subject belonging to the Short-Term Study Group was in Foundation custody at the time of symptom onset.

{{**NOTE:** As required by Ethics Committee guidelines, SCP-9940-1 instances must be deemed terminally ill to be approved for Short-Term Study. Class-D Personnel are exempt from this requirement.}}

#### Subject Information

---

D-29318 (redesignated SUBJ-9264-ST) is a presumably non-anomalous female, born on 06-19-1964. It was taken into custody on 11-08-1992, after it shot and fatally wounded a Foundation field agent. It reported a history of sexual abuse as an adolescent, with a boyfriend identified as the primary perpetrator. A history of familial abuse was also attested to, but the subject would not provide an answer as to the nature of this abuse.

SUBJ-9264-ST became involved in full service sex work at the age of 18, after it was no longer welcome at its parents' residence. At age 26, it desisted from its previous form of sex work and began appearing in low-budget adult videos. During these productions, it engaged in frequent, unprotected intercourse. It is likely to have been an instance of SCP-9940-1 for far longer than initially believed.

#### Events of Case Study

---

Midday on November 11th, 1992, SUBJ-9264-ST began complaining of intense abdominal pain, stating that it had been bleeding vaginally for approximately 36 hours. These concerns were dismissed, as it was believed by guards that the subject was either menstruating or had suffered a miscarriage. For approximately two hours after this complaint, the subject remained unobserved in its holding cell.

{{**11-11-1992 - 14:00**}}

Two guards return to the area after hearing multiple Class-D individuals shouting. Upon arrival, they are urged to observe SUBJ-9264-ST, who has recently started to wail. It can be seen laying in its bunk in the fetal position, with blood pooling underneath its lower torso and buttocks.

{{**11-11-1992 - 15:00**}}

SUBJ-9264-ST is brought to a medic who is able to perform an ultrasound. The scan shows a 4cm dilation of its cervix with no apparent cause, as it is confirmed at the same time that the subject is not pregnant. At this time it is heavily suspected that the subject is an instance of SCP-9940-1.

{{**11-11-1992 - 22:00**}}

The subject is placed in a medical containment chamber for observation. It is given sedatives and sterilized with iodine to prevent infection. Dr. *'A.B.'*, the SCP-9940 Research Head is informed at this time, after which he gathers several researchers to attend and document the observation. They arrive shortly after and begin to assess the subject.

{{**11-12-1992 - 10:00**}}

SUBJ-9264-ST is given an MRI, revealing that its cervical dilation has increased to 8cm. Its uterus has begun to distend upwards and outwards. Rupture within a day's time is likely. Bleeding continues to worsen, due to the onset of cervical tearing. Subject is given fluids and intravenous morphine.

{{**11-12-1992 - 18:00**}}

SUBJ-9264-ST remains sedated during this time. Its condition rapidly deteriorates, with the subject briefly falling close to death after a period of severe bleeding. It is observed that the uterus has ruptured. The subject soon experiences urinary incontinence, due to sudden compression of the bladder against the abdominal wall. Small blood vessels are cauterized in an attempt to stop the bleeding. Additional efforts to prevent both infection and exsanguination are somewhat successful.

{{**11-13-1992 - 7:00**}}

Further tearing of the cervix and upper vaginal canal is observed. Through the largest of these tears, a loop of the subject's small intestine is visible. Subject shows signs of pain and is given epidural anesthesia in lieu of intravenous morphine. Methods of preventing the partial expulsion of the subject's intestines are discussed but not decided upon. During the original 1996 study conducted by the Department of Anomalous Medicine, a laparoscopy and hysteroscopy were performed. No adhesions to the intestinal serosa could be observed. Neither the left, nor right adnexa were visible, which resulted in SUBJ-0169-LT being diagnosed with bilateral fallopian tube agenesis. No remnant fallopian tissue or anomalous structures were detected. Pelvic inflammation was observed, yet no evidence of a viral or bacterial infection was found. While unknown, it is possible that this may have been an early sign of damage caused by SCP-9940.

Another laparoscopy was performed in 2002, showing extensive adhesions to the intestinal serosa and abdominal wall (as seen in *Figure 1)*. SUBJ-0169-LT reported experiencing prior abdominal pain, yet she perceived this as normal, given unrelated health conditions that also cause her chronic pain. It is a legitimate concern that should SCP-9940 not be properly managed, the subject's health will further deteriorate. Should that occur, the risk of bowel obstruction and worsening pain cannot be overlooked.
