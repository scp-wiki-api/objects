# **SPECIAL CONTAINMENT PROCEDURES:**

SCP-9950 is Aizsaule The anomaly’s existence is known to the public, but its anomalous properties are not.  class.

Due to  the sheer ideatic density The  measure of intensity & concentration of a given memeplex in a physical/metaphysical domain. Measured in a percentile with ρω‘s.  of SCP-9950, full separation of its various related memeplexes from baseline reality, and thus proper containment, is infeasible. Current research is centered on the manufacturing of a hypothetical narritive space without the existence of SCP-9950, regardless of the logical impossibility of doing so within an SCP-9950-infected plane.

---

# **DESCRIPTION:**

SCP-9950 is a possibly-sentient theoretical and metaphysical parasite currently inhabiting all planes of conceptualization known to humanity.

SCP-9950’s anomalous properties have yet to be fully proven, however it has been provided an SCP designation due to the sheer improbability of its prevalence across planes which should not possess its ideatic characteristics, as well as recovered information from the Huǒjù zhi Zi occult organization Also known as the Children of the Torch. The group's religious beliefs focus on the worship of avian entities and the Sun, which is given a deific status., which suggests to a point in history without the existence of SCP-9950.

The true physical parameters of SCP-9950 cannot be determined due to the subjective nature of most of its manifestations.

---

The following is a comprehensive list of the planes of conceptualization SCP-9950 has affected, and the ideatic density of SCP-9950 within said planes.

Due to the nature of the Infosphere SCP-9950, while fully present in its entirety within it, has not been observed to have a structural impact on it as a whole.

<details>
<summary>PATASPHERE</summary>

**PLANE VISIBILITY:**  INTANGIBLE

**IDEATIC DENSITY:** 46ρω

#### ****DEFINITION****

---

*The collective metaspace of all possible narratives.*

#### ****EFFECTS****

As stated above, 100% of  narratives known to the Foundation have been infected by SCP-9950 in some form. SCP-9950 most commonly manifests within the Patasphere as either an external or internal antagonistic force, regardless of genre. However various culturally significant narratives within younger genre demographics have been altered to include aspects of SCP-9950 The rate of these alterations have been shown to increase during periods of societal destabilization attributed to ecological crisis.

While no metastructual damage to the noösphere cashed by SCP-9950 has been observed, the number of thought energy condensing around the "hexagonal" and the "color" nodes have increased, by proxy increasing essophysical manifestation events correlating to aspects of SCP-9950

Recent noöspheric examination of unconscious anti-concepts have revealed a continuous merging of SCP-9950 with certain "Primal fears" experienced by the human population. Current estimates date the beginning of this merging to have begun around 300,000 years ago.

# **ADDENDUM-2:**

On July 3rd 2034, the largest essophysical manifestation of SCP-9950 was recorded and designated SCP-9950-1.  Records of SCP-9950’s existence have been found dating back roughly 34 million years ago with various proven historical records. Considering its area of 14,200,000 km², the number of entities, settlements and landmarks found within it, the appearance of SCP-9950-1 has been declared largest semiohazardous event in history.

Due to the newly confirmed existence of SCP-9950 & SCP-9950-1, no containment, neutralization or planetary evacuation efforts are being considered at this time. Reclassification to -EX is pending.
