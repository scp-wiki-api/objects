**Item #:** SCP-9951

**Object Class:** Euclid

**Special Containment Procedures:** SCP-9951 has been successfully removed from public view and replaced with an equivalent game devoid of anomalous properties.

Online discussion of the *Touhou Project* series is to be monitored for references to SCP-9951. Offending materials are to be traced and their distributors amnesticized. All digital copies containing SCP-9951 are to be wiped following discovery.

For the purposes of reducing collateral damage of public and private property, the Foundation will maintain no less than 10 containment specialists possessing high strategic and mechanical proficiency in “bullet hell” video games. In the event that an individual in possession of SCP-9951 has already engaged the anomaly at the time of Foundation interception, one of these personnel will be dispatched to their location with the goal of training the individual in the “one credit clear” completion of SCP-9951.

**Description:** SCP-9951 is a difficulty setting found within *Touhou Fuminkyou ~ Echoes of Insomnia* (不眠響), a 2018 fan-made video game based on the larger Touhou Project series.

SCP-9951, presented as "Insomniac" difficulty, is entirely absent from the game’s code. The *Touhou Fuminkyou* development team similarly displayed nescience towards the game mode’s existence during Foundation interrogation.

SCP-9951 is not accessible through ordinary gameplay, and its activation requires the player to recite a spoken-word invocation while making visual contact with *betobeto.jpg*, an image file of a fan-created character downloaded alongside the game. The contents of the invocation and the mechanics of SCP-9951 as a game mode were rapidly spread through various online imageboards prior to Foundation interception, touting itself as a challenge for expert players.

The English contents of the invocation have been reproduced below. Containment efforts discovered 8 language variations of the following text.

 

*Dear Betobeto-san,*

*These games are too easy. Please give me an actual challenge.*

*My life contains too many pleasures, and I do not want to have fun.*

*I devote all parts of myself and my earthly possessions in service of the great people of Gensokyo and all of its surrounding territories.*

*Please, go ahead, Betobeto-san.*

Upon correct recitation, SCP-9951 will become accessible through the game’s difficulty selection menu. Concurrently, the player will gain implicit awareness of three conditions associated with beginning the game within SCP-9951:

* The player will become cognizant of their total number of deaths within SCP-9951, which are counted by the number of times a continue screen has been reached

* The player will understand that consequences will occur in reality for incurring further deaths within the game

* The player will remain awake until the "one credit clear" completion of SCP-9951, meaning that all of the game's stages are successfully finished without a "continue screen" loss

If the player begins SCP-9951, the game's consequential anomalous effects, detailed below, will remain active until the successful "one credit clear" completion of the difficulty.

**Addendum.1.9951:** Mechanics

*Touhou Fuminkyo* is a *danmaku* shooter, more commonly known as a *bullet hell*, a subgenre of video game whose core mechanic involves the player navigating their character through a series of complex, moving patterns of small, bullet-like shapes without getting hit. The genre has achieved notoriety for being challenging, requiring frequent repetition in order to master.

The game’s narrative is structured in the style of the other mainline *Touhou Project* games, and involves a plague of sleeplessness affecting Gensokyo, the fantasy setting the series takes place in. Reimu Hakurei, a shrine maiden tasked with resolving “incidents” within Gensokyo, as well as the avatar of the player, journeys through seven total game stages. Each stage ends in a fight against a “boss” character, whose *danmaku* patterns are significantly more difficult to avoid than the rest of the stage’s enemies.

*Touhou Fuminkyo*’s primary antagonist, Betobeto Bishou (ベトベト美翔), an original character created for the game, hopes to finally cure her insomnia by causing Gensokyo’s inhabitants to push her to the point of exhaustion through *danmaku* combat. In a non-diegetic context, this provides cause for the game’s intense mechanical difficulty, even when compared to other games within the genre.

SCP-9951 features an even larger increase in difficulty from the base game, with enemy bullet patterns taking on more rapid, erratic designs and lasting for longer durations. The mode also induces a series of 10 anomalous effects, occurring at increments of 10 total continues. Details of these effects have been included below.

"One Credit Clear" completion of SCP-9951 will result in the discontinuation of these effects. Upon winning, the player will receive the following items, distributed via extraphysical manipulation of their computer screen:

* A large trophy made of 24 karat gold, modeled in the shape of Reimu Hakurei's head

* The equivalent of $500,000 in their local currency

* A multitude of personalized thank-you notes purportedly signed by various characters from the *Touhou Project* series, presently totaling 102 letters

Foundation interviews with players who successfully completed SCP-9951 during containment efforts have discovered a near unanimous positive reception of the game mode, with players finding it to be an enjoyable challenge. This is not believed to be compulsory.
