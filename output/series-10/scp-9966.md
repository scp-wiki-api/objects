= ****ADDITIONAL CONTAINMENT INITIATIVE****

### A.R.S.H.

##### Automated Real-time Star Hiding

A.R.S.H. is an automated Foundation-run scrubbing protocol that is embedded in major image-hosting platforms and astronomy forums to identify and censor hazardous star patterns. Public-facing images of the night sky are altered before release. Disinformation campaigns are utilized to reinforce the belief that any discrepancies between amateur photographs and official star maps are due to camera limitations, atmospheric distortion, and inexperience from the photographer.

Civilian and academic observatories are to remain under continuous Foundation oversight through agents embedded in national space agencies, academic institutions, and organizations performing astrometric surveys. All digital telescope software/firmware distributed to the public must include A.R.S.H. programming that automatically distorts stellar positions of celestial bodies by >10 arcseconds.

In the event of a prolonged electrical grid failure in major population centres, MTF Iota-707 "Let There Be Light" is authorised to deploy emergency light saturation measures, including the use of floodlight-equipped aerial vehicles and drone dispersal of photoreactive aerosol agents to simulate artificial skyglow until power is restored. Agents are equipped with Class-B amnestics, which are to be administered to any and all affected civilians. Misinformation campaigns are to be utilised to cover up any reported mass casualty.

DESCRIPTION: SCP-9966 is the night sky as observed from the surface of Earth. When instances of SCP-9966 digital or physical are viewed with the unaided human eye or through unaltered optical instruments located on Earth’s surface, or a dataset bearing the signature of SCP-9966 is read by a sufficiently sentient artificial intelligence model, the arrangement of stellar bodies constitutes a memetic phenomenon. This effect is not present when the same pattern is observed from outside Earth’s atmosphere.

Exposure to the unfiltered SCP-9966 pattern induces a compulsive drive to ascend vertically. Symptom onset occurs within 2–5 seconds of exposure and follows a consistent sequence:

**0–2 seconds:** Subject reports intense cognitive engagement characterized by sensations of comprehension and significance.

**2–3 seconds:** Subject enters a catatonic state. Voluntary motor function ceases. Pupillary dilation exceeds normal physiological limits, frequently resulting in ocular damage.

**3–5 seconds:** Subject regains motor activity and demonstrates singular behavioral fixation on vertical ascent. Movement is directed upward by any available means, including but not limited to climbing, scaling structures, or attempting to exit through elevated apertures.

Unrestrained subjects will continue ascent attempts until physical incapacity, external intervention, or amnestic treatment prevents further activity.

#### MTF IOTA-707

## LET THERE BE LIGHT

---

Specialized rapid-response task force responsible for immediate suppression of SCP-9966 exposure events through light saturation, civilian containment, and infrastructure stabilization.

5000 permanent operatives around the world, with jurisdiction to employ additional field agents and/or civilians as needed, divided into five (5) squads.

Personnel selected for expertise in optics, pyrotechnics, urban suppression tactics, emergency engineering, and cognitohazard containment.

All operatives undergo mandatory Class-W memetic inoculation and adaptive training for light-intensity endurance.

**Squad A (Floodlight Operations):** Deploys high-output stationary and vehicle-mounted light arrays for rapid urban/rural area coverage.

**Squad B (Aerial Saturation):** Operates drone and fixed-wing platforms fitted with airborne floodlighting systems and photoreactive aerosol dispersal units.

**Squad C (Civilian Containment):** Specializes in suppression of SCP-9966-induced compulsions via non-lethal restraint, sedation, amnestic treatment, and controlled extraction protocols.

**Squad D (Grid Restoration):** Provides field engineering capacity to reestablish failed electrical networks using portable generator arrays, emergency cabling, and localized light-grid reconstruction.

**Squad E (Pyrotechnics):** Utilizes pyrotechnic, incendiary, and large-scale controlled fire deployment to generate emergency light saturation in environments where electrical or artificial systems are unavailable. Authorized methods include mass flare dispersal, ignition of designated fuel reserves, and initiation of urban firebreaks under containment cover stories.

* Vehicle-mounted and portable floodlight systems.

* UAV and fixed-wing light-saturation platforms.

* High-output chemical flares, magnesium incendiaries, pyrotechnic mortar arrays.

* Personal Class-III optical filtration helmets with automatic polarization shutters.

* Rapid-distribution amnestic systems (aerosol and intramuscular).

Intervene in population centers experiencing SCP-9966 visibility events caused by grid failure, atmospheric conditions, or deliberate obstruction of light infrastructure.

Obscure SCP-9966 through artificial illumination by any means necessary.

Contain and neutralize exposed civilians, minimizing secondary exposure cascades.

Reinforce or replace compromised infrastructure to reduce recurrence probability.

Employ pyrotechnic and fire-based saturation (Squad E) where other measures are infeasible, prioritizing suppression of SCP-9966 exposure over collateral property damage.

= ADDENDUM 9966.1: **DISCOVERY**

ANTITHINK machines are to be deployed globally. All surviving civilians, and all remaining Foundation personnel, are to undergo compulsory induction into ANTITHINK conversion.

Archival records, digital or physical, containing references to:

* neutrino-cognitron entanglement

* extraterrestrial religious scriptures

* the concept of cognitohazards

are to be destroyed in their entirety.

DESCRIPTION: SCP-9966 is a cognitively-hostile phenomenon expressed through neutrino-cognitron entanglement.

Any neutrino emission originating external to Earth’s cognitosphere transmits SCP-9966’s data signature. Exposure to this data by a sapient cognitive circuitry results in immediate cognitive termination, resulting in extreme damage to the cognitive circuitry.

Analysis concludes that SCP-9966 targets sapient cognition exclusively. Non-sapient organisms, mechanical systems, and post-ANTITHINK subjects remain unaffected.

All prior containment measures (A.R.S.H. suppression, optical shielding, Protocol NOCTIS) are classified as ineffective. The only functional countermeasure is compulsory ANTITHINK conversion to remove sapience as a valid host state.

# PROTOCOL-ANTITHINK

#### DECOMMISSION PROTOCOL #9966

Dir. Calvin Bold

Department of Decommissioning

---

= **PROTOCOL OUTLINE**

****PURPOSE:**** Decommission SCP-9966 through induced regression of human sapience, creating a cognitively inert successor population resistant to SCP-9966.

****ABSTRACT:**** SCP-9966’s neutrino-cognitron entanglement renders all sapient organisms terminally vulnerable. Standard containment has proven insufficient to mitigate global extinction risk. Protocol-ANTITHINK authorizes the deployment of [Bold-Kelvin Auto-Reliant Artificial Sapience Cognitive Sink Domes](cognitive-sink-dome). Colloquially called ANTITHINK machines, these employ a cognitron-drain programme designed to forcibly recess the cognitive capabilities of volunteer sapient entities, generating a population of pseudo-humans that are capable of potential sapience but incapable of actualizing it. This regression creates a low cognitive field, immune to the effects of SCP-9966, theoretically allowing long-term re-emergence of sapience and reformation of an uncontaminated Cognitosphere..##77DDFF|Or, the crows beat us to it.##

= **METHOD**

****SECTION A:**** Subject Induction

Cognizant entities designated for ANTITHINK inoculation will be secured within the ANTITHINK machine. Once activated, the apparatus will initiate controlled siphoning of the subject’s cognitive capabilities. This process is accelerated for civilians through cognitively taxing stimulus, provided under the guise of leisure activities. To ensure the continuity of Foundation oversight over ANTITHINK inoculation, all stimulus given to Foundation personnel are altered to redact redundant and irrelevant information to reduce cognitive load.

****SECTION B:**** Cognitive Recession

Over the course of full drainage, the subject will undergo irreversible sapience recession, resulting in a sentient but non-sapient state. These entities designated Homo Sentiens retain baseline biological functionality while existing outside SCP-9966’s lethal cognitohazard parameters.

****SECTION C:**** Negative Space Generation

The collective presence of H. Sentiens induces a large-scale cognitively “blank” substrate. This cognitron-negative environment prevents SCP-9966 from maintaining anomalous resonance, causing the complete dissolution of Earth’s cognitosphere, effectively neutralizing SCP-9966's influence.

****SECTION D:**** Evolutionary Continuity

Foundation models predict that, given sufficient temporal scale, H. Sentiens populations may re-evolve into sapience under post-SCP-9966 conditions. Whether this outcome constitutes true "humanity" is formally deemed irrelevant under Decommission Directive 9966.
