**Item Number**SCP-5074 specifically requested no copy-pasted symbols, to the great convenience of the author of this article.**:** SCP-5074

 

**Object Class:** sdlkfjljkrjr r r r rrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrrEsoteric object class graciously provided by SCP-5074 itself. Class denotes an object that is *(aggressive hooting)* and *(picking mites out of navel)*.

 

**Special Containment Procedures:** SCP-5074 is to be kept within Site-19's most lavish and luxurious simian containment unit, and is to be provided with 10 bananas, 17 coconuts, and 257 termites daily by a detail of highly trained and attractive Wilson's Wildlife Solutions personnel. The entity is also to be provided with a camera, a typewriter, writing utensils, and paper, with which it is to record its great deeds and exploits.

 

**Description:** SCP-5074 is an adult male chimpanzee *(Pan troglodytes)*As the author has memorized the scientific name of chimpanzees, no copy-pasting was necessary notable for its anomalously enhanced physical strength, an intelligence surpassing that of most college professors, scientists, and mathematicians, and strikingly handsome facial features. 

 

SCP-5074 uses its extraordinary abilities quite liberally, and is known to record its adventures using its camera and aided by the photomanipulation software on its computer. The following is a gallery of such instances.
