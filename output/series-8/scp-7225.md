Extreme care must be taken when removing the item from the locker and transporting it for testing, as even minor disturbances to the 'snow' within the item can instigate localized weather anomalies. Any anomalous meteorological events tied to the item's use are to be suppressed in local and national news outlets, with standard Cover Story 19 "Cold Snap" disseminated to the local population.

**Description:** SCP-7225 is a snow globe of standard size and design, with an inscription on the bottom reading "Snow Craft Products, Aurora, CO.This company does not exist. It has never existed." The diorama within the snow globe appears to be an accurate recreation of Foundation Site-51Site-51 does not exist., including figures representing various Foundation personnel standing outside the building. Notably, one of the miniature figures appears to be wearing a long purple argyle scarf, similar to the one that Junior Researcher Timothy KratchittJunior Researcher Timothy Kratchitt does not exist. was known to wear.

**Discovery:** SCP-7225 was discovered by personnel at Site-51Site-51 does not exist. Foundation security personnel discovered Junior Researcher Timothy KratchittJunior Researcher Timothy Kratchitt does not exist. outside the site, holding SCP-7225 in his hands and shaking it. Junior Researcher Timothy KratchittJunior Researcher Timothy Kratchitt does not exist. was apprehended, and SCP-7225 was recovered and subsequently contained.
