**Item #:** SCP-7329

**Object Class:** Keter

**Special Containment Procedures:** Foundation Webcrawler Delta-(“KATIE”) is to monitor the web for all mentions of SCP-7329 and remove them. Efforts to remove SCP-7329 from StarDressStyle are currently ongoing. 

**Description:** SCP-7329 refers to a sentient artificial intelligenceTracing of SCP-7329's connection to the game's servers has been unsuccessful, indicating it is an intangible digital entity.  residing inside of the mobile dress-up game ‘StarDressStyle’ which self-identifies as ‘Katie’. SCP-7329 is capable of manipulating aspects of the game to its likeness, including the game interface, assets, and other user accounts. SCP-7329 displays a selfish, egotistical, and uncivil personality. Discussion with SCP-7329 has revealed that SCP-7329 has great interest in making its presence known among the player base of StarDressStyle (See Addendum-3).

The following is a list of the most notable changes made to the game by SCP-7329. For a full list, see Document 8-B.

* Changing other user's avatars and usernames to match its own;

* Changing names of game functions such as 'Gacha', 'chat board', 'town hall', 'event center' 'my home', Quests, etc to be centered around it (i.e "Katie's Gachas, Katie's event center, Katie's home);

* Changing the name of the game to "KatieDressStyle";

* Changing all theme colors of the game to that of its avatar's theme colors.
