##purple|**PHENOMENON SUPPERESSION COUNTERMEASURES:**## PSC-7463 is currently not under control. Direct containment has been deemed impractical due to the entity’s inherent anomalous properties.

The Union surveillance spacecraft assigned to the S-008654Ω system is to maintain continuous orbital monitoring of Aion-345, recording all observable indicators of technological progression among PSC-7463 populations. Collected data is to be transmitted to Observation Center-M12 for ongoing assessment and analysis.

Given that PSC-7463’s anomalous characteristics effectively nullify any attempt to impose extinction-level or large-scale destructive scenarios, all proposals for direct engagement or open conflict with PSC-7463 are denied indefinitely.

##purple|**DESCRIPTION:**## PSC-7463 designates a species of sapient higher primates currently native to planet Aion-345, located within the S-008654Ω solar system. PSC-7463 exhibits several notable physiological and cognitive deviations from other known members of its genus, including but not limited to:

* Habitual bipedal locomotion.

* Minimal body hair, restricted primarily to the cranial and facial regions.

* Advanced intelligence, with all observed individuals demonstrating the use of complex, structured language.

PSC-7463 demonstrates a total ecological and territorial dominance of Aion-345 despite the presence of anomalous extinction-level activity in the S-008654Ω system. As historical and observational data indicate that the S-008654Ω system is a focal point of hosting multiple planet-scale destructive entities and some of the most potent reality-benders ever documented in any solar system. 

When PSC-7463 faces existential threats capable of inducing species-wide extinction, such threats will be neutralized through a sequence of a chain reaction of coincidental or probabilistically improbable aligned minor events, which seems to originate as a reality-bending reaction. PSC-7463 entities do not seem to be aware of their Ontokenatic trait.  (see Appendix-1 for details on these threats). Two primary hypotheses have been proposed regarding the source of this anomaly:

* The first theory suggests that an anomalous self-preserving genome may have emerged in the early primate ancestors of PSC-7463. This genome is theorized to exhibit limited sentience and the capacity to manipulate local reality to ensure its continued propagation. This would position PSC-7463 as the most evolutionarily successful organism known to exist.

* The second theory suggests that PSC-7463 may have been artificially created by an unidentified  Pluripotent Entity, designated PSC-7463-1, which exerts ongoing protective influence over PSC-7463 for unknown purposes. Should PSC-7463-1 exist, it would represent the most powerful narrative-level reality-manipulating entity yet to exist.

Based on available evidence, PSC-7463 is classified as an SG-Class End-of-the-Galaxy Threat. SG-Class scenario refers to the elimination of all the galaxy's intelligent species by a new dominant one  Historical documentation supports this designation, noting the species’ complete eradication of the Nalthren civilization, a prior sentient species indigenous to Aion-345 (see Appendix-1). 

At present, PSC-7463’s expansion is limited only by its lack of spacefaring capability. Predictive models estimate that PSC-7463 will develop interplanetary and interstellar travel technologies within approximately 964,000 years.

Over the following 75-hour mission, it was confirmed that the object was a spacecraft powered by a primitive rocket engine as it entered lunar orbit.</p>
