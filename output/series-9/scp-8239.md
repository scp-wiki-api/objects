**Special Containment Procedures:** All SCP-8239-1 instances are to be stored at Site-58, with on-site counseling made available to help alleviate any feelings of body dysmorphia caused by their condition. Euthanasia is also to be made available to SCP-8239-1 instances on request.

Embedded Foundation agents within Kyrgyzstan should lobby to ensure that wolves are not used in the game of kok boru.Kok boru is a Kirgiz variant of buzkashi, a game native to the Central Asian steppe in which two teams of horse riders compete for control of a goat or calf carcass, which they then must place into a pre-determined goal. Kok boru is distinct in that a wolf carcass was traditionally used until wolf populations in Kyrgyzstan made the use of wolves impractical. If a game of kok boru using a wolf is discovered, Foundation agents are to ensure that no women are allowed to witness or participate in the game in any way.   

**Description:** SCP-8239 is a phenomenon affecting any woman who witnesses or participates in any game of kok boru that uses a wolf carcass, and which takes place within the region of modern-day Kyrgyzstan.Games meeting this criteria are designated SCP-8239-A. Any woman affected by SCP-8239 undergoes a rapid and complete biological metamorphosis into a female domesticated horse*Equus ferus caballus* and is designated SCP-8239-1. SCP-8239-1 instances are biologically indistinguishable from non-anomalous horses yet retain a human level of intelligence as well as all their prior memories.

**Osmonova:** You were sovereign until Blue Wolf beat you. If he can do it, so can I.

**Cloud:** I am stronger than you.
