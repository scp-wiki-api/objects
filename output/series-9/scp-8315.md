**Item #:** SCP-8315

**Object Class:** Euclid

**Special Containment Procedures:** Item SCP-8315 is currently contained within storage cell 3-10-005 at Site-41, pending a warehouse transfer.

SCP-8315 should not pose a threat to staff upholding the Standard Foundation Mental Regimen (SFMR). Exceptions apply to those with less than a year of continuous employment at the Foundation, unless interaction is ordered by Site-41's administration.

**Description:** SCP-8315 is a Philips 20PT5441/37 CRT television exhibiting unique predatory behaviour. SCP-8315 demonstrates the ability to leech off electrical potential from the cerebrum of living humans within a 5 meter radius, this sudden change in cerebral voltage is invariably and instantaneously fatal to the victim. Although SCP-8315 should theoretically be capable of feeding off any part of the cerebrum, it displays a marked preference for parts associated with memory, imagination and critical thinking, often solely targeting them.

SCP-8315 was acquired by the Foundation under a containment contract by the H&C Paranatural Office, the first to be awarded to the Foundation. It should be considered a baseline for staff's capability to handle psychophysical anomalies and the wider Foundation's competence as a contractor.
