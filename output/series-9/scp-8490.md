**Item Number:** SCP-8490

**Object Class:** Keter

**Special Containment Procedures:** Candles, aerosol dispensers, and scented beads have been distributed throughout the Site; to be replaced regularly. Mirrored surfaces marred as preventative measure.

Under no circumstances should personnel be unaccompanied. Search parties prohibited. Cleanup forbidden; constitutes direct interaction.

One's line of sight should not dwell above another individual for extended periods. 

**Description:** Inadvertently communicated to current secure facility. Cannot be transferred back to Site of origin. Transmission/selection vector unsolvable. To-date, sixty-seven staff assimilated/incorporated via retrocausal embodiment. 

Despite subjective proximity to all personnel, source ~~remains~~ is ineffable. Paranoid sensations are therefore ubiquitous and untreatable.  Twelve attempts made to document physicality; each invariably failed. 

Census:

* **Surface Level:**

 * Banner, De Jong, ~~Holden~~ Fig 1.1 . Maintenance operations continue uninterrupted. Partial conveyance of anomaly sent to main Site via coded missive; awaiting Special Response/Rescue.

* **Sublevel 1:**

 * Safe-Class Reliquary successfully placed on lockdown. Visser left to own devices. Stoepker and Barrow looked up.

* **Sublevel 2:** 

 * Majority of population (49). Southern wing off-limits. Security team set precedent for non-viable approach/offensive; considered responsible for the smell.

* **Sublevel 3:**

 * Formerly housed research team/test subjects. Acknowledgement of results triggers primary effects. Bulkheads sealed. 

* **Euclid Containment:**

 * SCP-████ affected despite unnatural lifespan. Containment chamber's sensors damaged by tachyon release. SCP-███ unaffected; file updated to confirm entity does not constitute living being.

* **Sublevel 4:**

 * Railway sabotaged to prevent spread to main site. Sanders, Baudelair, and Hannawald safe/sheltering in-place; cannot risk egress through Euclid Containment.

* **Sublevel 5:**

 * Initial contact point. Mortuary first affected. Nine staff learned the danger of identifying anomaly. Six determined proper conveyance at personal cost. Two attempted physical interaction. Dowell chose the furnace.
