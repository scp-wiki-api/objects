**Special Containment Procedures:** SCP-8561 remains under strict surveillance by the Abrahamic subdivision of the M.T.F. η-77 ("Spheres Within Spheres"), in constant collaboration with the Horizon Initiative, in accordance with established religious procedures for the control and monitoring of empyrean entities. 

Given its location in a public area and its harmless nature over long periods of time, SCP-8561 will remain open to regular visits by tourists for the cultural, historical and heritage benefit of the city of Rome and the Vatican itself.

Visits may not exceed 10-15 minutes per person to avoid potential adverse effects from the akiva radiation emitted by SCP-8561.

Due to the incident recorded on 3 November 2024 @@[@@[#LOG LOG 8561.2]@@]@@, approaching SCP-8561 within 5 metres is forbidden. The entity will remain on public display but without direct contact with the public.

**Description:** SCP-8561 refers to the statue situated at the highest point of Castel Sant'Angelo, in Vatican City, Rome (Italy). At first glance, SCP-8561 appears to be made entirely of weathered bronze with subsequent structural alterations, and is mounted on a base of carved and polished stone.

Historical records state that SCP-8561 was an exact replica of the original statue commissioned by Pope Gregory the Great following the alleged apparition of the Archangel Michael sheathing his sword atop the castle as a sign of the end of the plague ravaging Rome around the year 590 AD. In 1752, SCP-8561 was installed in place of the original statue, which remains stored in one of the castle's interior corridors to this day.

The anomalous nature of SCP-8561 was confirmed and discovered on 24 September 2022 during the COVID-19 pandemic, when Mobile Task Force η-77 investigated local rumours of "miraculous cures" attributed to visits to the monument. Subsequent analysis detected the complete disappearance of the viral strain in individuals situated within a specific range around SCP-8561, accompanied by an abrupt increase in akiva radiation emitted by the entity. Although the increase did not reach lethal levels, it did produce noticeable physiological and theological effects.

SCP-8561 remained in quarantine for the next four months following its discovery, during which time it underwent various tests to verify its functionality.

**Procedure:** Subjects were exposed individually to SCP-8561 in 1-metre increments within a 20-metre perimeter. Each exposure lasted a maximum of 10 minutes. Akiva radiation levels, viral progression rates, physiological changes and signs of theological reactivity were monitored.

**Results:** As expected, SCP-8561 exhibits miraculous capabilities in the internal alteration of biological systems belonging to Christian believers in its vicinity. The increase in akiva radiation emissions, and consequently the efficacy of SCP-8561's effects, is determined by the amount of "faith" and belief professed by the subject towards Catholic Christianity.

***Notes:** It is recommended that future studies be conducted with participants whose religious beliefs are more diverse.*

# LOG 8561.2.-

*"And then the lawless one will be revealed, whom the Lord Jesus will overthrow with the breath of his mouth and destroy by the splendor of his coming."*

@@   @@— **(2 Thessalonians 2:8-12)**

@@ @@

**Date:** 03/11/2024

**History:** On 3 November 2024, at 06:32 CET, a body was reported to have been found at the top of Castel Sant'Angelo. The body was that of a young man aged between 19 and 21. He is currently registered as [REDACTED], an Italian national.

On the night of 2 November, CCTV cameras on a nearby street captured the man heading towards the Castle carrying a rucksack.

At 00:49 on 3 November, the man is seen gaining access to the monument by climbing over one of the outer security fences of the site, paying little attention to his surroundings.

Finally, exactly 52 minutes after entering the Castle, the upper camera records the man leaving his rucksack and climbing onto the statue.

At 01:50 on 3 November, for no apparent reason, the camera stopped working, showing only continuous interference. Thanks to one of the Akiva sensors located in the area, readings from the site were recorded at that time until a specific hour when the sensors also ceased to function.
