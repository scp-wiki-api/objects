= **SCP-8582:** A pair of shoes that do not belong to you

= **Author:** [ GwenWinterheart](winterheart-page )

The following document has been flagged for revision. It may contain irregularities and out-of-date information.

-- Maria Jones, Director, RAISA

---

**Special Containment Procedures:**  SCP-8582 are to be kept in a shoebox in a standard storage locker, and may be viewed or handled by anyone who expresses interest in them. However, SCP-8582 are not to be worn by anyone until their rightful owner is established beyond doubt.

You are to stop thinking about SCP-8582.

**Description:** SCP-8582 are a pair of shoes that do not belong to you.  They are size █, which is notably too small for you to comfortably wear.  They are not practical footwear for everyday use, nor are they generally considered stylish or fashionable.  There is no indication that the wearing of SCP-8582 would improve your life in any tangible way.  There is every indication that the wearing of SCP-8582 would cause inconvenience for yourself and those around you.  

For the above reasons, and because they do not belong to you, it would be inappropriate for you to wear SCP-8582.
