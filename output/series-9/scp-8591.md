**Item #:** SCP-8591

**Object Class:** Anomalous

**Special Containment Procedures:** N/A

**Description:** SCP-8591 was an adult African Elephant that sporadically appeared and disappeared in Researcher Alex Thorley’s office.

After the publishing of this documentation, SCP-8591 vanished and has not reappeared in over a year.

= **TRANSCRIPT**

---

**Thorley:** *(On the phone)* It’s here again.

*[…]*

**Thorley:** Maybe it’ll go away.

*[…]*

**Thorley:** I didn't do *anything.*

*[…]*

**Thorley:** I didn't do anything wrong.

*[…]*

**Thorley:** Fine.

*(Thorley enters their office. There’s an elephant inside.)*

**Thorley:** ...I’m addressing you.

Rsr. Thorley has been appropriately reprimanded for their undisclosed misconduct prior to this interaction.
