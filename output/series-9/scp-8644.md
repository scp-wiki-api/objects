**Special Containment Procedures:**

**Direct containment of SCP-8644 is not possible.**

Foundation efforts are focused on controlling public awareness and maintaining informational stability. All astronomical data related to SCP-8644 is restricted to Level 5 personnel. Civilian observations are to be altered or dismissed as conventional astrophysical phenomena.

Deployment of Scranton Reality Anchors (SRAs) is authorized only in indirect configurations. Attempts to directly influence or stabilize SCP-8644 have produced no measurable effect.

Personnel assigned to SCP-8644 are to be regularly evaluated for cognitohazard exposure. Individuals displaying compulsive behavior related to the anomaly or attempts to interfere with containment protocols are to be removed from duty and consequently programmed a meeting with the Ethics Committee.

At the moment, all procedures are considered provisional.

**Description:**

SCP-8644 designates a non-observable spatial structure positioned around a visible cosmic rupture, collectively referred to as SCP-8644-A.

SCP-8644 is located above Earth at Right Ascension 19h 53m 41.2s and Declination +08° 44′ 17″, within the direction of the constellation Aquila, at an estimated distance of approximately 

3.2×10^^9^^ light years.

SCP-8644-A manifests as a localized distortion of the stellar field, in which background stars appear displaced, dimmed, or entirely absent. Spectrographic and gravitational analysis indicate inconsistencies with known astrophysical phenomena. The anomaly does not behave as a gravitational lens, singularity, or nebular formation, despite heavy similarities.

Surrounding SCP-8644-A is SCP-8644, an undetectable boundary region characterized by near-zero Hume levels. This region does not register through conventional or paratechnological instrumentation. Its presence is inferred exclusively through negative space measurements, missing data, and discontinuities in the current observed reality.

SCP-8644 functions as a containment barrier, hereafter referred to as the "Lock".

The "Lock" encloses an interior region from which no information, radiation, or matter has been successfully extracted, inserted, or measured. Attempts to map or model the interior of SCP-8644-A result in non-resolvable data loss or recursive contradictions.

Analysis indicates that the Lock is actively maintaining separation between baseline reality and the enclosed region.

**Two extranormal entities have been identified in association with SCP-8644-A:**

* SCP-8644-1 is the entity contained within the rupture, provisionally identified with the biblical figure *Jezebel*. SCP-8644-1 is classified as a Type Green reality bender of extreme magnitude. It exerts continuous pressure against the Lock, correlating with fluctuations in the size, luminosity, and instability of SCP-8644-A.

SCP-8644-1 demonstrates persistent escape-oriented behavior. All observed activity is consistent with attempts to breach SCP-8644 and access baseline reality. Indirect evidence suggests that SCP-8644-1 possesses strong cognitohazardous capabilities, capable of influencing human thought patterns at long range. Affected individuals may develop compulsive urges to interfere with containment protocols, rationalize the necessity of release of SCP-8644-1, or reinterpret SCP-8644 as non-hostile.

Recovered communications and behavioral models indicate that SCP-8644-1’s primary objective is unrestricted manifestation within baseline reality. Projected outcomes of a successful breach include large-scale reality restructuring, termination of existing universal constants, and potential recursive recreation of reality states for repeated destruction. Motivational analysis suggests extreme sadistic drive combined with an aversion to confinement.

* SCP-8644-2 designates the entity maintaining the Lock, provisionally identified with the biblical figure *Elijah*. SCP-8644-2 is also classified as a Type Green reality bender. Its presence is inferred through localized stabilization events, temporary restoration of Hume consistency, and resistance against expansion of SCP-8644-A.

SCP-8644-2 appears to function as a counterforce to SCP-8644-1, sustaining the integrity of SCP-8644 despite ongoing degradation. No direct communication from SCP-8644-2 has been verified. Its motives are interpreted as preservation of baseline reality.

Long-term observation indicates that SCP-8644 is undergoing gradual structural failure. This degradation is marked by increased irregularity in the boundary of SCP-8644-A, escalation in Hume instability, and a measurable rise in cognitohazardous incidents among assigned personnel.

Failure of SCP-8644 is projected to result in total collapse of baseline reality and Earth itself, resulting in a CK-End-Of-Reality Scenario and an XK-End-Of-The-World Scenario.

Current containment strategy is based on non-interference and suppression of cognitohazardous spread.

**Addendum 8644.2 — Cognitohazard Report**

Following the identification of SCP-8644-1, a consistent pattern of cognitohazardous influence has been observed among personnel assigned to SCP-8644.

The effect does not appear to rely on a single sensory trigger. Exposure through visual data, theoretical models, or even secondary documentation has been sufficient to produce measurable cognitive deviation from baseline. The influence is therefore considered non-localized and partially abstract in nature.

Affected individuals initially display increased attention toward SCP-8644-A and its associated distortions. This develops into prolonged cognitive fixation, accompanied by a gradual shift in interpretation of the anomaly itself. Over time, SCP-8644 is no longer perceived as a containment structure, but as "an obstruction, or something requiring correction", quoting an affected researcher. 

As exposure continues, subjects begin to reorganize existing beliefs to support this conclusion. It is important to note that individuals consistently report that their reasoning remains their own.

In advanced stages, this reinterpretation produces extreme behavioral consequences. Personnel may seek unauthorized access to restricted data, challenge containment directives, or attempt to interfere with stabilization systems. 

**These actions are to be punished accordingly, after discussion with the Ethics Committee.** 

Language patterns across affected personnel show convergence toward a small set of recurring ideas centered on release, correction, and inevitability. Notably, descriptions associated with threat, danger, or hostility diminish over time, even in individuals previously aware of projected end-of-reality scenarios.

All currently available evidence attributes these effects to SCP-8644-1. No cognitohazardous influence linked to SCP-8644-2 has been identified.

Attempts at mitigation have had limited success. Standard memetic resistance protocols slow progression but do not prevent it. Increased exposure correlates directly with severity, with higher-clearance personnel demonstrating faster cognitive restructuring and fixation.
