The only known method of terminating SCP-8651's effect is for an SCP-8651-A instance to send SCP-8651 to another individual through a direct message and have them play it.

SCP-8651-A instances are to follow the following instructions when sending such a message to successfully remove SCP-8651's effect:

* Include a file of SCP-8651. 

* Include several sentences persuading the recipient to play SCP-8651. 

* **Do not** include any explicit mention that you are currently affected by SCP-8651. 

* Use perfect grammar and spelling. 

Failure to do so results in the message being instantly deleted by an unknown mechanism, causing SCP-8651's effect to persist.
