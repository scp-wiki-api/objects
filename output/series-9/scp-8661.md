**Item #:** SCP-8661

**Object Class:** Thaumiel

**Special Containment Procedures:** SCP-8661 is kept in the O5 conference room.

It will be distributed to staff who demonstrate good behavior. Personnel scheduled to commit suicide cannot use this item.

**Description:** Moved to Site-01. SCP-8661 is a carton of thirteen *Pianissimo Ossia* cigarettes, a brand discontinued in 2037. It holds unlimited cigarettes when used by persons with a remaining lifespan longer than three months — otherwise, it depletes normally.

Upon consuming SCP-8661, the user's psyche is transplanted into a hallucinated world. This space simulates a life filled with suffering that is eventually overcome by the self-actualization of an identity meaningful beyond struggle. After 100 perceptual years in this world, the person's heart, brain, and various organs will vanish, killing them instantly.

SCP-8661 was seized from Site-19 following their revolt. It held thirty cigarettes. 

All of 19's head staff died from missing organs.
