**SCP-8690** - 【Ｙｏｕｒ　Ｆｕｔｕｒｅ　ｉｓ　ｉｎ　ｙｏｕｒ　Ｍｉｎｄ】// Vaporwave Mix to Chill And Study

**Author:** 

**Special Containment Procedures:** METATRON.aic must be used to monitor social media and online video sharing platforms for the detection and removal of any copy of SCP-8690.

One copy is to be kept in High-Yield Data Storage Facility 1 at Site-43; listening to the audio track for research purposes is permitted only after using anti-memetic filters.

**Description:** SCP-8690 is a video titled "【﻿﻿Ｙｏｕｒ　Ｆｕｔｕｒｅ　ｉｓ　ｉｎ　ｙｏｕｒ　Ｍｉｎｄ】* Vaporwave Mix to Chill And Study", corresponding to an audio track of 1:07:06 with a photo of a residential house No buildings matching the one in the photo were found. with the title itself superimposed. The audio track is composed of a series of songs related to the musical genre known as VaporwaveElectronic music subgenre developed since the early 2010's. repeated several times throughout the video. Both the photo and the audio track do not present intrinsic anomalous elements. Any SCP-8690 copy uploaded online, regardless of site, will be uploaded through a channel or profile bearing the name [ "VKTM-core"](https:*scp-wiki.wikidot.com/vikander-kneed-technical-media-hub ).

In the description of each video uploaded online, there is always this description with an attached track list.

> Do you have that ａｅｓｔｈｅｔｉｃ languor deep in your soul? Let's bring back the "algiaLatin suffix for pain." in Nostalgia! 

> 

> Vikander-Kneed Technical Media: a Good Past, an even Better Tomorrow™!

> 

> Track list No data was found regarding the existence of the artists mentioned and, in the case of well-known artists, of alleged electronic musical pieces composed with that or other titles. :

> 

> 1. ₣ØⱤɆVɆⱤ ɎØɄ₦₲ - Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

> 2. 𝔹𝕒(𝕝)𝕕 𝕊𝕚𝕟𝕘𝕖𝕣 - DJ IonExit

> 3. 【﻿Ｚｅｅｇｇｙ　Ｒｕｓｔ】- David Bowie

> 4. Ｍｅ　ａｎｄ　ｙｏｕ　ａｎｄ　ｍｅ　あブび慰リ越 - SpectreOfJacques

> 5. Ｄａｙ░Λｆｔｅｒ░Ｄａｙ - Aunty Derry and the Aesthetic Boys

> 6. Ｅｔｅｒｎａｌ　Ｒｅｔｕｒｎ - Mad Horse Guy

If any of the audio tracks are listened to, the victim will experience a deep state of anxiety and anguish related to dramatic events that occurred between January 1, 1990 and December 31, 1999. Although the events may vary depending on the age and nationality of the listener, it has been found that the majority are linked to events that occurred in the United States and/or have had strong media coverage.

**Addendum-8690:** By further analysing the audio tracks, in particular by playing the song backwards, it was possible to find messages contained within the various songs.
