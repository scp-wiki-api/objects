"The Archive Beneath Romania"

Item #: SCP-8694

Object Class: Keter

Special Containment Procedures

The physical location of SCP-8694 remains inaccessible.

Containment efforts are focused on suppressing manifestations associated with SCP-8694.

The Foundation maintains observation programs within Romania, Moldova, Ukraine, Serbia, Hungary, and Bulgaria for the appearance of Type-8694 Events.

Individuals exposed to SCP-8694 manifestations are to receive Class-B amnestics.

All archaeological excavations conducted within the Carpathian Basin deeper than 120 meters require Foundation oversight.

Discovery of any underground structure containing nonhuman writing systems is to be treated as a potential SCP-8694 manifestation.

No attempts to physically locate SCP-8694 are authorized without O5 approval.

Current Foundation consensus is that successful discovery of SCP-8694 may constitute an XK-Class End-of-History Scenario.

---

Description

SCP-8694 is a location believed to exist beneath Romania.

The precise position remains unknown.

The anomaly first came to Foundation attention in 1974 after multiple unrelated archaeological teams reported discovering identical underground chambers separated by hundreds of kilometers.

Each chamber contained a single stone doorway.

The doorway always led to normal bedrock.

However, all individuals who viewed the doorway reported identical recurring dreams.

Within these dreams, subjects observed a vast underground archive extending beyond visible limits.

The structure contained shelves, tunnels, vaults, and chambers carved directly into stone.

Most notably, every witness described the presence of books.

The books were reportedly impossible to read.

Subjects stated that whenever they attempted to focus on the text, they experienced overwhelming certainty that the books contained events that had not yet occurred.

---

Discovery of Pattern

Between 1974 and 2019, over four hundred Type-8694 Events were documented.

Analysis revealed a statistically impossible pattern.

Every chamber appeared precisely where archaeological evidence suggested an ancient settlement should have existed but did not.

Entire cultures appeared absent from the historical record.

Excavations consistently found evidence that populations once existed in these locations.

However, all records of their existence had vanished.

Artifacts survived.

Buildings survived.

Human remains survived.

Written history did not.

Addendum 8694-A

The Empty Civilization

In 1998, Foundation historians discovered evidence for a previously unknown Bronze Age civilization.

Evidence indicated a population exceeding one million individuals.

The civilization possessed cities, trade networks, agriculture, and metallurgy.

Yet no surviving historical source from any neighboring culture mentioned them.

The civilization had effectively been erased from history.

The only common factor linking every discovered settlement was the presence of SCP-8694 chambers.

---

Addendum 8694-B

Exploration Attempt Alpha

In 2003, Foundation personnel successfully entered a dream-state manifestation associated with SCP-8694 through oneiric induction procedures.

The following account was recovered from Agent V. Stănescu.

---

I found myself standing in a corridor.

The walls were carved from black stone.

There were shelves everywhere.

Millions of books.

Every shelf stretched farther than I could see.

The strange part wasn't the size.

It was the silence.

I knew there should have been people there.

I couldn't see them.

But I knew they were missing.

Every book had a title.

The titles were dates.

Not names.

Not subjects.

Dates.

Some were in the future.

Some appeared millions of years old.

One shelf contained books dated before the formation of Earth.

I pulled one from the shelf.

The cover read:

"17 April 2048"

When I opened it, every page was blank.

Something behind me laughed.

Not a human laugh.

Not an animal.

Something old.

Then I woke up.

Addendum 8694-C

The Librarian Hypothesis

Analysis of dream reports uncovered recurring references to an unidentified entity.

Witness descriptions vary.

Age, gender, appearance, and species are inconsistent.

However, all witnesses report certainty that the entity performs the same function.

The entity is allegedly responsible for removing books from the archive.

When asked what happens to removed books, witnesses provide identical answers:

«"Those events never happened."»

---

Incident 8694-Delta

In 2016, an unexplained global historical anomaly occurred.

For approximately nineteen minutes, all digital records relating to the city of Târgu Jiu disappeared.

Maps, census records, photographs, government databases, satellite imagery, and Foundation archives became inaccessible.

The phenomenon resolved spontaneously.

Subsequent investigation revealed no evidence of tampering.

Three unrelated dreamers exposed to SCP-8694 reported observing a newly empty space on one shelf within the archive.

The shelf label reportedly read:

«"Târgu Jiu"»

No explanation has been found.

Addendum 8694-E

O5 Restricted Material

The following document was recovered from a sealed Foundation archive predating the Foundation itself.

Carbon dating suggests creation between 900 and 1100 CE.

The document was written in Latin.

Translation follows:

«There exists beneath the mountains a place where histories are kept.

Not our histories.

All histories.

What was.

What is.

What might be.

We have learned the terrible truth.

The world remembers only what remains in the Archive.

The Archive does not preserve history.

History survives because the Archive remembers it.

Should the final Keeper depart, reality itself shall become unwritten.»

---

Addendum 8694-F

O5 Council Memorandum

Following Incident Delta, a complete review of Foundation records was initiated.

Investigators discovered evidence that SCP-8694 has appeared in Foundation documentation since the organization's earliest days.

Several references were dated decades before the Foundation officially existed.

More concerningly, numerous documents describe prior expeditions to SCP-8694.

No records of these expeditions currently exist.

Only references to the missing records remain.

Researchers estimate that at least seventeen major Foundation operations involving SCP-8694 have been erased from history.

How these references survived remains unknown.

---

Final Note

Current containment strategy assumes SCP-8694 is not an archive of history.

The archive is history.

Foundation researchers increasingly believe reality is not a sequence of events.

Instead, reality may be a continuously maintained record.

The purpose of SCP-8694 remains unknown.

The identity of the Keeper remains unknown.

The location of SCP-8694 remains unknown.

One question remains unresolved:

If events disappear when books are removed — who is still removing them?
