**Item #:** SCP-8741

**Object Class:** keter

**Special Containment procedures:** Due to SCP-8741's capability to execute "narrative assassinations"—collapsing entire alternate universes like grains of sand to eliminate a specific target—physical containment is impossible. Instead, containment relies entirely on psychological management and contracts. SCP-8741 is voluntarily housed in a retrostyled civilian apartment at Site-01, resembling a mid-century safehouse.

**Description:** SCP 8741 is an supreme entity that appears to be an ordinary man. Calm and collected. Avoids direct attention but has been observed "Interfering" reality. He exhibits the powers to turn anything he wills turns to reality. Direct perceptions of other lower dimensions. It treats other universes, multiverses as nothing more than a grain of sand. Existential independence, Self awareness ( SCP 8741 knows he isnt supposed to exist) Scp 8741 understand the pattern of the universe. He rewrote it.

Despite his powers SCP 8741 is mentally unstable. No one treats him like a normal human being. This cold isolation causes his mental state to be deteriorate. 

During the early 20s period scp 8741 has been assigned to be an undercover hitmen. To assassin any entities.

**Status:** Active
