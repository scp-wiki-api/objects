**SCP-8766:** I Know Your Secrets

**Author:** [atypica](https://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/atypica-s-awfully-alliterative-anomalous-author-page)

CW: Brief mentions of drug use, sex, medical malpractice, ableism, child death, and abuse of power in institutional/law enforcement contexts.

**Item #:** SCP-8766

**Object Class:** Safe

**Special Containment Procedures:** SCP-8766 is to be kept in a standard soundproofed object containment unit, with no personnel of Clearance Levels 3 and above allowed within. Under no circumstances is SCP-8766 to be destroyed or otherwise damaged.

**Description:** SCP-8766 is a small sculpture depicting a kneeling bipedal creature with a grinning humanoid face. It exhibits a limited range of movement, rotating its neck to face anyone in its vicinity. Upon a subject entering SCP-8766's area of effect (estimated to be around 4 meters) in the presence of at least one other observer, SCP-8766 will proceed to vocalize compromising or shameful information pertaining to the subject, seemingly to mock them. Subjects describe SCP-8766's voice as breathy and hoarse. It displays fluency in several languages, and has been observed to recall information from as far back as 1885 with perfect recollection.
