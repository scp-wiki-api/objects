**Item #:** SCP-8793

**Object Class:** Simpatico

**Special Containment Procedures:** Instances of SCP-8793-1 are to undergo BFATTH treatment.

**Description:** SCP-8793 refers to a phenomenon which causes Foundation Research Advisory Committee members (hereafter refered to as SCP-8793-1) to view anomalies that do not fit their specific view of what constitutes an SCP object as "real" to be a joke. SCP-8793-1 instances will typically reject all research projects on anomalies that they deem "unserious" or "implausible," offering statement such as "If this were a joke, I would like it more." 

Given the nature of the work of the Foundation, this can lead to multiple containment failures as well as failure to catalog certain anomalies due to lack of approval from SCP-8793-1 instances in RAC positions. Because anomalies do not follow common sense by definition, having a close-minded nature about them should not be encouraged by the Foundation.

 SCP-8793-1 instances typically cease exhibiting these properties following minor blunt force application to the head, otherwise known as BFATTH treatment.
