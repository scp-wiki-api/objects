= **SCP-8937:** I am the Worst Thing to Have Ever Happened to Me

= **Author:** 

**Content Warning:** This work contains a brief mention of self-harm.

@@ @@

@@ @@

**Special Containment Procedures:** SCP-8937-1 instances are to be housed in single-occupancy quarters at Site-17. All interaction is to be limited to written correspondence only. Reflective surfaces are not permitted within SCP-8937-1 housing units. SCP-8937-1 instances are to receive weekly psychological assessment. Assigned counselors are to be rotated every six weeks. Counselors exhibiting more than three symptoms consistent with SCP-8937 exposure are to be quarantined and monitored for a period of no less than thirty days. 

**Description:** SCP-8937 is a cognitohazardous phenomenon transmitted by direct mutual eye contact with an instance of SCP-8937-1. Incidental or peripheral contact has not resulted in confirmed transmission. 

Confirmed symptoms of exposure include: 

* Prolonged unresponsiveness when addressed by name

* Withdrawal from established relationships 

* Attempts to sever contact with individuals from prior stages of life

* Anxiety towards being photographed

* Belief that photography causes physical disfigurement

* Inability to recognize self in recordings

* Distress towards being described

* Expressions of grief with no identifiable referent

* Attempts to occupy less physical space

* Cessation of normal social behavior

* Cessation of use of given name

* Avoidance of medical facilities

* Disposal of objects associated with personal history

* Destruction of legal documentation

* Avoidance of eye contact

* Self-directed violence

* ███████

* Anguish 

---

**Addendum 8937-A: Recovered Effects**

Items recovered from the residence of an SCP-8937-1 instance prior to containment included garments with wear ranging from new to heavily worn, and a collection of photographs in which the subjects' face had been obscured by means consistent with SCP-8937 activity. Other items recovered include a hand mirror found face-down and cracked, pressed and dried flowers, and a journal with blank pages. 

Also recovered was one intact instant print photograph of the SCP-8937-1 instance. SCP-8937-1 instance expressed extreme levels of agitation and grief upon learning of the photographs' existence.
